Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 29, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended October 29, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $18,788,000, or $0.17 per share on net sales of $1,041,046,000 against net loss of $32,063,000, or $0.29 per share on net sales of $980,027,000 for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported net loss of $24,740,000, or $0.22 per share on net sales of $3,191,189,000 against net loss of $33,443,000, or $0.29 per share on net sales of $2,994,175,000 for the same period a year ago."
