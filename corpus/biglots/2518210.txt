Big Lots Inc. Reports Retail Sales for the Five Weeks, Nine-Week Quarter to Date and Forty-Eight Week Year to Date Ended December 31, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the five weeks ended December 31, 2005 of $708.1 million, a 6.1% increase compared to retail sales of $667.5 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 2.3% in December. For the forty-eight week year to date period ended December 31, 2005, retail sales increased 6.2% to $4,319.9 million, compared to $4,068.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2004.
