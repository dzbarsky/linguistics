Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the Month, Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year Ended January, 28, 2006.
"Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the month, fourth quarter and fiscal year ended January, 28, 2006. For the month, sales jumped 6.4% at stores open two years or longer, surpassing Wall Street views. Total sales grew 5.6% to $272.4 million from $257.9 million. Fiscal year sales grew 1.8% on a same-store basis. Overall, sales rose 6.1% to $4.59 billion."
