Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended January 28, 2006 ; Provides Earnings Outlook for the First Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2007.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended January 28, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $14.7 million, or $0.13 per diluted share, compared to net income of $57.2 million, or $0.51 per diluted share for the same period of fiscal 2004. Net sales were $1,394.9 million, a 6.1% increase compared to net sales of $1,314.1 million for the same period of fiscal 2004. The company provides earnings outlook for the first quarter and full year"
