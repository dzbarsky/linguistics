Big Lots Reports May 2003 Comparable Store Sales Increase of 0.3% on Retail Sales of $287.8 Million.
Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended May 31, 2003 of $287.8 million, a 5.0% increase compared to retail sales of $274.1 million for the same period of 2002. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 0.3% in May, with the number of customer transactions increasing 1.1% and the value of the average basket decreasing 0.8%.
