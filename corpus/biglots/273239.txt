Big Lots Reports First Quarter results for the year 2003.
Big Lots Inc. reported first quarter net income of $10.2 million, or $0.09 per share, compared to net income of $12.2 million in the first quarter of 2002, or $0.11 per share. For the thirteen-week quarter ended May 3, 2003, the Company reported net sales of $948.4 million, a 4.9% increase compared to net sales of $904.1 million for the first quarter of 2002.
