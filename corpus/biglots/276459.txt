Big Lots Reports March Retail Sales 2003.
Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the five weeks ended April 5, 2003 of $351.9 million, a 2.3% increase compared to retail sales of $344.0 million for the same period of 2002. Retail sales increased 2.0% to $629.1 million for the nine-week quarter to date period ended April 5, 2003, compared to $616.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2002.
