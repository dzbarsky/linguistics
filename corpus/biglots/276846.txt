Big Lots Reports Third Quarter Comparable Sales Increase for 2002.
"Big Lots Inc. has reported retail sales for the five weeks ended November 2, 2002 of $349.7 million, an 8.6% increase compared to retail sales of $321.9 million for the same period of 2001. Retail sales increased 12.0% to $850.5 million for the thirteen week third quarter ended November 2, 2002, compared to $759.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. Third quarter loss per share of $0.05 compared to a loss per share of $0.14 in the prior year. For the thirty-nine week year to date period ended November 2, 2002, retail sales were $2,608.8 million, a 15.3% increase compared to $2,261.7 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales increased 10.3% for the year to date period, with the number of customer transactions increasing 4.8% and the value of the average basket increasing 5.5%."
