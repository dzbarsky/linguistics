Big Lots Reports on Plan November 2002 Retail Sales.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended November 30, 2002 of $405.6 million, a 1.4% decrease compared to retail sales of $411.4 million for the same period of 2001. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open for at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 5.9% in November, with the value of the average customer basket increasing 0.7% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 6.6%. November comparable store sales results were consistent with the Company's plan, which called for a decrease in the mid to high single digit range. For the forty-three week year to date period ended November 30, 2002, retail sales were $3,014.5 million, a 12.8% increase compared to $2,673.2 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales increased 7.9% for the year to date period, with the number of customer transactions increasing 3.3% and the value of the average basket increasing 4.6%."
