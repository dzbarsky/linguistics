Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended April 29, 2006.
Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended April 29, 2006. For the quarter ended April 29, net income was $13.7 million, or 12 cents per share, compared with the year-ago profit of $7.8 million, or 7 cents per share. Earnings from continuing operations doubled to $14.5 million, or 13 cents per share, from $7.27 million, or 6 cents per share, in the 2005 quarter. Sales climbed to $1.09 billion from $1.04 billion, with same-store sales, or sales in stores open at least two years, up 2.5%.
