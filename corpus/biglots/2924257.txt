Big Lots Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $150 mm.
The Board of Directors of Big Lots Inc. (NYSE: BLI) authorized the repurchase of up to $150 million of the Companys common shares on February 22, 2006, as it believes that the repurchase plan builds value for shareholders and that the size of the repurchase program approximates the Companys fiscal 2005 free cash flow and fits well within the Companys capital structure. The Company said it expects the purchases to be made from time to time in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions with such purchases to be completed within the next 12 months. Common shares acquired through the repurchase program will be available for general corporate purposes. As of April 29, 2006, the Company repurchased 2,345,400 shares at a total cost of $31.2 million.
