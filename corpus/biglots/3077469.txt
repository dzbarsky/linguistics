Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended July 29, 2006.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended July 29, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported retail sales increased 5.7% to $1,046.5 million, compared to $989.9 million as revised for the same period in fiscal 2005. For the six months, the company reported retail sales increased 5.2% to $2,124.6 million, compared to $2,019.7 million for the same period in fiscal 2005. Comparable store sales increased 3.8% for the year to date period."
