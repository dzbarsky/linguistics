Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter of 2006; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third and Fourth Quarter of 2006; Revised Earnings Guidance for 2006.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter of 2006. For the second quarter, the company reported earnings per share of 4 cents on revenue of $1.06 billion. The company raised its full-year 2006 earnings per share outlook to 62 cents - 67 cents from 44 cents - 49 cents."
