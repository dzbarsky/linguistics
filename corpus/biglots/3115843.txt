Big Lots Inc. Announces Resignation of Ned Mansour as Board of Director.
On August 15, 2006, Big Lots Inc. announced that Mr. Ned Mansour resigned from the Board due to health related issues. Mr. Berger, was elected to serve on the Board. Mr. Berger is the Executive Vice President, Global Foodservice and President and Chief Executive Officer, Heinz North America Foodservice, a world-wide manufacturer and marketer of processed food products.
