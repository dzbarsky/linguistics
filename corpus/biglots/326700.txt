Big Lots Reports Third Quarter 2003 Results.
"Big Lots Inc. reported a third quarter fiscal 2003, Net loss for the three months ended November 1,2003, was $6.4 million, 0.05 per share, compared to net loss of $5.1 million, or $0.04 per share, during the same thirty-nine week period of fiscal 2002. Net sales for the three months ended November 1, 2003 were $948.1 million, compared to net sales of $868.2 million for the third quarter of fiscal 2002. For 39 week period ended November1, 2003. net loss was $4.2 million, or $0.04 per share, compared to net income of $10.3 million, or $0.09 per share, during the same thirty-nine week period of fiscal 2002. Net sales for the third quarter ended November 1, 2003 were $2,845.7 million, compared to net sales of $2,651.5 million for the 39 week of fiscal 2002."
