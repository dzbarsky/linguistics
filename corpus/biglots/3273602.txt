Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales for the Third Quarter and Nine Months of 2006.
"Big Lots Inc. reported sales for the third quarter and nine months of 2006. For the quarter, the company reported that sales at stores open at least two years, or same-store sales, grew 5.8%, driven by an increase in the value of items customers purchased per visit. Total sales increased 5.8% to $1.03 billion from $974.3 million as revised for the same period in fiscal 2005. For the nine months, the company reported that retail sales increased 5.4% to $3.16 billion from $2.99 billion a year ago. Same-store sales grew 4.4% for the year to date period."
