Big Lots Reports Second Quarter Ended August 3, 2002 Results.
"Big Lots Inc. reported second quarter net income of $3.2 million or $0.03 per share, compared to a loss of $10.7 million or $0.09 per share, in the second quarter of 2001. For the quarter ended August 3, 2002, the Company reported sales of $879.3 million, a 17.5% increase compared to sales of $748.4 million in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales for stores open two years at the beginning of the fiscal year were up 12.4% for the quarter driven by a 6.5% increase in customer transactions and a 5.9% increase in the value of the average basket. Year to date net income was $15.4 million, $0.13 per share, compared to a net loss of $10.4 million, or $0.09 per share, during the same twenty-six week period of 2001. On a year to date basis, sales increased 17.2% to $1,783.4 million compared to $1,522.0 million in 2001. Comparable stores sales increased 12.0% for the year to date period with customer transactions increasing 5.7% and the value of the average basket increasing 6.3%."
