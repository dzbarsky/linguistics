Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter Ended October 28, 2006.
Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter ended October 28, 2006. For the third quarter, the company earned $1.7 million, or 2 cents a share, compared with a loss of $18.8 million, or 17 cents a share, a year-ago-quarter. Sales grew to $1.05 billion, from $991.4 million last year, and comparable store sales were up 5.8% at outlets open at least two years.
