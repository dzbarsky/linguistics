Big Lots Reports Retail Sales For The Four Weeks And Forty-three Week Year To Date Period Ended November 29, 2003.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended November 29, 2003 of $447.4 million, a 10.3% increase compared to retail sales of $405.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 5.6% in November, with the number of customer transactions increasing 3.0% and the value of the average basket increasing 2.6%. For the forty-three week year to date period ended November 29, 2003, retail sales were $3,250.1 million, a 7.8% increase compared to $3,014.5 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales increased 3.2% for the year to date period, with the number of customer transactions increasing 1.7% and the value of the average basket increasing 1.5%."
