Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended February 3, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Year of 2007.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended February 3, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $104,299,000 or $0.94 per diluted share on net sales of $1,545,354,000 compared to net income of $14,652,000 or $0.13 per diluted share on net sales of $1,394,902,000 reported for the same period last year. For the year, the company expects income from continuing operations of $1.18 to $1.23 per share versus income from continuing operations of $1.01 per share last year. This guidance for EPS"
