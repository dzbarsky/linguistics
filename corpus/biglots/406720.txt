Big Lots Inc. Reports Retail Sales For The Five Weeks Ended January 3, 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the five weeks ended January 3, 2004 of $633.7 million, an 11.0% increase compared to retail sales of $571.0 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 6.2% in December, with the number of customer transactions increasing 3.4% and the value of the average basket increasing 2.8%. Retail sales increased 10.7% to $1,081.1 million for the nine weeks ended January 3, 2004, compared to $976.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales increased 6.0% for the quarter to date period with the number of customer transactions increasing 3.3% and the value of the average basket increasing 2.7%. For the forty-eight week year to date period ended January 3, 2004, retail sales were $3,883.7 million, an 8.3% increase compared to $3,585.5 million for the same period in fiscal 2002.
