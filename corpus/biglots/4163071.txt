Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the First Quarter Ended May 5, 2007.
Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the first quarter ended May 5, 2007. The company said its same-store sales in the first quarter increased 4.9%, fueled by the effect of an extra advertising circular and sales of furniture and electronics. The companys total sales for the three months ended May 5 advanced 3.5% to $1.12 billion from $1.08 billion during the same period a year ago. Big Lots said the addition of one advertising circular compared to the prior year boosted sales in the quarter by about 1%.
