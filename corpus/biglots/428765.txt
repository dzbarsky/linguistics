Big Lots Inc. Reports Retail Sales for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended January 31, 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended January 31, 2004 of $236.1 million, a 3.7% increase compared to retail sales of $227.7 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 0.6% in January, with the number of customer transactions decreasing 0.9% and the value of the average basket increasing 0.3%. For the year ended January 31, 2004, retail sales were $4,119.9 million, an 8.0% increase compared to $3,813.2 million for the same period in fiscal 2002.
