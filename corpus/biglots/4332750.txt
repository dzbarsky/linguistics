Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended May 5, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2008.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended May 5, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported income from continuing operations of $29.0 million, or $0.26 per diluted share, compared to income from continuing operations of $14.5 million, or $0.13 per diluted share, in the first quarter of fiscal 2006. Net sales increased by 3.4% to $1,128.4 million, compared to $1,091.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2006. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year increased by 4.9% for the quarter. For the full year, the company increases fiscal 2007 annual guidance for income from continuing operations to $1.25 to $1.30 per share versus income from continuing operations of $1.01"
