Big Lots Raised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2007 ; Provides Earning Guidance for the Third and Fourth Quarter of 2007.
"Big Lots Inc. lifted its full-year income from continuing operations guidance on strong second-quarter results and current business trends. The company now sees 2007 earnings from continuing operations in a range of $1.43 to $1.48 per share, up from its prior forecast of $1.25 to $1.30 per share. The company forecast fourth-quarter earnings between 87 cents and 92 cents per share, assuming the same increase in same-store sales as the third quarter."
