Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended August 4, 2007.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended August 4, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $23,384,000 or diluted net income per share $0.22 on net sales of $1,084,891,000 compared to net income of $4,299,000 or diluted net income per share $0.04 on net sales of $1,056,535,000 for the second quarter ended July 29, 2006. For the six months, the company reported net income of $52,148,000 or diluted net income per share $0.48 on net sales of $2,213,290,000 compared to net income of $18,012,000 or diluted net income per share $0.16 on net sales of $2,148,157,000 for the period ended July 29, 2006."
