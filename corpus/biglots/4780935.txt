Update to Big Lots Incs Equity Buyback on August 4, 2007.
As of August 4, 2007, Big Lots Inc has repurchased 7,900,000 shares at a weighted average price of $28.91 per share for an aggregate value of $228.7 million. Additionally, during the first quarter of fiscal 2007 as part of the repurchase program, the company received 2,800,000 of its outstanding common shares representing the minimum number of shares purchased under a $100.0 million guaranteed share repurchase transaction. The Company may receive up to 400,000 additional shares of common stock from the counterparty in settlement of the GSR.
