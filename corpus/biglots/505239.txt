Big Lots Inc. Reports Retail Sales Results for the Five Weeks and Nine Weeks Ended April 3, 2004; Affirms Outlook for the First Quarter of 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the five weeks ended April 3, 2004 of $394.9 million, a 12.2% increase compared to retail sales of $351.9 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 6.7% in March, with the number of customer transactions increasing 2.0% and the value of the average basket increasing 4.7%.
