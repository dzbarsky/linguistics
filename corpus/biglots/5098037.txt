Big Lots Inc. Announces Executive Changes.
Big Lots Inc. announced that effective July 11, 2005, Michael J. Potter resigned as the Companys Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and President. The company announced the selection of Steven S. Fishman as the Company's new Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and President. Mr. Fishman became the Companys Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and President on July 11, 2005. Mr. Fishman, 54, joins the Company from Rhodes Inc. where he was a member of its Board of Directors and is the President, Chief Executive Officer and Chief Restructuring Officer.
