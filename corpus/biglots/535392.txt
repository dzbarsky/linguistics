Big Lots Inc. Reports Retail Sales Results for the Four Weeks Ended May 1, 2004 and Retails Sales for the First Quarter Ended May 1, 2004 ; Affirms Outlook for the First Quarter of 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended May 1, 2004 of $306.0 million, a 0.3% increase compared to retail sales of $305.2 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two fiscal years as of the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 4.2% in April, with the value of the average basket increasing 0.8% and number of customer transactions decreasing 5.0%. The Company affirmed its first quarter earnings guidance, which calls for earnings per share from continuing operations in the range of $0.03 to $0.07."
