Big Lots Inc. Updates Sales Guidance for the First Quarter Ending May 3, 2008.
Big Lots Inc. updated its sales expectations for the first quarter ending May 3, 2008. Based on sales trends quarter to date and the forecast for the remaining 5 days of the quarter, the Company now expects comparable store sales to increase in the low to mid 3% range. This compares to the Company's original expectations which called for an increase in comparable store sales in the range of 1% to 2% against a 4.9% comp increase last year.
