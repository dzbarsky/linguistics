Big Lots Inc. Opens Fifth Store in Tulsa Area.
Big Lots Inc. opened a store at 8939 S. Memorial Drive. The company committed to making a difference where it matters--in the communities serve. The 20,000-square-foot location will carry essentials such as toothpaste and batteries as well as furniture, home decor, grocery items, seasonal products, toys and gifts. Big Lots works with more than 3,000 manufacturers to bring canceled orders, discontinued items, test market products and excess inventory back into the marketplace, often for a fraction of their original retail prices, a company press release states.
