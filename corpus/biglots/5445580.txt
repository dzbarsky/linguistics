Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the First Quarter Ended May 3, 2008.
Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the first quarter ended May 3, 2008. For the period, the company reported retail sales increased 2.1% to $1,139.0 million, compared to $1,116.0 million for the first quarter of fiscal 2007. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year increased 3.4% for the first quarter of fiscal 2008. As anticipated, first quarter results were positively impacted by two large closeout deals: one from a major furniture manufacturer and one from a national drugstore chain. The Company estimates these two deals benefited comparable store sales by approximately 1.5% to 2%. Comparable store sales in the first quarter were driven by an increase in the value of the average basket as the Company's raise the ring strategy continues to deliver positive results.
