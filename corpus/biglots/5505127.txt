Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended May 3, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of Fiscal 2009 ; Raises Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of Fiscal 2009.
"Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended May 3, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $34.5 million or $0.42 per diluted share compared to net income of $28.8 million, or $0.26 per diluted share, for the first quarter of fiscal 2007. Net sales increased 2.1% to $1,151.6 million, compared to $1,128.4 million for the same period in fiscal 2007. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year increased 3.4% for the quarter. The company raised fiscal 2009 guidance for earnings from continuing operations and cash"
