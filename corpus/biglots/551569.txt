Big Lots Reports Results for the First Quarter Ended May 1, 2004.
Big Lots Inc. reported first quarter net income of $6.7 million, or $0.06 per share, compared to net income of $10.2 million in the first quarter of fiscal 2003, or $0.09 per share. Net sales for the first quarter ended May 1, 2004 were $1,019.2 million, a 7.5% increase compared to net sales of $948.4 million for the same period of fiscal 2003.
