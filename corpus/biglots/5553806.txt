Big Lots Inc. Names Christopher T. Chapin as New Senior Vice President of Store Operations.
Big Lots Inc. announced the appointment of Christopher T. Chapin as Senior Vice President of Store Operations, effective immediately. Mr. Chapin will report to CEO Steve Fishman. Chapin will oversee store standards, field operations, customer service, process development, and program implementation for the company's 1,300+ closeout retail stores in 47 states. Chapin has more than two decades of senior retail operations experience with leading companies, including Frito-Lay, PepsiCo, YUM Brands, Priceline.com, Limited Brands Inc., and FacilitySource. He most recently served as president of FacilitySource.
