Big Lots Opens $70 Million Distribution Center.
Big Lots Inc. announced the opening of the company's new $70 million, 1.2 million sq. ft. distribution center in Bryan County, Durant, Oklahoma. The center, which is the largest one-time investment in the history of the City of Durant, has created 250 jobs and is the fifth Big Lots distribution center, which collectively service over 1,400 stores across the country. At full capacity the facility could provide as many as 500 jobs to the community. In addition to opening the new distribution center, Big Lots opened a new 37,000 sq. ft. store at 2418 West Main Street, joining 22 other Big Lots stores already in Oklahoma.
