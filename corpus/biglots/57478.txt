Big Lots Reports Fourth Quarter and year ended fiscal 2002 Sales ;confirmed 2002 First Call EPS guidance.
"Big Lots Inc. announced that sales for the fourth quarter ended 2002 $1,138,214,000 and $1,111,936,000 for 2001. Net income for the fourth quarter ended 2002 $6,531,000 or $0.06 and $95,222,000 or $0.85 for 2001. The Company has confirmed 2002 First Call EPS guidance of $0.35 to $0.40. These EPS estimates are based on a comparable store sales increase of 3%-4%, with total sales up in the range of 7%-8%."
