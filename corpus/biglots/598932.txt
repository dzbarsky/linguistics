Big Lots Reports Sales for Five Weeks, Nine Weeks, Twenty Two Weeks Ended July 3, 2004; Provides Outlook for July, Second and Third Quarter of 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the five weeks ended July 3, 2004 of $381.8 million, a 2.2% increase against retail sales of $373.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 2.4% in June with the value of the average basket increasing 0.4% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 2.8%. For the twenty-two week year to date period ended July 3, 2004, retail sales increased 7.3% to $1,712.2 million, compared to $1,595.7 million for the same period in fiscal 2003.
