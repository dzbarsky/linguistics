Big Lots Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended November 1, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2009.
"Big Lots Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended November 1, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported income from continuing operations of $12.4 million or $0.15 per diluted share compared to income from continuing operations of $14.4 million or $0.14 per diluted share in the third quarter of fiscal 2008. Including the impact of discontinued operations, net income totaled $12.2 million or $0.15 per diluted share compared to $14.3 million or $0.14 per diluted share in the prior year. Net sales decreased 0.9% to $1,021.6 million, compared to $1,030.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2008. For the year to date, income from continuing operations totaled $73.0 million or $0.89 per diluted share compared to income from continuing operations of $65.5 million or $0.61 per diluted share for the same period in fiscal 2008.
