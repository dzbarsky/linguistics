Big Lots will Open New Store.
Big Lots Inc. will open a store Wednesday in the former Gordmans building at 132nd Street and West Center Road in the Orchard West Shopping Center. At 30,400 square feet, the new store at 2502 S. 133rd Plaza is the largest Big Lots in the state and features a furniture section, said Pat Zeigler, a spokeswoman for the Columbus, Ohio-based chain.
