Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended August 1, 2009.
"Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the second quarter and six months ended August 1, 2009. For the second quarter, the company's retail sales decreased 2.3% to $1,071.2 million, compared to $1,095.9 million for the second quarter of fiscal 2008. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 2.4% for the second quarter of fiscal 2009. This result is consistent with company's previously communicated guidance issued on May 28, 2009 which called for a comparable store sales decrease in the range of 1% to 3%. For the six months ended August 1, 2009, retail sales decreased 1.5% to $2,200.6 million, compared to $2,234.9 million for the same period in fiscal 2008. Comparable store sales decreased 1.5% for the year to date period.
