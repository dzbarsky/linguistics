Big Lots Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended August 1, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter, Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2009.
Big Lots Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended August 1, 2009. The company reported second quarter fiscal 2009 income from continuing operations of $28.6 million, or $0.35 per basic and diluted share, compared to income from continuing operations of $26.1 million, or $0.32 per basic and diluted share, in the second quarter of fiscal 2008. Including the impact of discontinued operations, second quarter fiscal 2009 net income totaled $28.4 million, or $0.34 per diluted share, compared to $26.0 million, or $0.32 per basic and diluted share, in the prior year. Net sales for the second quarter of fiscal 2009 decreased 1.7% to $1,086.6 million, compared to $1,105.2 million for the same period in fiscal 2008. Operating profit for the second quarter of fiscal 2009 was $47.7 million, or 4.4% of sales, compared to last year's operating profit of $43.5 million, or 3.9% of sales.
