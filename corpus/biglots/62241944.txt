Big Lots Inc. Reports Capital Expenditure for the Second Quarter of 2009 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2009.
"Big Lots Inc. reported capital expenditure for the second quarter of 2009. For the second quarter, capital expenditures totaled $26 million, down $8.6 million compared to the second quarter of last year. The company increased guidance for the full year. The company now expects CapEx for the year to be in the range of $85 to $90 million, an increase of approximately $5 million compared to prior guidance of $80 to $85 million."
