Big Lots Inc. Announces New Unsecured Revolving Credit Facility.
Big Lots Inc. announced the signing of a new three-year revolving credit facility consisting of a syndicate of 14 participating banks. New facility is unsecured and provides a $500 million committed credit line and replaces the prior $500 million credit facility which was set to expire in October 2009. The facility was arranged jointly by PNC Capital Markets and Wells Fargo Bank. The new facility will be used to fund working capital needs and for general corporate purposes and can be drawn upon and repaid at the Company's discretion.
