Big Lots Reports Results for the Four Weeks and Thirty Weeks Ended August 28, 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended August 28, 2004 of $289.2 million, a 2.9% increase against retail sales of $281.0 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 2.0% in August with the value of the average basket flat against last year and the number of customer transactions decreasing 2.0%. For the thirty-week year to date period ended August 28, 2004, retail sales increased 5.9% to $2,281.4 million, against $2,154.0 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales increased 1.0% for the year to date period with the value of the average basket increasing 2.1% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 1.1%."
