Big Lots Reports Comparable Store Sales for the First Quarter of 2009.
Big Lots Inc. reported first quarter retail sales for the fiscal quarter ended May 2, 2009 decreased 0.8% to $1,129.4 million, compared to $1,139.0 million for the first quarter of fiscal 2008. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 0.5% for the first quarter of fiscal 2009.
