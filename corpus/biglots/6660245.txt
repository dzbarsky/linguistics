Big Lots Inc. Announces Unaudited Condensed Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended May 2, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2010.
Big Lots Inc. announced unaudited condensed consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended May 2, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $36.2 million, or $0.44 per diluted share. This compares to net income of $34.5 million, or $0.42 per diluted share, for the first quarter of fiscal 2008. Income from continuing operations was $36.3 million, or $0.44 per diluted share, compared to income from continuing operations of $34.5 million, or $0.42 per diluted share, for the same period of fiscal 2008. Net sales decreased 0.9% to $1,141.7 million, compared to $1,151.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2008. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 0.5% for the quarter. Operating profit was $60.3 million, or 5.3% of sales, compared to first quarter of fiscal 2008 operating profit of $58.2 million, or 5.1% of sales. Net cash provided by operating activities was $97.87 million compared to $58.64 million for the same period
