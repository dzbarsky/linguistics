Big Lots Reports Capital Expenditure for the First Quarter of 2009.
Big Lots Inc. reported capital expenditures totaled $12.9 million for the first quarter of 2009 down $5.4 million compared to the first quarter of 2008. The decrease in CapEx in first quarter is primarily related to lower IT spending partially offset by more new store openings in first quarter of 2009 against 2008.
