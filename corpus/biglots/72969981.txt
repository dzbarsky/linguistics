Big Lots Inc. Reports Capital Expenditure for the Second Quarter of 2009; Revised Cash Flow Outlook for the Year 2009; Plans to Open Approximately 50 Stores in 2009.
"Big Lots Inc. reported that for the second quarter of 2009, capital expenditures totaled $26 million, down $8.6 million compared to the second quarter of last year. The company expects to open approximately 50 stores in 2009 compared to its original plan of 45."
