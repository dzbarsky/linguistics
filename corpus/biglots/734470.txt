Big Lots Inc. Reports Results for the Four Weeks and Thirty Nine Weeks Ended October 30, 2004 ; Company Completes $500 Million Five-Year Unsecured Revolving Credit Facility.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended October 30, 2004 of $317.7 million, a 5.5% increase against retail sales of $301.1 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year were flat in October with the value of the average basket increasing 2.3% against last year and the number of customer transactions decreasing 2.3%. The Company announced the completion of a new, 5-year, $500 million revolving credit facility and retirement of its $204 million of senior notes. The new bank facility is unsecured and replaces the prior $300 million secured"
