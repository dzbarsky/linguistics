Big Lots Inc. Reports Third Quarter and Nine Months results for the Period Ended October 30, 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported third quarter and nine months results for the Period ended October 30, 2004. for the third quarter the company reported net loss was $31.5 million, or $0.28 per share on net sales of $980 million compared to a net loss of $6.4 million, or $0.05 per share on net sales of $948.1 million for the same period of fiscal 2003. For the year to date period ended October 30, 2004, the net loss was $32.0 million, or $0.28 per share, over net sales of $2.99 billion compared to a net loss of $4.2 million, or $0.04 per share, on net sales of $2.84 billion for same period in fiscal 2003."
