Big Lots and Boys & Girls Clubs of America Celebrate the Opening of New Learning Center in Metro Atlanta.
On April 9,Big Lots Inc. celebrated the opening of a vibrant new learning center at the Samuel L. Jones Boys & Girls Club of Metro Atlanta. The development of the learning center is part of Big Lots' three year, seven figure gift to B&GCA. Big Lots, the nation's largest broadline closeout retailer joined forces with B&GCA in early 2002 to help foster positive life values for the nation's youth. As part of Big Lots' commitment to B&GCA, the company launched a program called Values for Life which includes the construction of learning centers, educational grants and a series of learning guides on various life skills and learning topics.
