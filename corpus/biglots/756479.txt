Big Lots Promotes Charles W. Haubiel II to Senior Vice President, General Counsel and Corporate Secretary.
Big Lots Inc. has announced the promotion of Charles W. Haubiel II, to Senior Vice President, General Counsel and Corporate Secretary. Mr. Haubiel will also serve on the company's Executive Committee. Haubiel joined Big Lots in 1997 as Senior Staff Counsel and was later promoted to Director, Corporate Counsel. In 2000, he was named Vice President and General Counsel. Prior to joining Big Lots, Haubiel practiced with the law firm of Vorys, Sater, Seymour & Pease LLP, in Columbus, Ohio.
