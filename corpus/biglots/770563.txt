Big Lots Inc. Reports Retail Sales Results for the Month and Year to Date Ended November 27, 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended November 27, 2004 of $444.4 million, a 0.7% decrease compared to retail sales of $447.4 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 6.1% in November with the value of the average basket decreasing 0.9% compared to last year and the number of customer transactions decreasing 5.2%. For the forty-three week year-to-date period ended November 27, 2004, retail sales increased 4.6% to $3,401.1 million, compared to $3,250.1 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales decreased 0.3% for the year-to-date period with the value of the average basket increasing 1.8% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 2.1%."
