Big Lots Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 31, 2009 ; Increases Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2010, Expects to Close a Total of 30 Locations and to Increase 2% Store Count in 2009.
Big Lots Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended October 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company has reported operating profit of $47,577,000, income from continuing operations before income taxes of $47,084,000, income from continuing operations of $30,256,000 or $0.37 per diluted share and net income of $30,329,000 or $0.37 per diluted share on net sales of $1,035,269,000 compared to operating profit of $20,125,000, income from continuing operations before income taxes of $18,500,000, income from continuing operations of $12,358,000 or $0.15 per diluted share and net income of $12,248,000 or $0.15 per diluted share on net sales of $1,021,580,000 for the same period last year. On non-GAAP, the company has reported operating profit of $34,613,000, income from continuing operations of $22,093,000 or $0.27 per diluted share and net income of $22,166,000 or $0.27 per diluted share.
