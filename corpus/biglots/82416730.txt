Big Lots Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $150 mm.
The Board of Directors of Big Lots Inc. (NYSE:BIG) authorized a share repurchase program on December 4, 2009. Under the program, Big Lots will repurchase up to $150 million of its shares from time to time in the open market and/or in privately negotiated transactions at the Company's discretion. The repurchases will be subject to market conditions and other factors. The repurchased shares will be used to meet obligations under equity compensation plans and for general corporate purposes. The Board believes that the size of the new repurchase program fits well within the Company's capital structure and its cash flow potential.
