BG Strategic Advisors, BGSA Supply Chain 2010 Conference, Jan-20-2010 through Jan-22-2010. Venue: The Breakers Hotel, Palm Beach, Florida, United States. The BGSA Supply Chain 2010 Conference is known for many things: Top CEOs. Interactive debates on hot supply chain issues. BGSA Supply Chain provides an intimate setting to explore ideas, learn about industry trends and network with top industry executives. In addition, the conference takes a unique focus on key strategic ideas driving change in the supply chain marketplace, such as consolidation and global expansion, strategic international opportunities and ideas for positioning supply chain businesses for success.
