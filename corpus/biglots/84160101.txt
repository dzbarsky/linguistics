Big Lots Inc. Revised Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ending January 30, 2010.
Big Lots Inc. revised earnings guidance for the fourth fiscal quarter ending January 30, 2010. The company expects a comparable store sales increase in the range of 3.5% to 4.5% compared to previous guidance of an increase of 1.5% to 2.5% communicated on December 4, 2009. Based on this higher level of sales, the company now expect income from continuing operations for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2009 to be in the range of $1.19 to $1.24 per diluted share compared to previous guidance of income from continuing operations of $1.09 to $1.14 per diluted share and last year's record fourth quarter performance of $1.00 per diluted share. Including the revised guidance for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2009, income from continuing operations (on a non-GAAP basis as scheduled below) for fiscal 2009 is now expected to be in the range of $2.25 to $2.30 per diluted share compared to fiscal 2008's record income from continuing operations of $1.89 per diluted share.
