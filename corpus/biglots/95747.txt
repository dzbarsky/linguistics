Big Lots Inc. Announces the Election of Three New Independent Members to the Company's Board of Directors.
Big Lots Inc. announced the election of three new independent members to the Company's Board of Directors. At the Company's 2003 Annual Meeting of Shareholders, Michael J. Potter, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, welcomed Philip E. Mallott (45), Ned Mansour (53), and Russell Solt (55) to the Company's Board of Directors. The three new members are replacing W. Eric Carlborg, Michael L. Glazer, and William A. Wickham, each of whom did not stand for re-election.
