Discovery Holding Co. Announces Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2005; Provides Earnings Guidance for 2005.
"Discovery Holding Co. announced earnings results for the third quarter of 2005. The company's operating income advanced to $166 million from $129 million the year before. Revenue totaled $639 million, up 15% from $557 million a year earlier. Revenue from its U.S. networks, which include the Discovery Channel and Animal Planet, gained 11% to $428 million, while overseas revenue jumped 24% to $181 million. Commerce, education and other revenue rose 155 to $30 million. Annual operating income is forecast to increase by about 10%, with revenue growth in the low to mid teens level."
