Discovery Holding Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2005.
"Discovery Holding Co. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2005. For the quarter, net income fell 28.6% to $11.2 million, or 4 cents a share, from $15.7 million, or 6 cents a share, in the year-ago period. Revenue increased to $174.3 million from $172.9 million in the year-ago period. For the full year, net income fell to $33.3 million, or 12 cents a share, from $66.1 million, or 24 cents a share, in 2004. Revenue for the year increased to $694 million from $631 million in the previous year."
