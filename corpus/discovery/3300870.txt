Discovery Holding Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2006.
Discovery Holding Co. reported earnings results for the third quarter of 2006. The company reported that for the quarter losses totaled $76.6 million, or 27 cents per share, compared with a profit of $1.2 million, or breakeven per share during the same period last year. Revenue edged up about 1% to $169.9 million, from $167.9 million last year.
