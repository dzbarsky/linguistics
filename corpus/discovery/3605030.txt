Discovery Holding Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year December 31, 2006.
"Discovery Holding Co. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year December 31, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported operating loss of $9 million on revenue of $199 million against operating income of $2 million on revenue of $174 million for the same period a year ago. The increases in revenue were due to growth in distribution and advertising revenue across the portfolio. For the year, the company reported operating loss of $115 million on revenue of $688 million against operating loss of $1 million on revenue of $695 million for the same period a year ago."
