Discovery Holding Co., Annual General Meeting, May 01, 2007.
"Discovery Holding Co., Annual General Meeting, May 01, 2007, at 09:30 US Mountain Time. Location: Denver Marriott South at Park Meadows, 10345 Park Meadows Drive, Littleton, Co. 80124,United States. Agenda: To vote in the election of Paul A. Gould and M. LaVoy Robison to serve as Class II directors until 2010 annual meeting of stockholders; to consider and vote upon a proposal to ratify the selection of KPMG LLP as independent auditors for 2007; and to transact any other business as may properly come before the annual meeting."
