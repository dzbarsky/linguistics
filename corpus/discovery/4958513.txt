Discovery Holding Co. Announces Earnings Results for the Third Quarter Ended September 30, 2007.
Discovery Holding Co. announced earnings results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2007. Consolidated revenue of the company increased $8 million, or 5% to $178 million from $170 million revenue reported in the same quarter last year. Operating loss for the quarter is $2 million compared to operating loss of $97 million for the same period last year.
