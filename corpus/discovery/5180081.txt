Discovery Holding Co. Reports Sales Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2007.
"Discovery Holding Co. reported sales results for the fourth quarter and full year of 2007. For the quarter, sales slipped 11% or $21 million to $178 million against $199 million reported in 2006. For the full year of 2007, sales rose 3% or $19 million to $707 million."
