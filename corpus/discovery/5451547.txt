Discovery Holding Co. Announces Earnings Results for the First Quarter of 2008.
Discovery Holding Co. announced earnings results for the first quarter of 2008. For the quarter, the company reported that profit jump 33% due to soaring earnings at its cable television network subsidiary that runs the Discovery channels. The company reported $29.8 million in first-quarter profit, or 12 cents per share on $189.3 million revenue. That compares to $22.3 million profit, or 7 cents per share, on $173.9 million in the first quarter of 2007.
