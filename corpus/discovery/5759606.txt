Discovery Holding Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter of 2008.
Discovery Holding Co. reported earnings results for the second quarter of 2008. For the period, it's chain of television networks had revenue of $863 million, up 10% from a year earlier. Excluding the Travel Channel, which was traded to Cox Communications Inc. in May 2007, revenue rose 13% and operating income before depreciation and amortization increased 22% to $315 million.
