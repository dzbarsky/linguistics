Discovery Holding Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2007.
"Discovery Holding Co. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported operating loss of $162 million compared to $9 million for the same period in previous year. For the year, the company reported operating loss of $168 million compared to $115 million for the last year."
