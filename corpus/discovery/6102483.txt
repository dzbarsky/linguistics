Discovery Communications Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 11, 2009.
Discovery Communications Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 11, 2009, at 10:00 US Eastern Time. Location: One Discovery Place, Silver Spring, MD 20910,United States. Agenda: To approve the election of directors; to ratify the appointment of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as company's independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2009, and to consider such other matters that may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment or adjournments thereof.
