Discovery Communications Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Ending December 31, 2009.
"Discovery Communications Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company's revenues of $881 million were down slightly compared with the second quarter a year ago as 2% growth at U.S. Net income available to shareholders of company of $183 million or $0.43 diluted per share increased $140 million compared to $43 million or $0.16 diluted per share for the second quarter a year ago. The increased results primarily reflect the $45 million growth in Adjusted OIBDA and a net of tax gain of $46 million as a result of the sale of 50% of the Discovery Kids channel. Income before income taxes were $446 million compared to $164 million for the same period a year ago. Operating income was $486 million compared to $208 million for the same period a year ago.
