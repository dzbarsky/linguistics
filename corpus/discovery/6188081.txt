Discovery Communications Inc. Promotes Rebecca Glashow to Senior Vice President, Digital Media Distribution.
Discovery Communications Inc. has promoted Rebecca Glashow to senior vice president, Digital Media Distribution. Glashow reports to Bill Goodwyn, president, Domestic Distribution and Enterprises. In her new role, Glashow remains charged with creating and executing opportunities to expand Discovery's digital footprint and support the evolving cable business. Asserting Discovery's leadership in the space, she will continue to work with operators to drive the growth of their VOD, interactive TV and internet services as well as broker broadband and mobile deals for content from across Discovery's family of U.S. networks. Prior to joining Discovery, Glashow was director, video-on-demand and interactive television, content acquisitions for Comcast Cable Communications.
