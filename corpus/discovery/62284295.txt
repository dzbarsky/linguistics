Discovery Communications, Inc. (NasdaqGS: DISC.A) completed the acquisition of additional 25% stake in Animal Planet LLC from BBC Worldwide Ltd..
"Discovery Communications agreed to acquire additional 25% stake in Animal Planet LLC from BBC Worldwide Ltd. for $80 million on April 30, 2004. Discovery Communications Inc. (NasdaqGS: DISC.A) completed the acquisition of additional 25% stake in Animal Planet LLC from BBC Worldwide Ltd. on April 3, 2006."
