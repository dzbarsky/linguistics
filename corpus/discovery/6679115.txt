Discovery Communications Inc. Appoints Brent Spitzer as Vice President, Digital Advertising Sales, Eastern Region.
Discovery Communications Inc. announced the appointment of Brent Spitzer to the role of Vice President, Digital Advertising Sales, Eastern Region. Spitzer will be based at the company's advertising sales headquarters in New York, and will report to Kathleen Kayse, Executive Vice President, Digital Advertising Sales. In his new position, Spitzer will manage and develop Discovery's digital ad sales business across the Eastern region through the use of Discovery's set of multi-platform assets. He will also be responsible for the creation of digital ad sales offerings and the development of strategic plans. Spitzer joins Discovery Communications from Platform A where he served as Vice President, Northeast Sales.
