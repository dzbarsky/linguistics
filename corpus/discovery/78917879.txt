Crave Entertainment Group Enters into an Agreement with Discovery Communications.
Crave Entertainment Group Inc. has entered into an agreement with Discovery Communications Inc. to develop and produce video games based on two of Discovery Channel's television shows. Both Discovery Channel titles are slated to appear on PlayStation (R)3 system, Nintendo Wii(TM) system, Nintendo DS(TM) system. Man vs. Wild will appear on the Xbox 360 system. Digital downloadable versions will also be available on the PlayStation (R) Network and Xbox Live.
