Gaiam Inc. and Discovery Communications, Inc. Release Five Hit Titles from Discovery Channel, TLC and Animal Planet in February.
Gaiam Inc. announced the release of five hit titles in February under their exclusive home video license agreement with Discovery Communications, Inc. The launch includes TLC's 18 Kids and Counting: Season 2, Animal Planet's Jockeys Season 2 and T-Rex: A Dinosaur in Hollywood, as well as Medicine Men Go Wild, and Who Was Jesus? from Discovery Channel.
