Discovery Networks Asia-Pacific Launches Discovery Science and Discovery Turbo in India.
Discovery Networks Asia-Pacific, a division of Discovery Communications announced the launch of its two new channels Discovery Science and Discovery Turbo in India. The new networks will initially air on the Tata Sky DTH platform, but will soon be available on both analog and digital platforms across the country. Broadcast in English, the 24-hour channels will bring a wide range of compelling, distinct and ground-breaking content, enhancing the television viewing experience for viewers. Discovery Science will provide a stimulating look at the impact of science and technology on everyday lives through a range of entertaining programmes focused on cutting-edge scientific research and discoveries, human body, astronomy, genetics, exploration, medical advances and space.
