Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2007.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2007. For the quarter the company reported a net earnings of $83.399 millions or $0.37 diluted earnings per share on revenue of $1,369.062 millions as compare to a net earnings of $106.371 million or $0.61 diluted earnings per share on revenue of $2,354.499 millions for the same period year ago.
