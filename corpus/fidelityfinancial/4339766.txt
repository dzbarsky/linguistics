Fidelity National Financial Inc. Announces Executive Changes.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced the promotion of Alan L. Stinson, currently Co-Chief Operating Officer, to the position of Chief Executive Officer. Raymond R. Quirk, currently Co-Chief Operating Officer and Brent B. Bickett, currently President, have been named Co- Presidents. Each of these executives has been a long-time member of the FNF senior management team. William P. Foley, II will continue as Chairman of the Board of Directors, actively focusing on strategic issues and acquisitions.
