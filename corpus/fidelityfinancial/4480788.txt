Thomas H. Lee Partners and Fidelity National Financial Inc. Receive $3.7 Billion in Financing.
The proposed buyers of Ceridian Corp. recently received a commitment for $3.7 billion of debt financing. According to a filing with SEC, Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P. and Fidelity National Financial Inc. received the funds to aid them in their $5.3 billion purchase of Ceridian. Deutsche Bank and Credit Suisse Group have pledged to provide a $2 billion term loan and a $300 million revolving credit facility. The filing also detailed that Ceridian is to receive $1.4 billion of bridge loans that will be used in the event it doesn't complete the sale of bonds before the consummation of the buyout.
