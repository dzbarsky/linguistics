Fidelity National Financial Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2007.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $84.8 million or $0.38 per diluted share on total revenue of $1.49 billion against net earnings of $125.1 million or $0.56 per diluted share on total revenues of $1.66 billion for the same period last year. For the six months, the company reported net earnings of $168.2 million or $0.75 per diluted share on total revenue of $2.86 billion against net earnings of $223.9 million or $1.01 per diluted share on total revenues of $3.17 billion for the same period last year."
