Fidelity National Financial, Inc. signed a definitive agreement to acquire Access Cash International aka ATM Holdings, Inc..
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) signed a definitive agreement to acquire Access Cash International aka ATM Holdings, Inc. on August 1, 2007. The transaction is subject to customary closing conditions, including the receipt of Hart Scott Rodino approval and is expected to close before the end of the third quarter of 2007.
