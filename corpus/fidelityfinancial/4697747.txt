Fidelity National Financial, Inc. agreed to acquire Property Insight LLC from Fidelity National Information Services Inc. for $95 million..
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) agreed to acquire Property Insight LLC from Fidelity National Information Services Inc. (NYSE: FIS) for $95 million on August 2, 2007. The Board of Directors of Fidelity National Financial and Fidelity National Information Services approved the transaction. The transaction is subject to certain regulatory approvals and customary closing conditions and is expected to be close by the third quarter of 2007.
