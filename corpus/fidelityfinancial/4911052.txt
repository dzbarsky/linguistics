Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2007.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $6,472,000 or $0.03 diluted earnings per share on total revenues of $1,364,169,000 against net earnings of $127,571,000 or $0.73 diluted earnings per share on total revenues of $2,634,822,000 for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported net earnings of $174,706,000 or $0.79 diluted earnings per share on total revenues of $4,227,908,000 against net earnings of $366,563,000 or $2.11 diluted earnings per share on total revenues of $7,634,090,000 for the same period a year ago."
