Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P. and Fidelity National Financial, Inc. completed the acquisition of Ceridian Corporation..
"Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P. and Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) signed a definitive merger agreement to acquire Ceridian Corporation (NYSE:CEN) on May 30, 2007 in a transaction valued at $5.32 billion in cash. Under the terms of the agreement, Ceridian shareholders will receive $36.00 per share in cash. As on December 31, 2006 Ceridian had 9.376 million options outstanding at a weighted average exercise price of $20.03. This values the option at $149.73 million. The termination fee is $165 million to be paid by Ceridian if the transaction is terminated under certain circumstances. THL Partners and FNF expect to bring co-investors into the transaction. FNF will own less than 50% of Ceridian at closing and will treat the Ceridian investment under the equity method of accounting. Deutsche Bank Securities Inc. and Credit Suisse are providing firm financing commitments to THL Partners and FNF. The transaction is subject to certain closing conditions, including the approval of Ceridian's shareholder"
