Fidelity National Financial Inc. Announces Board Changes.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. appointed Willie M. Davis, John F. Farrell, Jr., Phillip G. Heasley, William A. Imparato, Donald M. Koll, General William Lyon and Frank P. Willey to serve on its Board of Directors, effective as of October 18, 2005. Messrs. Farrell, Heasley and Imparato were appointed to serve on the Audit Committee of the Board until the next regular quarterly meeting of the Board, which is scheduled to be held on October 25, 2005. None of the other newly appointed directors were appointed to any committees of the Board.
