Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Twelve-Months Ended December 31, 2007.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and twelve-months ended December 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $44.9 million or $0.21 loss per diluted share on total revenue of $1.30 billion against net income of $71.2 million or $0.34 per diluted share on total revenue of $1.80 billion a year ago. For the year, the company reported net income of $129.8 million or $0.59 per diluted share on total revenue of $5.52 billion against net income of $437.8 million or $2.39 per diluted share on total revenue of $9.44 billion a year ago."
