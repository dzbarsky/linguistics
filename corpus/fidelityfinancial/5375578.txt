Fidelity National Financial Inc. Appoints Brent B. Bickett to Executive Vice President, Corporate Finance.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced that effective April 1, 2008, Brent B. Bickett agreed to change his title from Co-President to Executive Vice President, Corporate Finance, in recognition of his ongoing management responsibilities at Fidelity National Information Services Inc., where Mr. Bickett serves as Executive Vice President, Strategic Planning. Mr. Bickett is a long-time member of the senior management teams at FNF and FIS.
