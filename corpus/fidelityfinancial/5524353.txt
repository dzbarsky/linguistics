"UnitedHealth Group (NYSE: UNH) agreed to acquired 20%stake in Sedgwick Claims Management Services, Inc. from Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF), Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P., Evercore Capital Partners and David A. North, minority shareholders, on June 2, 2008. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The transaction is subject to antitrust regulations. After this purchase, UnitedHealth Group will own approximately 20% of Sedgwick CMS, and FNF will own approximately 32% of Sedgwick CMS. As on June 4, 2008, the transaction was approved by antitrust regulations.
