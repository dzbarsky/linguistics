Fidelity National Title Group Inc. Announces Board Changes.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced that on April 19, 2006, Robert M. Clements was elected to the Board of Directors of Fidelity National Title Group Inc. Mr. Clements is the Chairman and CEO of EverBank Financial Corp. Mr. Clements joined EverBank in 1994 and has served as the President and CEO of EverBank Financial Corporation since its formation in 1997. On April 19, 2006, Peter O. Shea, Jr. was elected to the Board of Directors of FNT. Mr. Shea is the President and Chief Executive Officer of J.F. Shea Co. Inc. Mr. Shea was not appointed to any committees of the Board of Directors. On April 19, 2006, Donald M. Koll resigned from the Board of Directors of FNT.
