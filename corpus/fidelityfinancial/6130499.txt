Fidelity National Financial Inc. Opens New FNF 1031 Exchange Company Offices.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced the expansion of FNF 1031 Exchange company with the opening of offices in Chicago, New York and Boston. 1031 industry expert and attorney Scott Nathanson will manage the new offices, joined by 1031 veterans Anna Barsky in Chicago, Nicole De Santis and Mathew Narby in New York and Edward Zaval and Mary Lambert in Boston.
