Fidelity National Financial, Inc. announces an Equity Buyback.
The Board of Directors of Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE:FNF) authorized a share repurchase program on July 21, 2009. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to 15,000,000 shares over a 3-year period. The purchases will be made in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions, depending on market conditions and other factors.
