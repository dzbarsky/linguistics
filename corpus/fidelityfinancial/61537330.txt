Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported earnings from continuing operations before taxes of $131,191,000, net earnings from continuing operations of $92,536,000 and net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $91,943,000 or $0.40 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,569,712,000 against earnings from continuing operations before taxes of $16,475,000, net earnings from continuing operations of $7,883,000 and net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $6,925,000 or $0.03 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,168,068,000 for the same period a year ago. Cash flow from operations for the period was $154.8 million against $12.8 million for the same period a year ago.
