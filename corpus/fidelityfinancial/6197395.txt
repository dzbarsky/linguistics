Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Twelve-Months Ended December 31, 2008.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and twelve-months ended December 31, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $1.7 million or $0.01 loss per diluted share on total revenue of $1.02 billion against net loss of $44.9 million or $0.21 loss per diluted share on total revenue of $1.30 billion a year ago. For the full year, the company reported net loss of $165.8 million or $0.79 per diluted share on total revenue of $4.33 billion against net income of $129.8 million or $0.59 per diluted share on total revenue of $5.52 billion a year ago."
