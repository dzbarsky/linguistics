Fidelity National Financial Inc. Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2009.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. provided earnings guidance for the first quarter ended March 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company expects pre tax loss in between of $0.3 million to $10.5 million or $0.06 loss per share to $0.10 loss per share. The company expects total title and escrow revenue of $1,246.1 million.
