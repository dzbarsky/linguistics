Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Names New Board Members; Reports Earnings Results for the Full Year of 2008.
"Fidelity National Financial, Inc.'s shareholders approved Frank P. Willey and Willie D. Davis to a three year term on its board ending in 2012. Both men served as directors on the board after the old FNF spin-off in October 2005. Willey is also vice chairman of the company and was president and vice chairman on the board of the former FNF. Davis is president and director of All-Pro Broadcasting Inc. The company reported a net loss of $179 million in 2008 compared with a net income of $130 million the previous year."
