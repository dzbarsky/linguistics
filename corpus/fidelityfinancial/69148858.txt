"Winthrop Resources Corporation acquired Fidelity National Capital, Inc. from Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) for approximately $280 million in cash on September 25, 2009. Total net proceeds to FNF from the sale of Fidelity National Capital stock, after the repayment of certain Fidelity National Capital debt, were approximately $49.2 million. Winthrop Resources Corporation completed the acquisition of Fidelity National Capital, Inc. from Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) on September 25, 2009."
