Ecosphere Energy Service, Inc. announced that it has received $7.5 million in funding from "Fidelity National Financial, Inc..
"Ecosphere Energy Service, Inc. announced that it has raised $7.5 million in a round of funding on November 10, 2009. The round included participation from other new investor Fidelity National Financial, Inc. After the closing of the transaction, Fidelity National Financial, Inc. holds 19% stake in the company. On November 10, 2009, Ecosphere Energy Service, Inc. closed the transaction."
