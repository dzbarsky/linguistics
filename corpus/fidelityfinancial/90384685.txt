Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Reports Unaudited Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2009.
"Fidelity National Financial, Inc. reported unaudited earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported earnings from continuing operations before taxes of $106,004,000, earnings from continuing operations of $69,536,000 and net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $69,322,000 or $0.30 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,455,287,000 against loss from continuing operations before taxes of $20,986,000, loss from continuing operations of $14,014,000 and net loss attributable to common shareholders of $14,884,000 or $0.07 per diluted share on total revenue of $998,844,000 for the same period a year ago. Cash flow from operations was $12.6 million against $60.5 million a year ago.
