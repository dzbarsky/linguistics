Fidelity National Information Services Inc. announced it will receive $250 million of funding from Fidelity National Financial, Inc. and Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P..
Fidelity National Information Services Inc. (NYSE: FIS) announced a private placement of 16,000,000 common shares at $15.625 per share with affiliates of Thomas H. Lee Partners L.P. and Fidelity National Financial, Inc. for gross proceeds of $250,000,000 on March 31, 2009. As part of the transaction, Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P has the right to designate one member to the companys board of directors.
