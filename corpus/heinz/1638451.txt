HJ Heinz Co. Announces the Promotion of Jeffrey P. Berger to Executive Vice President.
Jeffrey P. Berger, 55, has been promoted to Executive Vice President of the H.J. Heinz Company. In this position, Mr. Berger will be responsible for the company's global foodservice business. As executive vice president, Mr. Berger will retain his responsibilities as president and CEO of Heinz North America Foodservice. He will be based at the Heinz 57 Center offices in downtown Pittsburgh.
