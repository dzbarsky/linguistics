HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter Ended April 27, 2005 ; Provides Earning Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2006.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter ended April 27, 2005. For the period, the company reported net income of $206.487 million or $0.58 diluted earnings per share on sales of $2,448.492 million compared to net income of $196.524 million or $0.55 diluted earnings per share on sales of $2,331.372 million for the same period ended April 28, 2004. HJ Heinz Co. provided earnings guidance for the fiscal 2006. The company earnings per shares is expected to be in the range of $2.35 to $2.45."
