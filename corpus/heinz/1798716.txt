HJ Heinz Appoints Paul Kaye as Top Marketer.
HJ Heinz Co. has appointed Paul Kaye as its top marketer in the wake of the advertising budget freeze (MW July 21) and the departure of brand vice- president Scott Garrett. Kaye, vice-president of quick-serve meals at Heinz, has been handed responsibility for overseeing all marketing and the sales services team at HJ Heinz as the company restructures its marketing department and conducts a review of its brand portfolio.
