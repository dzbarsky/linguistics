HJ Heinz Co. Announces Group Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended July 27, 2005.
HJ Heinz Co. announced consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended July 27, 2005. For the quarter, the group reported net income of $157,274,000 or diluted EPS of $0.45 on sales of $2,110,153,000 against net income of $194,836,000 or diluted EPS of $0.55 on sales of $2,003,026,000 for the same quarter a year ago. The group reported operating income of $281,202,000 against operating income of $339,654,000 for the same quarter a year ago.
