HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividend on Common Stock and Preferred Stock Payable on October 1 and October 10, 2005.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The company declared 30.0 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable October 10, 2005 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 23, 2005, 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable October 1, 2005 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 23, 2005.
