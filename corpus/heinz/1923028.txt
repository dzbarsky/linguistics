HJ Heinz Co. Announces Karen L. Alber Is Joining the Company as Vice President & Chief Information Officer.
HJ Heinz Co. has announced that Karen L. Alber is joining the company as Vice President & Chief Information Officer. Ms. Alber joins Heinz after 19 years of experience in Supply Chain Management, Systems Implementation, Operations, Process Improvement, and Change Management. She most recently was employed at PepsiCo, where she served as Vice President, Change Readiness-Enterprise Systems.
