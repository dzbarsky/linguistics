HJ Heinz Co. acquired Delimex Holdings, Inc. from Fenway Partners Inc..
"HJ Heinz Co. (NYSE:HNZ) announced the acquisition of Delimex Holdings, Inc. from Fenway Partners Inc. on August 2, 2001. Terms of the transaction were not disclosed. UBS Investment Bank provided financial advice to Fenway Partners Inc. and Delimex Holdings, Inc. HJ Heinz Co. completed the acquisition of Delimex Holdings, Inc. on August 2, 2001."
