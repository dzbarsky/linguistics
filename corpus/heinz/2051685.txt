Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Vlasic Foods International, Inc.; Buyer(s): HJ Heinz Co.; Transaction Size: $678.04 mm.
Vlasic Foods International will sell Vlasic pickles, Open Pit barbecue sauce to H.J. Heinz Company for $195 million in a cash deal. Annual sales of Vlasic pickles and Open Pit barbecue sauce are $275 million. The acquisition is subject to bankruptcy court and regulatory approvals.
