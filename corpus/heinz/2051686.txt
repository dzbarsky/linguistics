Cancelled Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Vlasic Foods International, Inc.; Buyer(s): HJ Heinz Co.; Transaction Size: $678.04 mm.
"Vlasic Foods International will sell Vlasic pickles, Open Pit barbecue sauce to H.J. Heinz Company for $195 million in a cash deal. Annual sales of Vlasic pickles and Open Pit barbecue sauce are $275 million. The acquisition is subject to bankruptcy court and regulatory approvals. During 2001 Vlasic foods International announced that the aggrement to sell its ""Vlasic"" pickles and condiments and ""Open Pit"" barbecue sauce businesses to the HJ Heinz Company for an approximate adjusted price of between $174 to $179 million has been terminated."
