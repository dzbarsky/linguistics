HJ Heinz Co. (NYSE: HNZ) announced the acquisition of Milnot Holdings Corporation from Madison Dearborn Partners, Inc. in a transaction valued at $185 million on February 28, 2000. The transaction is subject to the normal U.S. regulatory review. On July 7, 2000, the Commissioners of the Federal Trade Commission challenged the company's acquisition of Milnot Holding Corporation. Jonathan Weintraub of UBS Investment Bank, formerly known as Warburg Dillon Reed, was financial advisor of Heinz. David Cohen of Chase JP Morgan Chase & Co. (NYSE:JPM) was financial advisor and Edward Swan of Kirkland & Ellis LLP was legal advisor of Milnot.
