Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Anchor Food Products Inc., Branded Retail Products Business; Buyer(s): HJ Heinz Co.; Seller(s): Anchor Food Products Inc..
Anchor Food Products Inc. agreed to sell its branded retail products business to HJ Heinz Co. As part of the same transaction, Anchor Food Products itself has agreed to be acquired by Mc Cain Foods Ltd. The financial terms of the deal were not disclosed.
