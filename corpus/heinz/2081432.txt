Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Rosetto Foods Inc.; Buyer(s): Hain Celestial Group Inc.; Seller(s): HJ Heinz Co..
"The Hain Celestial Group (Nasdaq: HAIN), announced that it will acquire the Rosetto brand of frozen Italian products from H.J. Heinz Company (NYSE: HNZ). The acquisition is expected to be accretive in the first fiscal year following closing. Terms of the transaction were not disclosed. Cahill Gordon & Reindel LLP acted as the legal advisor for The Hain Celestial Group. The Hain Celestial Group acquired Rosetto brand of frozen Italian products from H.J. Heinz Company on 05/27/2004."
