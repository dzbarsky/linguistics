Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Sonnen Bassermann ; Buyer(s): HJ Heinz Co.; Seller(s): Groupe DANONE.
"H.J. Heinz Co. announced that it has entered into an agreement to acquire Sonnen Bassermann from Groupe DANONE. Terms of the deal were not disclosed. Sonnen Bassermann had 1997 sales of $50 million. Heinz is the $10 billion purveyor of brands such as StarKist, Ore-Ida, 9 Lives and Weight Watchers. Sonnen Bassermann was acquired by H.J. Heinz Co. on 10/26/1998."
