Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: HJ Heinz, Frozen Bakery Products Business; Buyer(s): The Pillsbury Company; Seller(s): HJ Heinz Co.; Transaction Size: $178 mm.
HJ Heinz, announced that it had agreed to sell Heinz Bakery, its frozen bakery products business, to Pillsbury, part of the UK's Diageo group, for $178 million. The business has annual revenues of about $200 million. It will become part of Pillsbury's bakeries and food service unit. UBS Investment Bank acted as financial to HJ Heinz.
