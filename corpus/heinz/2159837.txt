Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Appetizers And, Inc.; Buyer(s): Heinz Foodservice.
"H.J. Heinz Company (NYSE:HNZ) announced that its Foodservice Division has acquired Appetizers And, Inc. Terms of the acquisition were not released. Heinz Foodservice, a division of H.J. Heinz Company, completed the acquisition of Appetizers And, Inc. on 03/22/2005."
