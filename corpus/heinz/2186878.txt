Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: HJ Heinz Co., Sunar Brands; Buyer(s): Hero AG; Seller(s): HJ Heinz Co..
"Hero AG acquired Sunar Brand from HJ Heinz Co. on April 20, 2004. The purchase price related to the transaction was not disclosed. Allen & Overy acted as the legal advisor to HJ Heinz Co. for the transaction. Rothschild & Sons Ltd. was the financial advisor for Hero AG. Hero AG completed the acquisition of Sunar Brand from HJ Heinz Co. on April 20, 2004."
