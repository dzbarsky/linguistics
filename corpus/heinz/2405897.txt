HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividend on Common Stock and Preferred Stock Payable on January 10 and January 1, 2006.
HJ Heinz Co. declared that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The company declared the following dividends- Common Stock of 30.0 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable January 10, 2006 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 23, 2005. Preferred Stock- 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable January 1, 2006 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 23, 2005.
