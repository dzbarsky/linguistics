ED Smith Income Fund Reaches Agreement with HJ Heinz Co. for Early Termination of HP Foods Contract.
ED Smith Income Fund announced that it has reached an agreement with HJ Heinz Co. with respect to the early termination of the contract under which E. D. Smith markets and exclusively distributes HP Foods products in Canada. The contract was originally scheduled to terminate on December 31, 2006. As a result of the terms of this agreement, effective January 1, 2006, the early termination is not expected to negatively impact the 2006 net income that the Company would otherwise have earned under the contract.
