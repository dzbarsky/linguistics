HJ Heinz Co. Announces Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended Oct. 26, 2005.
"HJ Heinz Co. announced consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended Oct. 26, 2005. For the second quarter of 2005, the company reported net income of $203.8 million or $0.60 per diluted share on sales of $2.4 billion, against net income of $199 million or $0.56 per diluted share on sales of $2.2 billion for the same period last year. For the nine months ended Oct. 26, 2005, the company reported net income of $361,095,000 or $1.04 per diluted share on sales of $4,449,001,000 against net income of $393,801,000 or $1.11 per diluted share on sales of $4,202,586,000 for the same period a year ago."
