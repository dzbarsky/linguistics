Heinz to Explore Sale of Hain Celestial Stake.
H.J. Heinz Co. said it plans to explore the sale of about 6.1 million shares it holds in Hain Celestial Group Inc., a maker of natural foods and personal-care products under a plan to focus on its core businesses. "We are now redeploying our assets and concentrating on driving growth and shareholder value in the three core categories of Condiments and Sauces, Meals and Snacks and Infant Nutrition where we have leading brands and world-class capabilities," Heinz said in a statement.
