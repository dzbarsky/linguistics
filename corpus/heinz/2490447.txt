HJ Heinz Co. Promotes David C. Moran to Executive Vice President.
HJ Heinz Co. has announced that it has promoted David C. Moran, 47, to Executive Vice President of the worldwide food marketer. Mr. Moran also serves as CEO and President, U.S. Consumer Products. Prior to leading the U.S. Consumer Products business, Mr. Moran was President of Heinz Sales Company in the United States. Mr. Moran was recently appointed to the board of directors of Imperial Sugar Company. He also is active in the Pittsburgh community, serving as a director of the Pittsburgh Zoo.
