Pacific Equity Partners, Intermediate Capital Group plc, and management of Tegel Foods Limited announced the acquisition of the company from HJ Heinz Co. for NZD 250 million..
Pacific Equity Partners, Intermediate Capital Group plc (LSE: ICP), and management of Tegel Foods Limited entered into an agreement to acquire the company from HJ Heinz Co. (NYSE: HNZ) for NZD 250 million on December 22, 2005. Intermediate Capital Group plc provided senior and junior mezzanine loans in addition to equity in support of Pacific Equity Partners. The transaction is subject to approval of the New Zealand overseas investment office. UBS Investment Bank was the financial advisor of H.J. Heinz Company on the transaction.
