NPM Capital acquired HAK B.V. from  HJ Heinz Co..
"HJ Heinz Co. entered into a letter of intent to sell HAK B.V. to NPM Capital on October 14, 2005. Terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The proposed sale will be subject to regulatory approvals and Dutch Works Council consultation and is expected to close in the next few months. HAK has annual sales of approximately $65 million. UBS Investment Bank acted as exclusive financial adviser to H.J. Heinz on the transaction. NPM Capital acquired HAK B.V. from  HJ Heinz Co. on December 8, 2005."
