Heinz flows past third-quarter of fiscal 2003 earnings mark.
H. J. Heinz Foodservices reported third-quarter net income of $151.6 million, or 43 cents per diluted share, compared with $201.7 million, or 57 cents per diluted share, in the year-ago period. Excluding items, Heinz reported earnings from continuing operations of $191.5 million or 54 cents per diluted share, up from $161.2 million, or 46 cents per diluted share in the year-ago period. The figure of 54 cents per share beat the forecast of 52 cents per share in a survey of analysts by Thomson First Call. Revenue climbed 9.1 percent to $2.11 billion amid higher prices, foreign exchange translation rates and acquisitions.
