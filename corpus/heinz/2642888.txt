Heinz Reportedly Targeted By Activist Billionaire Peltz.
H.J. Heinz Co. may become the next prized target of so-called activist investors who pressure companies and management to improve performance. Billionaire Nelson Peltz is seeking co-investors to purchase a sizeable stake in the maker of ketchup, sauces and packaged foods, CNBC reported on February 21st. Peltz is chairman and chief executive of Triarc Cos., which owns the Arby's Restaurant Group and other restaurants.
