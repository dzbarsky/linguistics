HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter Ended January 25, 2006; Provides Earnings Guidance for Fiscal 2007.
"HJ Heinz Co. announced earnings results for the third quarter ended January 25, 2006. Quarterly net income fell to $116.6 million, or 35 cents per share from $152.4 million, or 43 cents per share, in the year-ago quarter. Revenue rose 6% to $2.19 billion from $2.07 billion the previous year. The company announced that special items in the third quarter for downsizing, integration, separation and preparation for sale totaled $27.9 million. The company also recorded a loss of $19.5 million related to potential sales. For fiscal 2007, the company is projecting sales growth of 3 to 4% and earnings growth also of 6 to 8% from a range of $2.10 to $2.16 per share anticipated for fiscal 2006."
