HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividends, Payable on April 10, 2006 and April 1, 2006.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. On Common Stock, 30.0 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable April 10, 2006 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2006 and on preferred Stock, 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable April 1, 2006 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2006.
