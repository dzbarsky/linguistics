HJ Heinz Co. Plans to Nominate Five Candidates to the Board.
HJ Heinz Co. plans to nominate five candidates to the food company's 12-member board. Trian's nominees include Peltz, who is chief executive of Trian Fund Management; Peter May, president of Trian Fund; and Edward Garden, portfolio manager of Trian Fund. Trian will also nominate Michael Weinstein, chairman of Inov8 Beverage Co., and Gregory Norman, chairman and CEO of Great White Shark Enterprises Inc.
