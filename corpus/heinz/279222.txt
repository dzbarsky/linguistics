Heinz Reports Third-Quarter 2003 Results.
HJ Heinz Co. announced results for the third quarter ended January 29, 2003. Heinz reported net income of $151.6 million, or 43 cents per diluted share and its earnings from continuing operations were $191.5 million or 54 cents per diluted share. The company's third-quarter sales grew by 9.1% to $2.11 billion.
