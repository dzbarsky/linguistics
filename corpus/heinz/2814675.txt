HJ Heinz Co. Reiterates Earnings Guidance for the Year of 2006 and 2007.
"HJ Heinz Co. reiterated earnings guidance for the year of 2006 and 2007. For the year, the company expects a earnings of $2.10 to $2.16 per share. For the year, 2007 the company expects a earnings-per-share growth at the upper end of its range of 6% to 8%. Sales in fiscal 2007 are expected to grow between 3 and 4%, and operating cash flow is expected to total $800 million to $900 million."
