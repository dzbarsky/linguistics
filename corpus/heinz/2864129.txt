H. J. Heinz Company weight control business was acquired by Artal Luxembourg, S.A..
"H. J. Heinz Company (NYSE: HNZ) agreed to sell its weight control business to Artal Luxembourg, S.A. for $735 million. The Invus Group, Ltd. of New York acts as exclusive investment advisor to Artal. Warburg Dillon Read and Davis Polk advised HJ Heinz Co. the parent company of Weight Watcher's International Inc. H. J. Heinz Company weight control business was acquired by Artal Luxembourg, S.A. on September 29, 1999."
