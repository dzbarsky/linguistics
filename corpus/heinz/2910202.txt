HJ Heinz Co. Plans to Close Birmingham Steak-Sauce Factory.
HJ Heinz Co. putting that to the test with plans to shut the Birmingham steak-sauce factory that are creating an uproar. The mainstay of English cuisine was first sold in 1903 and is advertised with the jingle HP sauce is the great British sauce. Heinz, which acquired the plant last year, said it operates three days a week. Workers planto strike and Birmingham's MP Clare Short has joined calls for a boycott.
