H.J. Heinz to Explore Alternatives for Zimbabwean Operations.
H.J. Heinz Company has announced that it has identified its Zimbabwe operations as non-core. The company will explore strategic options to exit this business. Management's determination is based on a current evaluation of political and economic conditions existing in Zimbabwe and the ability for the company to recover its cost in this investment. The company also said that the net loss on dispositions and impairments in anticipation of potential asset sales, shown in financial figures was $174.1 million pre-tax for continuing operations and relates primarily to the write-down of the company's operations in Zimbabwe.
