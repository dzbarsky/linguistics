Hain Celestial Group, Inc. acquired Linda McCartney from H. J. Heinz Company..
"Hain Celestial Group, Inc. acquired Linda McCartney from H. J. Heinz Company on June 13, 2006. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The transaction is expected to be accretive to the earnings of Hain Celestial during its fiscal year 2007. Hain Celestial Group, Inc. completed the acquisition of Linda McCartney from H. J. Heinz Company on June 13, 2006."
