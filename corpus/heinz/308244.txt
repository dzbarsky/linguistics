H. J. Heinz Company Announces Quarterly Dividend for 2002.
The Board of Directors of HJ Heinz Co. at a meeting on December 11, 2002, declared the quarterly dividend of: Common stock: 40 1/2 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable January 10, 2003 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 23, 2002. Prefered stock: 42 1/2 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable January 1, 2003 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 23, 2002.
