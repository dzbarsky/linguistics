HJ Heinz Co. Appoints Jason A. Fincke as New Executive Director.
HJ Heinz Co. has appointed Jason A. Fincke as its new executive director. Fincke replaces John Turyan, former executive from H J Heinz, who is retiring after serving as executive director of the Builders Guild since 1999. For the past 28 years, he has worked in public service with the Port Authority of Allegheny County, including 15 years as a member of the Authority's senior staff, most recently as its chief of staff. Fincke has extensive experience in corporate administration, including communications, public relations and marketing. During his career in public transportation, he was extensively involved in the development of such construction and engineering projects as Port Authority's Light Rail Transit System and extensive busway facilities.
