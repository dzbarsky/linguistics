H. J. Heinz Company Declares Quarterly Dividend payable October 10, 2003.
HJ Heinz Co. declared a quarterly dividend of 27 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable October 10, 2003 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 23, 2003 and 42 1/2 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable October 1, 2003 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 23, 2003.
