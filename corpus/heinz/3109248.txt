HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividends on Common and Preferred Stock Payable on October 10, 2006 and October 1, 2006.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its board of directors has declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. A dividend of $0.35 per share was declared on the company's $0.25 par value common stock payable on October 10, 2006 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 22, 2006. The dividend on the company's third cumulative preferred stock, $1.70 first series, is $0.425 per share, and is payable on October 1, 2006 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 22, 2006.
