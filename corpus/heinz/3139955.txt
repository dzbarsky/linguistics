HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended August 2, 2006 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2006.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended August 2, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported profit jumped 23%, helped by sales of its Weight Watchers, Smart Ones and other brands. Profit for the quarter ended Aug. 2 grew to $194.1 million, or 58 cents per share, compared with a profit of $345.7 million, or 45 cents per share during the same period last year. Revenue climbed 8% to $2.06 billion, from $1.9 billion. The company said it remains on track for 10% plus growth in earnings per share for the fiscal year."
