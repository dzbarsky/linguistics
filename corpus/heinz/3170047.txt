HJ Heinz Co. completed the acquisition of Nancy's Specialty Foods, Inc. from United Signature Foods, Inc..
"HJ Heinz Co. (NYSE:HNZ) announced an agreement to acquire Nancy's Specialty Foods, Inc. from United Signature Foods, Inc. for an undisclosed amount on July 19, 2005. Scott LaRue and John Barrymore of Piper Jaffray & Co. acted as a financial advisor United Signature Foods, Inc. Jeff Knetsch of Brownstein Hyatt & Farber provided legal advice to Kohlberg & Company, L.L.C. HJ Heinz Co. completed the acquisition of Nancy's Specialty Foods, Inc. on July 29, 2005."
