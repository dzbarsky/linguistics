HJ Heinz May Seek Acquisitions In China.
HJ Heinz expects to double its sales in China to an annual $300 million within 5 years, the China Business News reported, citing William Johnson, the president and Chief Executive Officer. Johnson also indicated the company may look for acquisitions or build new factories in the country, though he gave no further details. As of this year, Heinz had invested $800 million in China with its annual sales reaching $150 million.
