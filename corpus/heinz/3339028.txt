HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter Ended November 1, 2006 ; Raises Earnings Outlook for the Fiscal Year 2007.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the second quarter ended November 1, 2006. For the second quarter, the company reported that net income totaled $191.6 million, or 57 cents per share, compared with net income of $203.8 million, or 60 cents per share during the year-ago quarter. Net income from continuing operations was 59 cents per share. Revenue grew nearly 4% to $2.23 billion, from $2.16 billion, helped partly by 9% sales growth of Heinz branded product and 17% growth of Weight Watchers Smart Ones meals. The company said results were hurt by a higher tax rate of 34.8%, compared with 23.8% last year, excluding special items. Heinz raised its fiscal 2007 earnings outlook to a range of $2.35 to $2.39 per share. Heinz expects full-year earnings of about $2.35 per share."
