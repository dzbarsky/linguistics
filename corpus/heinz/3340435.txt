HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Six Months Ended November 1, 2006.
HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the six months ended November 1, 2006. For the period, the company reported net income of $385,676,000 or $1.15 per diluted share on sales of $4,292,145,000 compared to net income of $361,095,000 or $1.04 per diluted share on sales of $4,057,262,000 for the same period year earlier. Sales increased 5.8% favorably impacted by a volume increase of 3.7%. Positive increases were generated in all of the company's segments, led by the North American Consumer Products and U.S. Foodservice segments.
