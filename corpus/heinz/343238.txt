Heinz Reports Second Quarter results for the month ended ocotber 2003.
HJ Heinz Co. reported results for the second quarter 2003. Total revenues for the second quarter 2003 is $2.09 billion compared to $2.09 billion of 2002 for the same period. Net income for the second quarter 2003 is $191.5 million or $0.54 per share compared to $212.1 million or $0.60 per share of 2002 for the same period.
