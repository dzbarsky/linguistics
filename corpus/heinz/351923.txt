Heinz Reports Third Quarter Earnings Of 2002 ; Forecasts Results For the Fourth Quarter.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings of $201.7 million, or 57 cents per share, compared with $270.5 million for the third quarter last year, when earnings were 77 cents a share. The results for the quarter ended Jan. 30 met analysts' reduced expectations. Analysts are forecasting earnings of 62 cents a share for the fourth quarter ending May 1."
