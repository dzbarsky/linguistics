HJ Heinz Co. Announces Management Changes.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that Margaret R. Nollen will join as vice president of investor relations. Nollen will replace Jack Runkel, who has been named vice president of business services. Nollen has more than 20 years of experience in finance and administration. Most recently, she was vice president of investor relations at Georgia-Pacific Corp. before the company was bought and taken private by Koch Industries LLC.
