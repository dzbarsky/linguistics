HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended January 31, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Fiscal 2007.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the nine months ended January 31, 2007. For the period, the company reported net income of $604,714,000 or $1.81 per diluted share on sales of $6,587,337,000 against net income of $477,695,000 or $1.39 per diluted share on sales of $6,243,786,000 for the same period a year ago. Sales were favorably impacted by a volume increase of 2.9%, led by the North American Consumer Products segment and the Australian business. The company provided earnings guidance for the full year fiscal 2007. Full Year EPS Outlook is $2.35 to $2.39, an increase of 12 to 14% versus last year (excluding prior year special items)."
