Heinz North America Appoints David C. Moran as Executive Vice President, President, and Chief Executive Officer.
On March 14, 2007, the Board of Directors determined to create the position of Executive Vice President, President, and Chief Executive Officer of Heinz North America, and appointed David C. Moran, who is currently Executive Vice President and Chief Executive Officer of Heinz Consumer Products, to that position effective May 3, 2007.
