H. J. Heinz Company (NYSE: HNZ) has reached an agreement to purchase UB Frozen and Chilled Foods from United Biscuits on October 22, 1999. The transaction is valued at $317 million. UB Frozen and Chilled Foods has sales of $360 million in 1998. The transaction is subject to clearance under EC Merger Regulation and will be completed by early December. Morgan Stanley Dean Witter & Co. provided financial advice to UB Frozen and Chilled Foods.
