HJ Heinz Co. Appoints Jeffrey P. Berger as Executive Vice President and Chairman - Global Foodservice.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that on May 8, 2007, the Management Development and Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors determined to create the position of Executive Vice President and Chairman - Global Foodservice, and appointed Jeffrey P. Berger, who was serving as Executive Vice President - Global Foodservice, to that position effective May 3, 2007.
