HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2007 ; Announces Quarterly Dividend.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year of fiscal 2007. Quarterly earnings grew to $181 million, or 55 cents per share, from $167.9 million, or 50 cents per share, during the prior-year period. Sales edged up 0.6%, to $2.41 billion from $2.40 billion. The company said its fiscal fourth quarter profit increased 7.8%, boosted by strong sales among the company's top brands. The company also said its quarterly dividend would be raised 8.6%, from 35 cents to 38 cents, for shareholders of record as of June 25, 2007. The increase follows a 16.7% dividend increase in fiscal 2007."
