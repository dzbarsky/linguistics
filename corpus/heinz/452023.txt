Heinz Reports Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended January 28, 2003; Provides Guidance for the Fiscal 2004.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported net income of $202.2 million, or $0.57 per diluted share on sales of $2,097.2 million, for its third quarter, ended January 28, 2004. For the quarter ended January 29, 2003 net income was $151.6 million, or $0.43 per diluted share on sales of $2,105 million. The company is targeting to achieve full-year Fiscal 2004 forecast range of $2.19 to $2.21 in Earnings Per Share, an 8-9% increase over the previous year."
