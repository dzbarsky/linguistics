HJ Heinz Co. closed an Equity Buyback.
"The Board of Directors of HJ Heinz Co. (NYSE: HNZ) authorized a share repurchase program of up to 30,000,000 shares on June 8, 2005. As of January 31, 2007, the company repurchased 25,971,308 shares under this plan. HJ Heinz Co completed its share repurchase program as effective on April 30, 2007. Under the program, the Company has repurchased 30,000,000 shares."
