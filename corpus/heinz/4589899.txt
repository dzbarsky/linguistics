HJ Heinz Co. to Amend the Company's By-laws and Amended and Restated Articles of Incorporation.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that at the AGM to be held on August 15, 2007, to amend the Companys By-Laws and Amended and Restated Articles of Incorporation to reduce the affirmative shareholder vote required to amend or repeal provisions relating to limitation of director liability and director and officer indemnification; To amend the Companys Amended and Restated Articles of Incorporation to reduce the affirmative shareholder vote required to approve certain business combinations with 10% shareholders; To amend the Companys Amended and Restated Articles of Incorporation to require that each director shall be elected by a majority of votes cast.
