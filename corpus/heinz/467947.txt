Heinz Board Declares Quarterly Dividend Payable in month of April 2004.
"The Board of Directors of HJ Heinz Co. declared a quarterly dividend of 27 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable April 10, 2004 to shareholders of record the close of business on March 24, 2004. The Heinz Board of Directors also declared a quarterly dividend of 42 1/2 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable April 1, 2004 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2004."
