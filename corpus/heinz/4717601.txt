HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Preferred and Common Stock Dividends, Payable on October 1 and October 10, 2007 Respectively.
The H. J. Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. 38.0 cents per share on the Company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable on October 10, 2007 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 21, 2007. 42.5 cents per share on the Company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable October 1, 2007 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 21, 2007.
