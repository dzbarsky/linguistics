HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended August 1, 2007.
HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended August 1, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported that sales increased sales increased 9.1% to $2.25 billion from $2.06 billion a year ago. Operating income increased 14.7% to $366.7 million. Net income increased 5.8% to $205.3 million, or $0.63 per share, from $194.1 million or $0.58 per share a year ago.
