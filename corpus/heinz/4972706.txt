HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividends on Preferred Stock Payable on January 1, 2008.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividend on preferred stock. The board declared 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable on January 1, 2008 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 21, 2007.
