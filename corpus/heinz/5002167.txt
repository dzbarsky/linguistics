HJ Heinz Co. Announces Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended October 31, 2007.
"HJ Heinz Co. announced earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended October 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $227,037,000 or $0.71 per diluted share on the sales of $2,523,379,000 compared to net income of $191,575,000 or $0.57 per diluted share on the sales of $2,232,225,000 for the same period last year. For the six months, the company reported net income of $432,331,000 or $1.34 per diluted share on the sales of $4,771,664,000 compared to net income of $385,676,000 or $1.15 per diluted share on the sales of $4,292,145,000 for the same period last year."
