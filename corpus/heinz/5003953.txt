Hj Heinz Co. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full-year of 2007.
HJ Heinz Co. provided earnings guidance for the full-year of 2007. The company was expecting full-year earnings of $2.54 to $2.60 per share, but raised the top end of the range to $2.62 per share. The company said it is on track to post record full-year sales.
