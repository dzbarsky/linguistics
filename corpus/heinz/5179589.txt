HJ Heinz Co. Revised Earnings Outlook for Fiscal Year 2008; Provides Earnings Outlook for Third Quarter of Fiscal 2008.
"HJ Heinz Co. revised its earnings outlook for fiscal year 2008. The company narrowed its profit forecast to the top end of its prior forecast for earnings in a range of $2.60 to $2.62 per share, which would be a 9% to 10% increase from the prior year. In September, the company reported that it expected profit near the upper end of previous earnings guidance of $2.54 to $2.60 per share. The company anticipates third-quarter earnings between 67 cents and 68 cents per share on an increased tax rate. It expects sales growth of about 14% partly due to the strength of its Heinz brand."
