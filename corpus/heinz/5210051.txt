HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended January 30, 2008.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended January 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $218,532,000 or $0.68 per diluted share on sales of $2,610,863,000 against net income of $219,038,000 or $0.66 per diluted share on sales of $2,295,192,000 for the same quarter last year. The strong sales results were driven by successful innovation and a 16% increase in marketing, as stronger volume and pricing behind the company's top brands fueled broad-based growth. The company also announced that income from continuing operations of $218,532,000 against $219,038,000 for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported net income of $650,863,000 or $2.01 per diluted share on sales of $7,382,527,000 against net income of $604,714,000 or $1.81 per diluted share on sales of $6,587,337,000 for the same period last year.
