HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividends Payable on April 10, 2008 and April 1, 2008.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The Company declared a dividend of 38.0 cents per share on the Company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable on April 10, 2008 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2008. On preferred Stock the Company declared a dividend of 42.5 cents per share on the Company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable April 1, 2008 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2008.
