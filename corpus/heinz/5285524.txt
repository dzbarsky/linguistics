HJ Heinz Co. Amends the Second Amended and Restated Articles of Incorporation; Announces Change of Registered Office.
"On March 13, 2008, HJ Heinz Co. filed with the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania's Department of State Corporation Bureau a statement of change of registered office, and which has the effect of amending the second amended and restated Articles of Incorporation. The registered office is changed from 600 Grant Street, Pittsburgh PA 15219, Allegheny to 1 PPG Place, Suite 3100, Pittsburgh PA 15222, Allegheny."
