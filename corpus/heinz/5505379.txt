HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividends on Common and Preferred Stock, Payable on July 10, 2008 and Payable on July 1, 2008.
"HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The common stock dividend will be raised by 9.2% from 38 cents to 41.5 cents quarterly, for all shareholders of record as of June 24, 2008 payable July 10, 2008. The annualized dividend is $1.66 which represents an increase of $0.14 versus last year's dividend. The 9.2% increase follows a 16.7% increase in Fiscal Year 2007 and an 8.6% increase in Fiscal Year 2008. The Board also declared a dividend of 42.5 cents per share on the Company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable July 1, 2008 to shareholders of record at the close of business on June 24, 2008."
