HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended April 30, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for Fiscal 2009 and Fiscal 2010 ; Provides Dividend Guidance for Fiscal 2010.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended April 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $194,062,000 or $0.61 per diluted share on sales of $2,688,251,000 compared to net income of $181,032,000 or $0.55 per diluted share on sales of $2,414,293,000 for the same period a year ago. The Company anticipates a dividend payout ratio of around 60% for Fiscal 2010."
