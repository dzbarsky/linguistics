Heinz Eyes Acquisitions In Australia And New Zealand.
H.J. Heinz Co. is aiming for at least 6% sales growth over the next two years. The company also plans to launch 400 new products, spend millions of dollars in marketing and improve productivity. The company reported net income of $845 million for the year. William R. Johnson, Chairman of the company said that the company continues to invest overall in new product development and has also broadened its search for potential acquisitions. The company stated that it is looking for acquisitions in Australia and New Zealand. A spokesperson stated: Heinz is always looking for strategic acquisition opportunities and would consider making additional acquisitions in both Australia and New Zealand.
