HJ Heinz Co. to Open Manufacturing Plant in Florence.
The Florence County Economic Development Partnership and the South Carolina Department of Commerce have announced that HJ Heinz Co. will build a manufacturing facility in Florence. The company plans to create 350 jobs at its new plant. The construction of the new facility is scheduled for completion in late summer of 2009. The company will manufacture frozen food products at the new facility. This plant will also support the company's southeast and northeast distribution centers.
