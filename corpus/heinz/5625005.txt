HJ Heinz Co., Annual General Meeting, Aug 13, 2008.
"HJ Heinz Co., Annual General Meeting, Aug 13, 2008, at 09:00 US Eastern Time. Location: The Westin Convention Center Hotel, 1000 Penn Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA 15222, United States. Agenda: To elect 12 directors; to ratify the Audit Committee's selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the Company's independent registered public accounting firm; to amend the Company's By-Laws and Second Amended and Restated Articles of Incorporation to reduce the affirmative shareholder vote required to amend or repeal provisions relating to limitation of director liability and director and officer indemnification; and to amend the Company's Second Amended and Restated Articles of Incorporation to reduce the affirmative shareholder vote required to approve certain business combinations with 10% shareholders; and to consider and act on such other business as may properly come before the Meeting ."
