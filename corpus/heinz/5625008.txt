HJ Heinz Co. Proposes Amendments to Articles of Incorporation.
HJ Heinz Co. proposed at AGM to be held on August 13, 2008, to amend the companys By-Laws and second amended and restated Articles of Incorporation to reduce the affirmative shareholder vote required to amend or repeal provisions relating to limitation of director liability and director and officer indemnification; and to amend the companys second amended and restated Articles of Incorporation to reduce the affirmative shareholder vote required to approve certain business combinations with 10% shareholders.
