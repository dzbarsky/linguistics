HJ Heinz Co. to Add 13 New Varieties Soups.
HJ Heinz Co. will be adding 13 new varieties to its ambient soups range this autumn with an investment of GBP 2.5 million. The range will see new additions for its Classics, Weight Watchers from Heinz, Taste of Home, Big Soup, Farmers' Market and Soups of The World ambient soups.
