HJ Heinz Co. Announces Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended July 30, 2008.
HJ Heinz Co. announced earnings results for the first quarter ended July 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $228,964,000 or $0.72 diluted per share on sales of $2,583,208,000 against net income of $205,294,000 or $0.63 diluted per share on sales of $2,248,285,000 reported a year ago.
