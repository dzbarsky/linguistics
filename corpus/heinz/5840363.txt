Heinz Introduces New TGI Friday's Complete Skillet Meals.
HJ Heinz Company has introduced the new TGI Friday's restaurants-inspired frozen meals called TGI Friday's Complete Skillet Meals. The new meals come in five varieties including: Sizzling Steak Fajitas, Sizzling Chicken Fajitas, Creamy Chicken Pasta Carbonara, Firecracker Sesame Chicken, and Cajun-Style Alfredo Chicken & Shrimp.
