HJ Heinz Launches Ore-Ida Steam n' Mash Potatoes.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Ore-Ida brand has introduced the new Steam n' Mash potatoes in the US. According to the company, the new frozen potatoes product contains ready-to-steam, real potatoes that have already been scrubbed, peeled and chopped. Ore-Ida Steam n' Mash potatoes are available in four varieties, including Cut Russet, Three-Cheese, Garlic Seasoned and Sweet Potatoes. To launch the Steam n' Mash line of products, Ore-Ida has partnered with home and food expert Sandra Lee to offer advice and useful tips on how to make homemade foods such as mashed potatoes, in a more convenient way.
