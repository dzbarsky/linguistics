HJ Heinz Co. Names Suzanne Douglas as Managing Director for Australian Unit.
HJ Heinz Co.'s chief marketing officer of UK and Ireland, Suzanne Douglas, has now been appointed as managing director of the firm's Australian business. Ms. Douglas has served as the chief marketing officer of Heinz UK and Ireland since July 2006. Prior to that, she was general manager marketing of Heinz Australia. Currently, the company has no intentions of finding an alternative to Ms. Douglas at Heinz UK and Ireland. Category marketing directors, who earlier reported to her, will now report directly to Dave Woodward, president of Heinz UK and Ireland.
