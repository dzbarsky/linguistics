HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividend on Common and Preferred Stocks Payable on January 10, 2009 and January 1, 2009, Respectively.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends of 41.5 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value common stock payable on January 10, 2009 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 22, 2008. On both common and preferred stock. The company also declared quarterly dividends of 42.5 cents per share on the company's third cumulative preferred stock, $1.70 first series, payable on January 1, 2009 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 22, 2008.
