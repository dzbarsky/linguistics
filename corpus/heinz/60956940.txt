HJ Heinz Co., Annual General Meeting, Aug 12, 2009.
HJ Heinz Co., Annual General Meeting, Aug 12, 2009, at 09:00 US Eastern Time. Location: The Westin Convention Center Hotel, 1000 Penn Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA 15222, United States. Agenda: To elect 12 directors; to ratify the Audit Committee's selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the company's independent registered public accounting firm; to amend the company's By-Laws to add a right permitting holders of 25% of the voting power of the outstanding shares to call a special meeting of shareholders; and to consider and act on such other business as may properly come before the meeting.
