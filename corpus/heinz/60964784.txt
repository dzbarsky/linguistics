Heinz to Introduce Reduced Sugar & Salt Beanz Snap Pots.
Heinz plans to launch the new Reduced Sugar & Salt Beanz Snap Pots, a new product in its beans category, reported Talking Retail. The company is likely to introduce the new Reduced Sugar & Salt Beanz Snap Pots in July 2009. The product reportedly contains 30% less sugar and 25% less salt than the standard recipe and is devoid of any artificial colors, flavors or preservatives. According to the news source, Heinz Reduced Sugar & Salt Beanz Snap Pots are available in a convenient to open and heat format and help serve one individual. In addition, the company also intends to launch an improved recipe across its whole Reduced Sugar & Salt Beanz range.
