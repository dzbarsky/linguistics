HJ Heinz Co. to Launch Line of Briannas Salad Dressings ; Plans a Private Placement of $250 Million in Notes.
"H.J. Heinz Co.'s portion control division is working with Del Sol Food Co., of Brenham, Texas, to launch a line of Briannas salad dressings in single-serve packets for use in restaurants and cafeterias. Heinz also plans a private placement offering of $250 million in notes that would come due in 2039. If that offer is successful, Heinz plans to exchange cash and additional notes due in 2039 for $800 million in securities."
