HJ Heinz Co. Announces Management Changes.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that Ted Smyth, Chief Administrative Officer and Senior Vice President of Corporate and Government Affairs has decided to retire from Heinz after 21 years of outstanding service to the company. The company also announced that Steve Clark, Chief People Officer, will be promoted to the Office of the Chairman and Michael Mullen will be promoted to Vice President of Corporate & Government Affairs, responsible for leading the communications and government affairs functions for Heinz.
