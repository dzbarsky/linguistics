Heinz Declares Quarterly Dividend for Common and Preferred Stock Payable on October 10, 2009 and October 1, 2009 Respectively.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its board of directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The board declared common stock 42 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value common stock payable on October 10, 2009 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 22, 2009. 42.5 cents per share on the company's third cumulative preferred stock, $1.70 First Series, payable October 1, 2009 to shareholders of record at the close of business on September 22, 2009.
