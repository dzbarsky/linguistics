HJ Heinz Co. Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended July 29, 2009 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Year 2010.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended July 29, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net income attributable to company of $212,564,000 or $0.67 per share on net sales of $2,467,923,000 compared to net income attributable to company of $228,964,000 or $0.72 per share on net sales of $2,583,208,000 for the same period of last year. Operating income was $365,969,000 compared to $392,264,000 for the same period of last year. Income before income taxes was $306,224,000 compared to $326,383,000 for the same period of last year. Capital expenditures were $48,708,000 compared to $41,634,000 for the same period of last year. Cash provided by operating activities was $168,868,000 compared to cash used for operating activities of $13,935,000 for the same period of last year. The company reaffirmed its previous guidance for full-year Fiscal 2010 results.
