HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended January 28, 2009.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended January 28, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported an operating income of $381.8 million, net income of $242.3 million or $0.77 per basic share and $0.76 per diluted share on the net sales of $2.4 billion compared to the operating income of $406.1 million, net income of $218.5 million or $0.69 per basic share and $0.68 per diluted share on the $2.6 billion for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported an operating income of $1.16 billion, net income of $747.9 million or $2.39 per basic share and $2.35 per diluted share on the net sales of $7.6 billion compared to the operating income of $1.19 billion, net income of $650.9 million or $2.04 per basic share and $2.01 per diluted share on the $7.4 billion for the same period a year ago."
