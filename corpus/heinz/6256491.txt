Heinz Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter Ended January 28, 2009; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2009.
"Heinz reported earnings results for the third quarter ended January 28, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported its profit rose to $242.3 million, or 76 cents per share. That's up from $218.5 million, or 68 cents per share, a year earlier. The company's revenue fell 7.5% to $2.41 billion. The company reaffirmed earnings guidance for the full year of 2009. For the year, the company expects 2009 profit will grow by 9% to 11%."
