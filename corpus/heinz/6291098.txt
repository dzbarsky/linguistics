HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividends on Common and Preferred Stock, Payable on April 10, 2009 and Payable on April 1, 2009 Respectively.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The board declared 41.5 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable on April 10, 2009 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2009. The board also declared 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable on April 1, 2009 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2009.
