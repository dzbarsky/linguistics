Solexa Inc. announced that it has received $50 million in funding from Illumina Inc...
"Solexa Inc. (NasdaqNM: SLXA) announced a private placement of 5,154,639 shares at $9.70 per share for gross proceeds of $50 million on November 12, 2006. The placement will be made to Illumina Inc. Lazard Fr?res & Co. LLC acted as financial advisor and Cooley Godward Kronish LLP acted as legal counsel to the company while Ballantine LLP acted as legal counsel to Illumina on this transaction. Merrill Lynch was financial advisor to Illumina. Latham & Watkins LLP was legal advisor to Merrill Lynch. Gibson Dunn & Crutcher acted as legal advisor to Lazard. On November 13, 2006, Solexa Inc. closed the transaction."
