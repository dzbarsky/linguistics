Illumina Inc. Appoints Scott D. Kahn as Vice President and Chief Information Officer.
Illumina Inc. appointed Scott D. Kahn, Ph.D., as Vice President and Chief Information Officer. In this new position, Dr. Kahn will be responsible for all scientific software and information technologies of Illumina. Dr. Kahn joins Illumina from Accelrys Inc., where he served as Chief Scientific Officer. Previously a key figure in product development and marketing at BioCAD Inc. and Molecular Simulations Inc., Dr. Kahn has over a decade of experience in computational chemistry and he has guided commercial development of some of the world's most widely used simulation products. His record includes involvement in the development of analysis tools for medicinal chemistry and in the creation of commercial modeling packages.
