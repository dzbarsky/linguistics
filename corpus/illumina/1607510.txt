Illumina Appoints Christian Henry as Chief Financial Officer.
Illumina Inc. announced the appointment of Christian Henry to the position of Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, effective June 6, 2005. In this position, Mr. Henry will be responsible for worldwide financial operations, controllership functions and facilities management. Mr. Henry has most recently served as the Chief Financial Officer for Tickets.com, a publicly traded, online ticket provider that was recently acquired by Major League Baseball Advanced Media, LP. Prior to that, Mr. Henry was Vice President, Finance and Corporate Controller of Affymetrix Inc.
