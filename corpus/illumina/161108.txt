Illumina Names Tristan Orpin to Lead Worldwide Sales.
Illumina Inc. announced the appointment of Tristan Orpin as Vice President of Worldwide Sales. In this new position, Mr. Orpin will be responsible for global sales of the Company's high-throughput BeadArray(TM) and Oligator(TM) technology platforms, products and services. He will manage Illumina's direct sales team as well as the Company's indirect and distributor sales channels.
