Illumina Promotes Kirk D. Malloy to Vice President of Customer Solutions.
Illumina Inc. announced the promotion of Kirk D. Malloy, Ph.D., to the position of Vice President of Customer Solutions. In his new position, Dr. Malloy will be responsible for worldwide customer service, field applications support and quality assurance operations. Dr. Malloy joined Illumina in 2002 as Senior Director of Customer Solutions, and later added quality assurance to his responsibilities. Prior to Illumina, Dr. Malloy was Director of Technical Service and Implementation at Biosite Inc.
