Illumina Reports Financial Results for Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2002.
Illumina Inc. announced its financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended December 29, 2002. For the quarter ended December 29, 2002, Illumina reported a net loss of $7.6 million, or $0.24 per share, compared to a net loss of $8.2 million, or $0.27 per share for the same period in 2001. The net loss for the year ended December 29, 2002 was $40.3 million, or $1.31 per share, compared to a net loss of $24.8 million, or $0.83 per share for the same period in 2001. Revenues for the fourth quarter and fiscal year were $3.9 million and$10.0 million respectively, as compared to $0.8 million and $2.5 million for the same periods in 2001.
