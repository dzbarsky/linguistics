Illumina Signs Genotyping Services Agreement With the University of North Carolina --UNC-- At Chapel Hill.
Illumina Inc. announced that it has signed a commercial agreement with the UNC Pulmonary and Cystic Fibrosis Research Center to provide single nucleotide polymorphism genotyping services on a sample collection provided by the Center. Under the terms of the agreement, Illumina will design functional assays for many of the SNP loci provided by the Research Center, and then use its BeadArray technology to genotype specified SNPs in the sample set. Further details about the agreement were not disclosed.
