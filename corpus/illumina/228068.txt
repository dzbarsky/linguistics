Illumina Reports Financial Results for Third Quarter Of 2003.
"Illumina Inc. announced financial results for the third quarter and nine-month period ended September 28, 2003. For the quarter ended September 28, 2003, the Company reported revenue of $8.2 million, a 176% increase compared to revenue of $3.0 million in the third quarter of 2002. For the quarter ended September 28, 2003, the Company reported a net loss of $5.5 million, or $0.17 per share, compared to a net loss of $7.6 million, or $0.24 per share, in the second quarter of 2002. Revenue for the nine-month period ended September 28, 2003 was $17.3 million, a 181% increase compared to revenue of $6.2 million for the same nine-month period in 2002. Net loss for the nine-month period ended September 28, 2003 was $23.1 million, or $0.72 per share, compared to a net loss of $32.7 million, or $1.06 per share, for the same nine-month period in 2002."
