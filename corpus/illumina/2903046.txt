Illumina Inc., Annual General Meeting, Jun 08, 2006.
Illumina Inc., Annual General Meeting, Jun 08, 2006, at 10:00 Pacific Daylight. Location: 9885 Towne Centre Drive, San Diego, CA 92121, United States. Agenda: to elect three directors to serve for a three-year term ending in the year 2009 or until their respective successors are duly elected and qualified; to ratify the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as independent auditors of the Company for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2006 and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment or adjournments thereof..
