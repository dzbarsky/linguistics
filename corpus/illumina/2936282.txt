Illumina Appoints Christian Cabou as Senior Vice President and General Counsel.
Illumina Inc. announced the appointment of Christian G. Cabou as Senior Vice President and General Counsel. In this new position, Mr. Cabou will have worldwide responsibility for all legal and intellectual property matters for Illumina. Mr. Cabou has deep legal, intellectual property and leadership experience. Most recently, he spent five years as General Counsel of GE Global Research, the research arm of General Electric Company, where he built a global team of attorneys charged with proactive creation of intellectual property to advance strategic business interests.
