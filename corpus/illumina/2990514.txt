Jack Goldstein to Join Illumina's Board of Directors.
Illumina Inc. announced that Jack Goldstein, Ph.D., will be joining the Company's Board of Directors. Dr. Goldstein was most recently President and Chief Operating Officer of Chiron Corporation until its acquisition by Novartis in April 2006. Prior to Chiron, he spent two years as a general partner at Windamere Venture Partners preceded by four years at Applied Imaging Corporation, first as President and Chief Executive Officer and then later as Chairman.
