Illumina Inc. Introduces the Illumina CSPro(TM) Program.
Illumina Inc. announced that it has introduced the Illumina CSPro(TM) Program, a new offering under which organizations with Illumina systems can become certified in order to provide a range of genetic analysis services to the research community using Illumina technologies. The Illumina CSPro Program is the only program of its kind to offer a collaborative partnership that includes co-marketing benefits with a certification process designed to help ensure delivery of the highest-quality data available for genetic analysis applications. Illumina has established high performance standards for each of the Company's assay technologies including GoldenGate(R) Genotyping, Infinium(TM) Whole-Genome Genotyping, Whole-Genome Expression and DASL(TM) Gene Expression. The Illumina CSPro Program is modular so that participants can elect to be certified for any or all of the assays.
