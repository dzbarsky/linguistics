Illumina Reports Financial Results for Third Quarter Ended 2002 Year Results.
Illumina Inc. announced its financial results for the third quarter and nine-month period ended September 29, 2002. For the quarter ended September 29, 2002, the Company reported a net loss of $7.6 million, or $0.24 per share, compared to a net loss of $5.8 million, or $0.19 per share, in the third quarter of 2001. Net loss for the nine-month period ended September 29, 2002 was $32.7 million, or $1.06 per share, compared to a net loss of $16.6 million, or $0.56 per share, for the same nine-month period in 2001. Revenues for the third quarter and nine-month period were $3.0 million and $6.2 million, respectively, compared to $0.7 million and $1.7 million for the same periods in 2001.
