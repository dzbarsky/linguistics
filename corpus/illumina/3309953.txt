Genome Institute of Singapore Selects Illumina's HumanRef-8 BeadChip for Gene Expression Profiling.
Illumina Inc. announced that in collaboration with the Genome Institute of Singapore (GIS) will utilize the HumanRef-8 Expression BeadChip to analyze up to 3,000 samples. The GIS is a flagship facility in Singapore that pursues the integration of technology, genetics and biology towards the goal of individualized medicine. The HumanRef-8 BeadChip allows researchers to generate genome-wide expression profiles for eight samples in parallel on a single microarray, with industry-leading performance. The new BeadChip is part of a complete gene expression solution that includes instrumentation, software and reagent kits.
