Illumina Approves an Amendment to Bylaws.
Illumina Inc. announced that board of directors approved an amendment to the company's bylaws. These amendments became effective upon such approval. Generally, the amendments: delete certain provisions that were superseded by the company's certificate of incorporation or were redundant with the Delaware General Corporation Law; provide that directors who appoint a new director to fill a vacancy created by an increase in the number of directors have the authority to specify which class of directors on which the new director will serve; and provide that directors appointed to fill certain vacancies will serve on the same class of directors on which the director being replaced served.
