Illumina Mulls Acquisitions.
Illumina, Inc. announced its intention to offer, subject to market and other conditions, approximately $325 million principal amount of Convertible Senior Notes due 2014 through offerings to qualified institutional buyers pursuant to Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. Illumina intends to use the net proceeds for other general corporate purposes, which may include acquisitions and additional purchases of common stock.
