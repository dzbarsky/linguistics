Illumina, Inc. plans to initiate a share buyback program.
Illumina, Inc. plans to use $200 million to buy stock in a private transaction. In a related transaction, it will first sell $325 million in convertible debt and then use the proceeds to buyback shares. Illumina did not disclose with whom it had arranged the share purchase program. The $200 million would buy about 5,900,000 shares, representing 8% of its 46,500,000 shares outstanding.
