Illumina Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $202 mm.
Illumina Inc. (NasdaqNM:ILMN) announced a share repurchase program on February 13, 2007. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $202 million of its common shares in privately negotiated deals. The company will fund the repurchase from the sale of convertible senior notes.
