Illumina Inc. closed an Equity Buyback in the amount of $201.60 mm.
"Illumina Inc. (NasdaqNM:ILMN) announced a share repurchase program on February 13, 2007. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $201.6 million of its common shares in privately negotiated deals. The company will fund the repurchase from the sale of convertible senior notes. Illumina Inc. (NasdaqNM:ILMN) completed the share repruchase program on February 16, 2007. Under the program, the company has repurchased 5,771,000 shares for $201.6 million."
