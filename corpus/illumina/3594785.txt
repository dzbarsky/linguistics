Illumina Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $75 mm.
The Board of Directors of Illumina (NASDAQ:ILMN) authorized a share repurchase program on February 27, 2007. Under the program, Illumina will repurchase up to $75 million of its outstanding common stock over a period of six months under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan. The program will be funded with a portion of the proceeds from Illumina's recent $400 million Convertible Senior Notes offering. The repurchased shares will be held in treasury.
