Illumina Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended April 1, 2007 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of Fiscal Year 2007.
"Illumina Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended April 1, 2007. For the first quarter, the company reported a net loss of $298.08 million or $5.58 per basic and diluted share on the total revenue of $72.15 million compared to a net loss of $0.104 million or $0.00 per basic and diluted share on the total revenue of $29.10 million for the same period last year. The Company expects second quarter total revenue to be between $74 and $78 million. The company expects non-GAAP net income to be between $12 and $14 million, or $0."
