Illumina Inc., Annual General Meeting, Jun 07, 2007.
Illumina Inc., Annual General Meeting, Jun 07, 2007, at 10:00 Pacific Daylight. Location: 9885 Towne Centre Drive, San Diego, CA 92121,United States. Agenda: To elect five directors to serve for various terms ending between the years 2008 and 2010 or until their respective successors are duly elected and qualified; to ratify appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as independent auditors for the fiscal year ending December 30, 2007; to approve an amendment to increase the maximum number of shares of company's common stock authorized for issuance under 2005 Stock and Incentive Plan by 1,250,000 shares; to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment or adjournments thereof.
