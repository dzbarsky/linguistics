Illumina Inc. Announces Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 28, 2003.
"Illumina Inc. announced its financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended December 28, 2003. For the quarter ended December 28, 2003, Illumina reported revenue of $10.7 million, a 176% increase compared to revenue of $3.9 million in the fourth quarter of 2002. For the quarter ended December 28, 2003, the Company reported a net loss of $4.0 million, or $0.12 per share, compared to a net loss of $7.6 million, or $0.24 per share, in the fourth quarter of 2002. Revenue for the fiscal year ended December 28, 2003 was $28.0 million, a 179% increase compared to revenue of $10.0 million for the fiscal year ended December 29, 2002. Net loss for the fiscal year ended December 28, 2003 was $27.1 million, or $0.85 per share, compared to a net loss of $40.3 million, or $1.31 per share, for the fiscal year ended December 29, 2002."
