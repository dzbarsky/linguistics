Illumina Inc. Appoints Mike Bouchard as Vice President of Finance.
Illumina Inc. announced the appointment of Mike Bouchard as Vice President of Finance. Prior to joining the company, Mike served as Vice President of Finance and Accounting for Websense. At Websense, Mike was responsible for all financial, accounting, treasury, and tax matters for the company. In his new position as Illumina's Vice President of Finance, Mike is responsible for overseeing Illumina's worldwide finance, accounting, treasury and tax functions.
