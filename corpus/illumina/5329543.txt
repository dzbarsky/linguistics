Illumina Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 16, 2008.
Illumina Inc.,Annual General Meeting,May 16,2008,at 11:00 Pacific Daylight. Location: 9885 Towne Centre Drive,San Diego,CA 92121,United States. Agenda: To elect two directors to serve for three years ending 2011 or until their respective successors are duly elected and qualified; to ratify the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as independent auditors for the fiscal year ending December 28,2008; to approve an amendment to increase the maximum number of shares of common stock authorized for issuance under 2005 Stock and Incentive Plan by 1,200,000 shares and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment or adjournments thereof.
