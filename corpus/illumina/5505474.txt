Clinical Data's Cogenics Division Completes Installation of Illumina's iScan System.
Clinical Data Inc. announced that its Cogenics(R) division has validated installation of the Illumina Inc.'s iScan System in its Morrisville, North Carolina site. Illumina's iScan System includes a high-resolution scanner that supports rapid, sensitive, and accurate imaging of Illumina's BeadChip-based genetic analysis products. Cogenics is the first commercial genetic services provider to install the iScan System within Illumina's early access program and plans to use the system to further strengthen its leading portfolio of genomic services and solutions.
