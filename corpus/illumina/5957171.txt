Illumina Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $120 mm.
The Board of Directors of Illumina Inc. (NasdaqGS:ILMN) authorized a stock repurchase program on October 24, 2008. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $120 million of its outstanding common stock in open-market purchases or through privately negotiated transactions as permitted under Securities Exchange Act of 1934 Rule 10b-18. The number and the timing of such repurchases will depend upon market conditions and other corporate considerations, as determined by Illumina's management team. The company will use its existing cash balances to fund repurchases under the program.
