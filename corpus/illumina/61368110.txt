Illumina Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and for the Year 2009.
Illumina Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. For the second quarter of 2009, the company reported revenue of $161.6 million, a 15% increase over the $140.2 million reported in the second quarter of 2008. The Company reported net income for the quarter of $24.7 million, or $0.18 per diluted share, compared to $12.7 million, or $0.09 per diluted share in the comparable period of 2008.
