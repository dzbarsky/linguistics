Kimberly-Clark Corp. Declares Dividend Payable on April 4, 2005.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported that its board of directors has approved the previously announced dividend increase of 12.5% in 2005. This will bring the quarterly dividend to 45 cents per share, up from 40 cents per share in 2004. The board of directors declared the dividend payable on April 4, 2005, to stockholders of record on March 4, 2005.
