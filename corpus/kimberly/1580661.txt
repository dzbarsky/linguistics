Kimberly-Clark Combines North Atlantic Personal Care and Family Care into One Organization ; Appoints Steve Kalmanson to Head the New Organization.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that it is combining its North Atlantic Personal Care and Family Care businesses into a single organization under one leader. Steve Kalmanson, currently Group President - North Atlantic Personal Care, will head the new organization as Group President - North Atlantic Consumer Products. He will assume responsibility immediately for all K-C's consumer businesses in North America and Europe, as well as related customer development and supply chain organizations. This move follows the departure of Rob van der Merwe, Group President - North Atlantic Consumer Tissue, who has been named President and CEO of Paxar Corporation, a publicly held company specializing in merchandising products for the retail and apparel industry."
