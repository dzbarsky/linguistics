Kimberly-Clark Corp. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of 2005 and Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2005.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. provides earnings guidance for the second quarter of 2005. For the second quarter, the company expects earnings to be in a range of 92 to 94 cents per share. Compared with net income from continuing operations of 88 cents per share in 2004, this would represent growth of approximately 5% to 7%, similar to the expected level of improvement for the full year. The company reaffirmed earnings guidance for the full year of 2005. For the full year, the company reaffirmed its previous guidance of earnings of $3.70 to $3.85 per share, representing mid-to high single-digit growth against with net income from continuing operations of $3.55 per share in 2004."
