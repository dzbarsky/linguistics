Kimberly-clark Lever Launches Huggies Ultra Comfort Diapers.
KIMBERLY-CLARK LEVER has launched Huggies Ultra Comfort diapers from its international range. They claim to have a triple leak-lock system which provides extra protection against side leakage by quickly and evenly absorbing fluid. They are priced at INR 70 for a pack of four and INR 175 for a pack of 10 diapers. They are available at select outlets in key cities.
