Kimberly-Clark Expands Investment in China.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced here on September 11 the completion of its Beijing factory. The Beijing factory will not only satisfy increasing domestic demands, but also be responsible for supplying personal care products for its global market, especially for the United States, Europe, and Southeast Asian countries. Kimberly-Clark has committed 90 million US dollars to create this facility. In terms of investment value, this is Kimberly-Clark's biggest program in China to date.
