Kimberly-Clark Corp. Announces Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended Sept. 30, 2005.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced earnings results for the nine months ended Sept. 30, 2005. For the period, the company reported net income of $1,197.2 million or $2.49 per diluted share on net sales of $11,893.7 million against net income of $1,354.9 million or $2.70 per diluted share on net sales of $11,181.8 million for the same period a year ago.
