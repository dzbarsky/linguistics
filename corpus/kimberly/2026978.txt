Kimberly-Clark Corp. (NYSE: KMB) agreed to acquire the remaining 45% stake in Kimberly-Clark Australia Pty. Ltd. from Amcor Ltd. (Nasdaq: AMCR) on May 7, 2002 for a reported value of approximately A$697.5 million in cash. Kimberly-Clark plans to fund the purchase through a combination of current cash flow and available financial leverage. The transaction will be accretive to earnings in the second half of 2002 by approximately one cent per share.
