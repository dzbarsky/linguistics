Kimberly-Clark Introduces New Improved Kotex Products.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation maker of the No. 1 feminine care brand in the U.S., announced three new products that will hit store shelves in August: Kotex BodyFit Ultra Thin pads, Kotex Ultra Thin Long pads with Wings and Kotex Lightdays Purse-Paks Ultra Coverage pantiliners. The company also announced improvements across its entire range of Kotex Ultra Thin and Thick pads. These new products and innovations are the result of listening to women's no-holds-barred opinions about what they expect from feminine products.
