Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Kimberly-Clark Corp., Southeast Timberlands Operations; Buyer(s): Southstar Timber Resources LLC; Seller(s): Kimberly-Clark Corp..
"Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB) agreed to sell its Southeast timberlands operations to Southstar Timber Resources, LLC. The sale, which is subject to regulatory clearances and completion of financing by Southstar, is expected to close in early 1999."
