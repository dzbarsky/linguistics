Cancelled Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Kimberly-Clark Corp., Southeast Timberlands Operations; Buyer(s): Southstar Timber Resources LLC; Seller(s): Kimberly-Clark Corp..
"Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB) agreed to sell its Southeast timberlands operations to Southstar Timber Resources, LLC. The sale, which is subject to regulatory clearances and completion of Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB) agreement to sell its Southeast timberlands operations to Southstar Timber Resources, LLC was terminated on 03/16/1999 because Southstar was unable to complete financing arrangements for the transaction."
