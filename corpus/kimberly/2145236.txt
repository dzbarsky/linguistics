Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Klabin SA, Tissue business; Buyer(s): Kimberly-Clark Corp.; Seller(s): Klabin SA.
"Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB) announced the purchase of a 50% equity interest in Klabin Tissue, S.A. Details of the transaction were not disclosed. Klabin Tissue is an affiliate of Industrias Klabin de Papel e Celulose, S.A. (IKPC). Kimberly-Clark Corp. acquired 50% interest in Klabin Tissue, S.A. on 05/28/1998."
