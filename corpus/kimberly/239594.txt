Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year of 2001.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp, the leading Dallas-based consumer products company with a strong Neenah presence reported net income of $1.63 billion in 2001, or 26 cents per share. That's 9.7 percent lower than 2000. In the fourth quarter, the company recorded net income of $358.3 million compared with $455.7 million, about 17 cents per share. That represents a drop of 21.4 percent from the same period in 2000."
