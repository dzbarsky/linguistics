Kimberly-Clark Corp. Announces Management Changes.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that Dudley Lehman, group president of the company's Business-to-Business (B2B) Group, has said he plans to retire later next year with the company. With Mr. Lehman's pending retirement, Joanne Bauer, president, Health Care, and Jan Spencer, president, K-C Professional, both of whom reported to Lehman, will report directly to Tom Falk, Kimberly-Clark's chairman and chief executive officer. Until his retirement, Mr. Lehman will continue to oversee the Nonwovens and Partnership Products businesses.
