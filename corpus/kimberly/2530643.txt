Kimberly-Clark Corp. (NYSE:KMB) has agreed to acquire an equity interest in Axela Biosensors Inc. on January 11, 2006. The terms of hte transaction were not disclosed. The acquisition of interest is a part of an exclusive in-licensing agreement between Axela Biosensors Inc. and Kimberly- Clark.
