Huggies Creates a New Way to Diaper.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. Introduces Huggies Convertibles Diaper-Pants. Through extensive research into parents' needs for a more flexible way of diapering for active babies, Huggies brand diapers, an innovator in the diaper category and the number one brand in North America, has developed a first-of-a-kind product, Huggies Convertibles diaper-pants.
