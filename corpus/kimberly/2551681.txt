Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2005 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2006; Provides Dividend Guidance.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $371.1 million or $0.79 per diluted share on net sales of $4,008.9 million against net income of $445.3 million or $0.91 per diluted share on net sales of $3,901.4 million for the same quarter a year ago. For full year 2006, the company anticipated revenue growth of 3 to 5%, in line with its long-term objective.
