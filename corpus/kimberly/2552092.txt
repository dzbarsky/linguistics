Kimberly-Clark Partners with Sunhealth Solutions to Provide Free Samples of Sunsignals UV Sensors.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that it is partnering SunHealth Solutions LLC to provide free samples of SunSignals UV Sensors on Huggies Little Swimmers disposable swimpants packages beginning in April 2006. SunSignals UV Sensors will provide parents an easy and reliable way to detect and monitor their children's exposure to Ultraviolet B (UVB) radiation, the type of UV radiation that causes sunburn and is most commonly linked to skin cancer. SunSignals UV Sensors are small, self-adhesive patches that can be applied easily to a child's skin or clothing.
