Kimberly-Clark Elects Former Coca-Cola Enterprises President and CEO John R. Alm to its Board of Directors.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that John R. Alm, former President and CEO of Coca-Cola Enterprises Inc., has been elected to its board of directors, effective February 22, 2006. Mr. Alm, 59, joined the Johnson Coca-Cola Bottling Group as senior vice president and chief financial officer in 1980. Following a merger with Coca- Cola Enterprises Inc. in 1991, Mr. Alm was named vice president and chief financial officer of the combined company. He held a number of senior management positions at Coca-Cola Enterprises, ultimately being named president and CEO in 2003.
