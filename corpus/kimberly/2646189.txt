Kimberly-Clark Corp. Declares Increased Dividend, Payable on April 4, 2006.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that its board of directors has approved a dividend increase of 8.9%. This will bring the quarterly dividend to 49 cents per share, up from 45 cents per share in 2005. The board of directors declared the dividend payable on April 4, 2006, to stockholders of record on March 10, 2006. This is the 34th consecutive year Kimberly-Clark has raised its dividend.
