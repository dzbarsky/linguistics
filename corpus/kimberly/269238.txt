George Everbach, Kimberly-Clark Senior Vice President of Law and Government Affairs, to Retire August 2003.
O.George Everbach, 64, senior vice president for law and government affairs, announced that he will retire from Kimberly-Clark Corporation at the end of August 2003. Ronald D. Mc Cray, 45, currently vice president, associate general counsel and secretary, has been elected by the company's board of directors to succeed him.
