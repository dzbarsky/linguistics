Kimberly-Clark Corp., Annual General Meeting, Apr 27, 2006.
Kimberly-Clark Corp., Annual General Meeting, Apr 27, 2006, at 11:00 Central Daylight. Location: Four Seasons Resort and Club, 4150 North MacArthur Boulevard, Irving, Texas,United States. Agenda: to elect four directors for a three-year term to expire at the 2009 Annual Meeting of Stockholders; to approve the selection of Deloitte & Touche LLP as the Corporation's independent auditors; to vote on four stockholder proposals which may be presented at the meeting; and to take action upon any other business which may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment thereof.
