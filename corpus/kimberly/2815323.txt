Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2006; Reaffirms Earnings Outlook for the Full Year of 2006; Provides Earnings Outlook for the Second Quarter of 2006.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $275.1 million or $0.60 per share on net sales of $4,067.9 million against net income of $275.1 million or $0.60 per share on net sales of $4,067.9 million for the same period a year ago. For the second quarter, the company expects earnings per share before unusual items will be similar to or slightly better than the first quarter, in a range of 93 to 95 cents per share."
