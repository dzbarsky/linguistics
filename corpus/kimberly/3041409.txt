CDM Group completed the acquisition of Italian plant of Kimberly-Clark Corp..
"CDM Group acquired Italian plant of Kimberly-Clark Corp. on December 8, 2005. The sale agreement will be valid as of January 1, 2006. All 71 workers of Kimberly-Clark plant will be re-employed by CDM. CDM Group completed the acquisition of Italian plant of Kimberly-Clark Corp. on December 8, 2005."
