Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2006 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter of 2006 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2006.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported that net sales rose 4.4% to $4,161.4 million against $3,987.1 million for the same period in the last year. Net income for the quarter was $377.6 million against $421.8 million for the same quarter a year ago. Diluted net income was 82 cents per share compared with 88 cents per share in 2005, a decrease of about 7%. However, excluding charges for strategic cost reductions to streamline the company's operations, earnings before unusual items in the second quarter of 2006 were 95 cents per share, in line with the company's previous guidance of 93 to 95 cents per share. The improvement in second quarter sales reflects broadly higher net selling prices, up 2% overall, and favorable product mix of 1%.
