Kimberly-Clark Corp. (NYSE:KMB) entered into the agreement to acquire a further 5% stake in Kimberly-Clark Australia Pty. Ltd. from Amcor Ltd. (ASX:AMC) in a transaction valued at AUD 77.5 million on May 7, 2001. Kimberly-Clark Corp will acquire remaining 45% within the next four years under option agreement. Kimberly-Clark Australia had sales of AUD 856 million in 2000. Transaction is subject to approval by the Australia Foreign Investment Review Board, is expected to close effective June 30, 2001.
