Kimberly-Clark Announces 13.3 Percent Dividend Increase dividend payable on April 2, 2003.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced it will increase its dividend in 2003 by 13.3 percent, nearly double last year's rate of increase. This will bring the quarterly dividend to 34 cents per share, up from 30 cents per share in 2002. The company's board of directors declared the dividend payable on April 2, 2003, to stockholders of record on March 7, 2003. This marks the 31st consecutive year Kimberly-Clark has raised its dividend.
