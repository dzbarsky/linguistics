Kimberly-Clark Corp., Annual General Meeting, Apr 26, 2007.
Kimberly-Clark Corp., Annual General Meeting, Apr 26, 2007, at 11:00 Central Daylight. Location: 223 West Las Colinas Boulevard, Irving, Texas,United States. Agenda: To elect three directors for a three-year term to expire at the 2010 Annual Meeting of Stockholders; to approve the selection of Deloitte & Touche LLP as independent auditors; to approve a proposal to amend the Restated Certificate of Incorporation to permit the annual election of directors; to vote on three stockholder proposals which may be presented at the meeting; and to take action upon any other business which may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment thereof.
