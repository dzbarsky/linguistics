Kimberly-Clark Corp. to Establish Innovation Center in South Korea.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced plans to create an Innovation Center of Excellence in Asia, to be located in KiHeung City in South Korea's Gyeonggi-do province, a few miles south of Seoul. This center will serve as a key resource supporting Kimberly-Clark's commitment to discover, develop and deliver emerging technologies that foster future growth and take the company to the next level of performance as a premier health and hygiene leader. The South Korean government's Korea Trade Investment Promotion Agency is providing a USD 1.6 million incentive package to Kimberly-Clark to create the Innovation Center, which the company plans to locate within the campus of the Yuhan Research Institute. The center is expected to open in the first quarter of 2007.
