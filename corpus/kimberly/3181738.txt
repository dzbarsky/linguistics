Kimberly-Clark to Build New R&D Center at Giheung in S Korea.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that it plans to set up an advanced research and development (R&D) center in South Korea in a move expected to bolster the local bio science and personal care industry. The U.S.-based household and personal care products maker said the construction of the new facility will begin early next year at the YUHAN-KIMBERLY LTD. plant in Giheung, south of Seoul.
