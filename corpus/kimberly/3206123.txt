Kimberly-Clark Names Anthony Palmer as Chief Marketing Officer.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that Anthony Palmer will join the company as chief marketing officer, reporting to chairman and CEO Thomas Falk. In this newly created position, Mr Palmer will be responsible for leading the development of a global, enterprise-wide focus on marketing to accelerate K-C's progress in generating insights that drive all aspects of its business. In addition, he will seek to establish a long-term marketing investment strategy to ensure that business initiatives are backed by high-return marketing programs. Dallas-based K-C said Mr Palmer is a seasoned business leader with global marketing and management experience at some of the world's top consumer packaged goods companies. He was most recently managing director for Kellogg Company in the UK.
