Kimberly-Clark Corp. to Close Its Lakeview Plant.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. forecasts that its cost-cutting plan will save it about $100 million in 2006. A major component of the company's plan called for reducing its global work force of 57,000 by 6,000 people. The Company announced that about 5,000 of those affected employees already have been notified that their jobs will be outsourced or eliminated. The company has announced it would close its Lakeview plant, which would eliminate 510 jobs.
