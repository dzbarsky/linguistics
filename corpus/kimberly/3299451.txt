Kimberly-Clark Corp. Announces Executive Changes.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that Cheryl Perkins, chief innovation officer, plans to resign at the end of the year to pursue other professional opportunities following a distinguished career spanning 22 years with the company. With Ms. Perkins' departure, Bob Black, chief strategy officer, will assume responsibility for the innovation organization. The move will align development of the company's growth strategies with its pipeline of new health and hygiene products.
