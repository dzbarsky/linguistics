Kimberly-Clark Corporation acquired the remaining stake of 30% of Kimberly-Clark Kenko Industria e Comercio Ltda. from Alsercom Comercio De Produtos Texteis e Plasticos Ltda. for $99.6 million..
"Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB) acquired the remaining 30% stake in Kimberly-Clark Kenko Industria e Comercio Ltda. from Alsercom Comercio De Produtos Texteis e Plasticos Ltda. on November 27, 2006. The transaction was valued at $99.6 million. ABN AMRO Corporate Finance Limited was financial advisor to Alsercom Comercio De Produtos Texteis e Plasticos Ltda. Kimberly-Clark Corporation completed the acquisition of remaining 30% of Kimberly-Clark Kenko Industria e Comercio Ltda. from Alsercom Comercio De Produtos Texteis e Plasticos Ltda. on November 27, 2006."
