Kimberly-Clark Announces 7% Dividend Increase and Says Share Repurchases Will Continue in 2002.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. increased its quarterly dividend 7.1 percent to 30 cents per share from 28 cents per share. The quarterly increase of 2 cents per share compares to an increase of 1 cent per share in each of the past five years. The board of directors declared the dividend payable on April 2, 2002, to stockholders of record on March 8, 2002. The company also said it expects to repurchase approximately 3 percent of its outstanding common stock during 2002, similar to the level of share repurchases in 2001."
