Kimberly-Clark Corp. to Cut 350 Jobs.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that it is to cut 350 jobs in its information technology, human resources, and sourcing and supply management departments. The positions to be axed are based at its US offices in Neenah and Fox Valley, and will be effective from January 1, 2007. The job cuts are due to the company's decision to outsource these operations to external providers.
