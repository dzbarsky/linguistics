Kimberly-Clark Corp. Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Full Year 2007.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. provided earnings guidance for the first quarter and full year 2007. The company expects first-quarter net income of 99 cents to $1.01 per share. The company sees 2007 adjusted operating profit growing 5% to 7% from $2.59 billion in 2006, implying a range of $2.72 billion and $2.77 billion, or $4.10 to $4.20 per share. Sales are expected to grow between 3% to 5% from 2006 revenue of $16.75 billion, implying 2007 sales of $17.25 billion and $17.59 billion."
