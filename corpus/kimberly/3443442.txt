Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2006.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported a net income of $482.6 million or $1.05 diluted per share on net sales of $4,307.2 million against a net income of $371.1 million or $0.79 diluted per share on net sales of $4,008.9 million for the same period a year ago. Pro forma net income was $363.5 million or $0.78 diluted proforma earnings per share. For the year, the company reported a net income of $1,499.5 million or $3.25 diluted per share on net sales of $16,746.9 million against a net income of $1,568.3 million or $3.28 diluted per share on net sales of $15,902.6 million for the same period a year ago. Pro forma net income was $1,531.9 million or $3.21 diluted proforma earnings per share."
