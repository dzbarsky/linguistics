Update to Kimberly-Clark Corp.'s Equity Buyback on December 31, 2006.
During the quarter ended December 31, 2006, Kimberly-Clark Corp. repurchased approximately 4,300,000 shares of its common stock at a cost of $286 million. As of December 31, 2006, the Company has repurchased approximately 16,818,000 shares, under the 50,000,000 shares repurchase program authorized in September 2005.
