Kimberly-Clark Corp. Appoints Ramon Baez as Vice President of Information Technology Services and Chief Information Officer.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that Ramon Baez has joined the company as vice president of Information Technology Services and Chief Information Officer. Mr. Baez will be responsible for leading Kimberly-Clark's enterprise-wide information systems initiatives to support the company's future growth and to maximize the return on its information technology investments. Prior to joining Kimberly-Clark, Mr. Baez served as chief information officer for Thermo Fisher Scientific Inc. Mr. Baez will be based at Kimberly-Clark's world headquarters in Dallas and will report to Buthman.
