Kimberly-Clark's Huggies Brand Launches New and Improved Baby Wipes and Toiletry Products.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced several new and improved baby and toddler hygiene products under its well-known Huggies brand. As part of announcement, K-C is introducing an improved Huggies Supreme baby wipe, a new naturally refreshing line of Huggies baby wipes and toiletries, and a new flashing light hand soap pump as part of the Huggies Cleanteam brand. K-C has built Huggies into a mega-brand offering a full line of baby and toddler hygiene products, including diapers, training pants, disposable changing pads, baby wipes, washcloths and mitts, rash cream and toiletrie.
