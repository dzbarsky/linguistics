Kimberly-clark Reports Bump In 2002 Second-quarter Profits.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp., the maker of Huggies and Kleenex, said Tuesday its profit rose slightly in the second quarter on higher sales of tissues and personal-care items. The company said it earned $425 million, or 81 cents per share, in the quarter ended June 30, about 2 percent higher than the $415 million, or 78 cents per share, in the same period last year. Revenue rose 5 percent to $3.41 billion from $3.25 billion. In the first six months of the year, the Irving-based company earned $864 million, or $1.65 per share, compared to $849 million, or $1.58 in the same period last year. Revenue rose nearly 3 percent, to $6.74 billion from $6.57 billion."
