Kimberly-Clark Corp. Proposes to Amend the Restated Certificate of Incorporation.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that at the AGM to be held on April 26, 2007 to consider a proposal to amend the Restated Certificate of Incorporation to permit the annual election of directors. Board of Directors is proposing, for approval by stockholders, an Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation that incorporates proposed amendments to certain provisions of the company's existing Restated Certificate of Incorporation. A stockholder proposal to declassify the Board of Directors was included in the 2006 Proxy Statement and received favorable votes from a majority of the shares of the common stock outstanding and entitled to vote.
