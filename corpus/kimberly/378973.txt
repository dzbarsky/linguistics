WWRE Announces First VICS CPFR Interoperability Project With Kimberly-Clark Corp.
WorldWide Retail Exchange announced its plans to launch a Collaborative Planning, Forecasting, and Replenishment implementation with Kimberly-Clark Corp. that is built on interoperability with the Kimberly-Clark collaboration hub. Data will be shared seamlessly between WWRE's Collaborative Planner solution, powered by Syncra Xt, WWRE members and Kimberly-Clark's behind-the-firewall Syncra solution. Kimberly-Clark will be the first trading partner to interoperate with WWRE members via WWRE's Collaborative Planner solution in this manner.
