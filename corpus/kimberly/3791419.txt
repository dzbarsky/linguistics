Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for 2007.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. reaffirmed its full-year earnings guidance ahead of an investor presentation and set a new three-year savings target of up to $450 million. The company said it continues to expect full-year earnings per share of $4.10 to $4.20, with first-quarter earnings per share at or slightly above its prior target of 99 cents to $1.01. The estimates are adjusted to exclude restructuring-related charges.
