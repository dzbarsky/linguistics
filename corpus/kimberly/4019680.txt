Kimberly-clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2007; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of Fiscal 2007.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $452.0 million or $0.98 per share on net sales of $4,385.3 million against net income of $275.1 million or $0.60 per share on net sales of $4,067.9 million for the same period a year ago. For the second quarter, company anticipate adjusted earnings per share will be similar to the first quarter, in a range of $1.01 to $1.03 per share. This will represent improvement of 6 to 8% versus the year-ago quarter."
