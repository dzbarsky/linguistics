Kimberly-Clark Corp. Announces Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2003.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2003. Net sales for the year were $14,348.0 million compared to $13,566.3 million for the same period previous year. Net income for the year were $1,694.2 million or $3.33 per share compared to $1,674.6 million or $3.22 per share for the same period previous year. For the fourth quarter ended December 31, 2003 sales was $3.7 billion, an increase of nearly 11 percent over the prior year. Net income for the quarter were $459.5 million or $0.91 per diluted share compared to $369.6 million or $0.72 per share for the same period previous year."
