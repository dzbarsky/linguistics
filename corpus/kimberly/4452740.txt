LPC Group PLC acquired duffel mill from Kimberly-Clark..
"LPC Group PLC acquired duffel mill from Kimberly-Clark on June 11, 2007. The fianancial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. Under the terms of agreement, business and personnel of duffel mill will be transferred to LPC Group PLC in August 2007. There will be no job losses due to change in ownership. Management and sales for Duffel will be controlled from LPC Group PLC head office. LPC Group PLC completed the acquisition of duffel mill from Kimberly-Clark on June 11, 2007."
