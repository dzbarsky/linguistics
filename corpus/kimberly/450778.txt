Kimberly-Clark Announces 17.6 Percent Dividend Increase Payable on April 2, 2004.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that its board of directors has approved the previously announced dividend increase of 17.6 percent in 2004. This will bring the quarterly dividend to 40 cents per share, up from 34 cents per share in 2003. The board of directors declared the dividend payable on April 2, 2004, to stockholders of record on March 5, 2004.
