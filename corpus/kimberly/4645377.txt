Update to Kimberly-Clark Corp.'s Equity Buyback on July 24, 2007.
On July 24, 2007, Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that it has entered into an accelerated share repurchase (ASR) agreement with Bank of America for the purchase of approximately 29,600,000 shares of its common stock at an initial purchase price of $67.48, for a total cost of $2 billion. The initial settlement of the ASR is expected to occur on or about July 27, 2007, and the company will fund the share repurchase with incremental debt financing. Bank of America is expected to repurchase an equivalent number of shares in the open market during the period from July 26, 2007 to June 20, 2008. The ASR Agreement also includes a provision that would allow Bank of America, at its discretion, to accelerate the program so that the Repurchase Period may end as early as March 10, 2008. The initial purchase price per share is subject to an adjustment based on the volume weighted average price per share of the Corporation's shares of common stock during the Repurchase Period.
