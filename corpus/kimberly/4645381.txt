Kimberly-Clark Corp. announces an Equity Buyback.
The Board of Directors of Kimberly-Clark Corp. (NYSE: KMB) authorized a share repurchase program on July 24, 2007. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to 50,000,000 shares of its common stock. The shares will be purchased in the open market over the next several years. The actual number of shares purchased under the authorization and the timing of the transactions will depend upon prevailing market conditions.
