Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter of 2007; Revised Earnings Guidance for Fiscal 2007.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced earnings results for the second quarter of 2007. For the quarter net income rose to $461.8 million, or $1 per share, from $377.6 million, or 82 cents per share, a year ago. Sales climbed 8% to $4.5 billion from $4.16 billion in the year-ago period. The company lifted its earnings guidance for fiscal 2007 to a range of $4.20 to $4.25, including a 3-cent benefit from increased share repurchases. Previously, the company forecast adjusted earnings per share of $4.10 to $4.20."
