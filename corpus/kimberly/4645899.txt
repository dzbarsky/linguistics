Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Six Months Ended June 30, 2007.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the six months ended June 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $913.8 million or $1.99 per diluted share on net sales of $8,887.3 million against net income of $652.7 million or $1.41 per diluted share on net sales of $8,229.3 million in the year ago.
