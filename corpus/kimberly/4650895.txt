Kimberly-Clark Corp. Elects Ian C. Read to its Board of Directors, Effective August 15, 2007.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that Ian C. Read, senior vice president of Pfizer Inc. and president of Pfizer's worldwide pharmaceutical operations, has been elected to its board of directors, effective August 15, 2007. Mr. Read, has been in his current position since 2006.
