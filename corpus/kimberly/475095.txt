Kimberly-Clark plans Beech Island Mill plant expansion.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that it could add up to 200 jobs and spend $200 million to expand its Beech Island Mill plant during the next decade. The company and state announced expansion plans for the plant, which makes tissues, paper towels, napkins, disposable diapers and other paper products. The plant has about 1,00 workers. The expansion of some product lines and modernization of other lines could take about 10 years to complete.
