Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2007; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2007; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2007.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2007. For the quarter, net sales increased 9.7% to $4,620.6 million from $4,210.4 million reported for the same period year earlier. The improvement was highlighted by excellent volume gains for many of the company's well-known Personal Care, Consumer Tissue and K-C Professional brands and a twelfth consecutive quarter of double-digit sales expansion in developing and emerging markets. The company reported net income of $453.1 million against $364.2 million for the same period year earlier. Diluted net income per share was $1.04 compared with $0.79 in the prior year. The growth in sales was driven by a 5% increase in sales volumes, along with improved net selling prices and product mix, each approximately 1% better than the prior year.
