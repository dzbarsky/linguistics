Update to Kimberly-Clark Corporations Equity Buyback on October 22, 2007.
On October 22, 2007, Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced that it has repurchased approximately 33,000,000 shares of its common stock at a cost of more than $2.2 billion, including the purchase of 29,600,000 shares on July 24, 2007, under an accelerated share repurchase agreement with Bank of America for an initial payment of $2.0 billion, during the third quarter. Year-to-date, the Company has spent more than $2.5 billion to repurchase about 37,300,000 shares of Kimberly-Clark stock. The Company reaffirmed its commitment to repurchase $2.8 billion of its common stock in 2007.
