(NYSE: KMB) approved the repurchase of 50,000,000 shares of Kimberly-Clark common stock during the next several years in an amount not to exceed $5 billion on September 15, 2005. The actual number of shares purchased and the timing of the transactions will depend upon prevailing market conditions. On July 24, 2007, Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that it has entered into an accelerated share repurchase (ASR) agreement with Bank of America for the purchase of approximately 29,600,000 shares of its common stock at an initial purchase price of $67.48, for a total cost of $2 billion. The initial settlement of the ASR is expected to occur on or about July 27, 2007, and the company will fund the share repurchase with incremental debt financing. Bank of America is expected to repurchase an equivalent number of shares in the open market during the period from July 26, 2007 to June 20, 2008.
