Kimberly-Clark Corp. Announces Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2007; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Full Year of 2008.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net sales of $4,758.1 million and net income of $456.0 million or $1.07 per share against net sales of $4,307.2 million and net income of $482.6 million or $1.05 per share for the same period a year ago. For 2008, the company expects net sales growth of 5 to 7%, as foreign currency effects, at current rates, should benefit sales comparisons by at least 1%.
