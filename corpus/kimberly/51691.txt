Kimberly-Clark Launches Thinner PULL-UPS Disposable Training Pants.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. has just launched an improved PULL-UPS training pant that looks and fits more like Big Kid underwear than any other training pant on the market, according to moms with a preference. The new product features new toddler size indicators, has new Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse characters and are nearly 30 percent thinner than current PULL-UPS.
