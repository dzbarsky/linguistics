Kimberly-Clark Provides Guidance for the Second Quarter of 2004.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. Provides Guidance for the second quarter of 2004, The company expects to overcome recent increases in fiber, polymer and oil costs to deliver earnings in a range of 87 to 89 cents per share, an improvement of 6% to 9% compared with earnings of 82 cents per share in 2003.
