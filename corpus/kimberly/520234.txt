Kimberly-Clark Reports Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2004; Reaffirms Guidance for Full Year 2004.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported details of its results for the first quarter of 2004. Net income was $459.3 million and diluted net income was 91 cents per share for the quarter compared to net income of $397.7 million and diluted net income of 78 cents per share for the same quarter of previous year. Net sales were $3.8 billion compared to $3.4 billion for the same quarter of previous year.
