Kimberly-Clark Corp., Annual General Meeting, Apr 17, 2008.
Kimberly-Clark Corp., Annual General Meeting, Apr 17, 2008, at 11:00 Central Daylight. Location: Four Seasons Resort and Club, 4150 North MacArthur Boulevard, Irving, Texas,United States. Agenda: To elect five directors; to ratify the selection of Deloitte & Touche LLP as the independent auditors for 2008; to approve a proposal to amend the Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation to eliminate supermajority voting provisions; to vote on five stockholder proposals that may be presented at the meeting; and to take action upon any other business that may properly come before the meeting or any adjournments of the meeting.
