Kimberly-Clark Corp. Announces Departure of Oliver Cleaver as Media Director for Europe.
Oliver Cleaver, Kimberly-Clark Corp.'s media director for Europe, is leaving the company after a decade. Cleaver, who is responsible for media spend on brands including Andrex, Huggies and Kleenex, joined Kimberly-Clark in 1998. Prior to Kimberly-Clark, Cleaver was a managing director of Mediahaus Germany. He has also worked as a media director of ad agency Ayer Europe.
