Era Infra Engineering Limited and Kimberly-Clark Corp. Wins DMRC Order.
Era Infra Engineering Limited a flagship company of Era Group, announced that its joint venture with Ukraine's Kimberly-Clark Corp. has secured a INR 1,484 million order from Delhi Metro Rail Corp. (DMRC) for construction of an underground metro station and a tunnel. With this contract, the order backlog of Era Group has rose to INR 47,3109 million. The contract involves construction of Dwarka Sector-21 underground station, cover tunnel and related works. The project is scheduled to be completed by November 2009.
