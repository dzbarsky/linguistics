Kimberly-Clark Corp. agreed to Spin-Off Neenah Paper, Inc..
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced the spin-off of Neenah Paper, Inc. on November 4, 2004. Under the terms of the transaction, the shareholders of Kimberly-Clark will receive one share of Neenah Paper for every 33 share of Kimberly Clark held by them The record date for the transaction is November 19, 2004. The transaction is expected to be completed by November 30, 2004.
