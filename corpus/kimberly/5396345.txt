Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter and Full Year of 2008.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported a net income of $440.9 million or diluted earnings of $1.04 per share on a net sales of $4,812.7 million against a net income of $452.0 million or diluted earnings of $0.98 per share on a net sales of $4,385.3 million for the same period in 2007. The first quarter sales growth of 9.7% reflected increased sales volumes of nearly 3%, higher net selling prices of about 2% and favorable product mix of approximately 1%. Stronger foreign currencies boosted sales by more than 4%. Adjusted earnings were $457.0 million or $1.08 per share against adjusted earnings of $474.7 million or $1.03 per share in the same period last year. The company provided earnings guidance for the second quarter and full year of 2008. For the second quarter, the company expects adjusted earnings per share to be in a range of $1.08 to $1.11.
