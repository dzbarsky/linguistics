On April 22, 2008, Kimberly-Clark Corp announced that it has repurchased approximately 3,100,000 shares for a total of approximately $200 million, during the first quarter of 2008. Till December 31, 2007, the company has repurchased 8,014,411 shares for a total cost of $552.56 million. As of March 31, 2008, the Company has repurchased approximately 11,114,411 shares for a total of $752.56 million.
