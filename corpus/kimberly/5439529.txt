Kimberly-Clark Mulls Acquisitions.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. said that the company will continue invest and grow its business and is mulling to do more tuck-in acquisitions. Tom Falk, CEO of Kimberly, said: Developing and emerging markets has been a place that we have found is a good opportunity to continue to invest and grow our business. In terms of the signal on toiletries, and whether that should, we should look for more tuck-in acquisitions.
