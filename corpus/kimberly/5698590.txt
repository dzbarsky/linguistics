Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Six Months Ended June 30, 2008 ; Declares Dividend for the Six Months Ended June 30, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Full Year of 2008.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the six months ended June 30, 2008. For the period, the company reported net income of $857.6 million or $2.04 per diluted share on net sales of $9,818.9 million compared to net income of $913.8 million or $1.99 per diluted share on net sales of $8,887.3 million for the same period a year ago. Adjusted earnings per share increased 2% to $2.11 in 2008 from $2.07 in 2007. For the full year to 2008, the company expects adjusted earnings per share in the range of $4.20-$4.30. Earnings per diluted share in the range of $4.07-$4.19."
