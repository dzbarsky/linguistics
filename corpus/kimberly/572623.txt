G. Craig Sullivan Elected to Kimberly-Clark's Board of Directors.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. announced that G. Craig Sullivan, former chairman and CEO of The Clorox Company, has been elected to its board of directors, effective September 1, 2004. Mr. Sullivan, 64, retired as Chairman of Clorox Company in December of 2003. Mr. Sullivan's career in the consumer products industry spans 37 years. He joined Clorox in 1971 and held a number of senior sales and management positions during his career, culminating in his election as chief executive officer and chairman of the board in July 1992.
