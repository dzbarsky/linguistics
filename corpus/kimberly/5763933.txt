Kimberly-Clark Corp. Invests $6 Million in Marketing its Core Brands.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. is investing $6 million in a new marketing communications strategy for its three core brands this autumn - Kleenex, Andrex and Huggies. The company is re branding its Andrex Moist toilet tissue as Andrex Fresh, to help trigger growth in the moist sub-category of the UK toilet-tissue market. At the same time, Kleenex Mansize tissues are being repackaged in smaller boxes, backed by a $2.5 million campaign.
