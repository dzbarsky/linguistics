REPEAT/Darius Enterprises Updates Lawsuit Against Kimberly-Clark.
In a lawsuit filed by Howard Rubin of Darius Enterprises Inc., against Kimberly-Clark Corp. In Utah State Federal Court is alleged to have fraudulently withheld true royalty payments and for misrepresenting contractual warranties by not disclosing all documents and agreements at the signing of contract. Darius Enterprises CEO, Howard Rubin, is an inventor of the respiratory product known as Mist-Assist. These charges are being added to the suit originally filed in September 1999.
