Kimberly-Clark Corporation Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2008 ; Updates Earnings Guidance for the Year 2008.
"Kimberly-Clark Corporation reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net sales advanced 8.2% to $5.0 billion. Diluted net income per share in the third quarter of 2008 was $0.99 compared with $1.04 in the prior year. Adjusted earnings for the quarter were $1.02 per share versus $1.07 per share in 2007 and in line with the company's previous guidance range of $0.98 to $1.03 per share. The net income for the quarter was $413.1 million compared to $453.1 million for the same period a year ago. For the first nine months of 2008, sales of $14.8 billion rose 9.7% from $13.5 billion in the prior year. Adjusted operating profit was $1,978 million, down approximately 3% from $2,038 million in 2007. Through nine months, diluted net income per share in 2008 was $3.03, the same as in 2007.
