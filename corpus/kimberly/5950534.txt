Update to Kimberly-Clark Corporation's Equity Buyback on September 30, 2008.
On October 22, 2008, Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced that during the quarter ended September 30, 2008, it repurchased 2,200,000 shares at a cost of $130 million. As of September 30, 2008, the company has repurchased 16,767,411 shares for a total cost of $1.1 billion under the program announced on July 24, 2007.
