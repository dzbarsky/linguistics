The Law Firm of Goldman Scarlato & Karon, P.C. Reminds Investors That May 2, 2005, is the Last Day to Join Class Action Lawsuit against Molex.
Goldman Scarlato & Karon, P.C. reminds investors that May 2, 2005 is the last day to join a lawsuit that has been filed in the United States District Court for the Northern District of Illinois, on behalf of persons who purchased or otherwise acquired publicly traded securities of Molex Inc. between July 27, 2004 and February 14, 2005, inclusive. The lawsuit was filed against Molex, J. Joseph King and Diane S. Bullock.
