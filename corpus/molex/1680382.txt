Molex Inc. Announces New Appointments.
As previously disclosed, on April 22, 2005 Molex Inc.'s Board of Directors appointed Martin P. Slark as Vice Chairman and Chief Executive Officer and Liam McCarthy to serve as President and Chief Operating Officer of Molex effective July 1, 2005. Certain terms of the compensation of Messrs. Slark and McCarthy in their new positions were determined by the Compensation Committee on June 1, 2005.
