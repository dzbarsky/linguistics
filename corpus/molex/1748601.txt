Molex Inc. Announces Board Changes.
Molex Inc. appointed David L. Landsittel to serve as a member of the Board of Directors. Mr. Landsittel was appointed as a Class I director, and will stand for stockholder election at the 2005 Annual Meeting of Stockholders. This appointment expands the Molex Board to 13 directors. David L. Landsittel replaced Michelle L. Collins on the Audit Committee upon his appointment.
