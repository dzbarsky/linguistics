Molex Inc. Offers New Optical Military Cable Assemblies.
Molex Inc. has introduced Optical Military Cable Assemblies for a variety of military, avionics, flight control, mobile tactical field command platforms, Electro Magnetic Interference sensitive and security applications. These high-end, cost effective military style connector solutions are available with fiber optic MIL-T-19504 approved termini, coupled with Commercial Off the Shelf (COTS) cabling and fiber optic connectors. They utilize ruggedized connectors designed for harsh environments, and can be terminated to both single-fiber and ribbon cable and are available in 2- to 29-pin configurations to meet MIL-STD-1560 requirements. Various finish types are also offered with plug, jam-nut and wall-mount receptacle options.
