Molex Inc. Reports Earning Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended 30 June, 2005 ; Announces Repurchase of 1,030,000 Shares of Class A Common Stock and 425,000 Shares of Common Stock.
"Molex Inc. reported earning results for the fourth quarter and full year ended 30 June, 2005. For the fourth quarter, the company reported net income of $4,882,000 or $0.03 diluted earnings per share on net revenues of $643,762,000, against net income of $57,201,000 or $0.30 diluted earnings per share on net revenues of $631,817,000 reported for the same period, a year ago. The company also announced that during the June quarter, the Company repurchased 1,030,000 shares of Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and 425,000 shares of Common Stock (MOLX) at a total cost of $34.6 million.
