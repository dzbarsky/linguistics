Announced Private Placement Transaction - Target: Sheldahl Inc.; Investor(s): Molex Inc., Morgenthaler; Transaction Size: $3 mm.
"Sheldahl Inc. (OTCPK:SHEL.Q) announced that it has entered into a subordinated notes and warrant purchase agreement with Morganthaler Venture Partners V, L.P. and Molex Incorporated. The investors extended $3 million in financing to the company. Morganthaler and Molex each purchased $1.5 million of 12% senior subordinated notes. In connection with the notes, each purchaser was issued 351,000 warrants for the purchase of the company's common stock at an exercise price of $0.01 per share of common stock issuable. The warrants expire on August 13, 2008. The Notes are subordinate to the obligations of the Company to Wells Fargo Bank Minnesota, N.A. On August 13, 2001, Sheldahl Inc. completed the transaction."
