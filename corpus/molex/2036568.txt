Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Schott Optovance, Optical Shuffle and Light Dealer Product Lines and Related Assets; Buyer(s): Molex Inc.; Seller(s): Schott Optovance Inc..
Schott Optovance sold certain assets including the Optical Shuffle and Light Dealer product lines, to Molex Inc. (Nasdaq: MOLX). The terms of the deal were not disclosed. Molex has completed the transfer of the operation to its Bolingbrook, Illinois facility.
