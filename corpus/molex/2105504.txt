Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Cardell Corporation; Buyer(s): Molex Inc.; Transaction Size: $69.4 mm.
"Molex Incorporated (NASDAQ:MOLX and MOLXA), entered into an agreement to acquire Cardell Corporation. In connection with this acquisition, the former shareholders of Cardell received 2.3 million shares of Molex Common Stock (MOLX), approximate market value $69.4 million, which were exempt from registration under Section 4(2) of the Securities Act of 1933. William Blair & Co. L.L.C. served as a financial advisor to Molex Incorporated. Molex Incorporated completed the acquisition of Cardell Corporation on 06/18/1999 for $69.4 million."
