Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Incep Technologies; Buyer(s): Molex Inc.; Seller(s): Kestrel Venture Management, Odyssey Venture Partners, LLC.
"Molex Incorporated (Nasdaq: MOLX, MOLXA) announced that it has acquired INCEP Technologies, Inc. Molex will incorporate INCEP's assets and programs into its own operations.
