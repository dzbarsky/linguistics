Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Sheldahl, Inc., Subtantially All Assets; Buyer(s): Ampersand Ventures, Molex Inc., Morgenthaler; Seller(s): Sheldahl Inc..
"Sheldahl, Inc. (NASDAQ: SHELQ) announced it has received a letter of intent from Ampersand Ventures, Molex Inc., and Morgenthaler Partners for the purchase of a substantial portion of its assets. The offer includes a majority of the company's operating assets, contracts, and certain obligations necessary to maintaining its operations. On August 26, 2002, Sheldahl, Inc. announced that the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the District of Minnesota has approved the sale of its continuing business assets to the company's three largest shareholders, Ampersand Ventures, Molex Incorporated and Morgenthaler, following the company's voluntary petition for Chapter 11 reorganization. The company said it anticipated that the transaction would be completed within 30 days. On August 30, 2002, Sheldahl, Inc. completed the sale of substantially all of its continuing business assets to Northfield Acquisition Co., a new company owned by Sheldahl's three largest shareholders, Ampersand Ventures, Molex Incorporated, and Morgenthaler"
