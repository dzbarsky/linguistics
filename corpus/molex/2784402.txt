Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended March 31, 2006 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ended June 30, 2006 ; Declares Dividend.
"Molex Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended March 31, 2006. Revenue for the quarter ended March 31, 2006 was a record $720.3 million, an increase of 17.5% over the same period last fiscal year. Net income for the March quarter was $61.5 million, or $0.33 per share, compared with $44.8 million, or $0.24 per share in the prior year quarter. Included in the current quarter results was a restructuring charge of $4.3 million, relating to the previously announced restructuring program. Net income for the March quarter rose 5.1% sequentially from the quarter ended December 31, 2005. Revenue for the nine-months ended March 31, 2006 was $2.08 billion, an increase of 9.1% compared with the same period in the prior fiscal year. Net income of $166.7 million, or $0.89 per share, rose 11.4% compared with last year's net income of $149.6 million, or $0.78 per share.
