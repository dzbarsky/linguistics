Molex Inc. Approves Regular Quarterly Cash Dividend Payable on July 25, 2006.
Molex Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has approved an increase in the regular quarterly cash dividend to $0.075 per share, an increase of 50% from the previous cash dividend of $0.05 per share. The new regular quarterly cash dividend will be paid on July 25, 2006 to shareholders of record on June 30, 2006 for each share of Common Stock (MOLX), Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and Class B Common Stock, and will continue quarterly until further action by the Board.
