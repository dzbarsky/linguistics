Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended June 30, 2006; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter Ending September 30, 2006 and Full Year Ending June 30, 2007.
"Molex Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended June 30, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $70,346,000 or $0.38 diluted earnings per share on net revenue of $783,799,000 against the net income of $4,882,000 or $0.03 diluted earnings per share on net revenue of $643,762,000 for the same period a year earlier. The company provides earnings guidance for the fiscal first quarter and full year of 2007. For the quarter, the company expects that revenue for the first fiscal quarter ending September 30, 2006, will be in a range of $760 to $780 million, representing a mid-range increase of 16.7%, compared with last year's first fiscal quarter.
