Molex Inc. Announces Resignation of Joe Laymon from Board.
Molex Inc. announced that board member Joe Laymon had resigned to focus on his work as an executive at Ford Motor Co. Following Laymon's resignation, the board fixed the number of directors at 12 and the number of independent directors at three. The three remaining independent nominees will stand for re-election at the annual meeting on Oct. 27, according to the filing.
