Molex Inc. Revised Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of 2007.
Molex Inc. revised earnings guidance for the second quarter of 2007. The company now expects to earnings of 34 to 37 cents a share vs. its previous forecast of 39 to 43 cents a share. Revenue is expected to be $830 million to $840 million, revised from a previous projection of $830 million to $850 million.
