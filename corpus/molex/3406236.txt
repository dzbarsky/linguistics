Molex Inc. Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of Fiscal 2007.
Molex Inc. revised earnings guidance for the full year of fiscal 2007. The company now expects its earnings results to be below its prior guidance due to the fewer orders in the second quarter. The company had predicted full-year net income in the range of $1.63 and $1.73 per share on revenue between $3.2 billion and $3.5 billion.
