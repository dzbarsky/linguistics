Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended December 31, 2006 ; Declares Dividend for the Second Quarter of Fiscal 2007; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter Ending March 31, 2007.
"Molex Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended December 31, 2006. For the second quarter, the company reported net income of $66,361,000 or $0.36 per diluted share on net revenue of $837,467,000 against net income of $58,503,000 or $0.31 per diluted share on net revenue of $697,348,000 for the same period last year. The Company estimates that revenue for the third fiscal quarter ending March 31, 2007 will be in a range of $800 to $830 million, and that earnings per share will be in a range of $0.32 to $0.36."
