Molex Reports 2002 Fiscal Third Quarter Results; Fourth quarter Results Forecast.
"Molex Inc. reported results for its fiscal third quarter and first nine months ended March 31, 2002. Revenues for the 2002 fiscal third quarter of $408.3 million decreased 31.9 percent versus last year's third quarter revenues of$599.8 million. Net income was $19.7 million, compared with $60.8 million of net income for the same quarter a year ago. Earnings per share were $0.10. This compares with $0.31 for the third quarter of last year. Currency translation decreased net revenues by $12.8 million and decreased net income by $0.8 million. Revenues for the nine months ended March 31, 2002 were $1.26 billion, compared with $1.86 billion in the prior fiscal year period, a 32.3 percent decrease. Net income prior to the charge and tax adjustments recorded in the December second quarter was $67.9 million, or 5.4 percent of sales.
