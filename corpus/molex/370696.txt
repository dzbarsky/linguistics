Molex Reports 2002 Fiscal Fourth Quarter and Year-End Results; Provides Outlook for Fiscal 2003.
"Molex Inc. reported results for its fiscal fourth quarter and fiscal year ended June 30, 2002. Revenues for the 2002 fiscal fourth quarter of $456.3 million, declined 10.6 percent versus last year's fourth quarter revenues of$510.5 million. Net income was $27.3 million compared with $10.8 million in the same quarter a year ago, which included a $30.3 million after tax charge. Earnings per share were $0.14, compared with $0.05 in the same quarter a year ago. Earnings per share for the same quarter a year ago, prior to the charge were $0.21. For the year ended June 30, 2002, revenues were $1.7 billion, compared with $2.4 billion in the prior fiscal year period, a 27.6 percent decrease. Net income was $76.5 million or 4.5 percent of sales, compared with $203.9 million or 8.6 percent of sales for the prior fiscal year. Net income for fiscal 2002 prior to these items was $96.8 million or 5.7 percent of sales. This compares to$234.2 million prior to the charge reported in fiscal 2001. Earnings per share in fisc"
