Molex Inc. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ending June 30, 2007.
Molex Inc. provides earnings guidance for the fourth quarter ending June 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company expects sales to rise between 3% and 8%. The company estimates earnings to range from 34 cents to 38 cents per share. Revenue is forecast between $810 million and $850 million. A year ago, the company earned $70.4 million, or 38 cents per share, on sales of $783.8 million.
