Molex Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended March 31, 2007.
Molex Inc. reported earnings results for the nine months ended March 31, 2007. For the nine months, the company reported a net income of $208.04 million or $1.12 per diluted share on the revenue of $2.5 billion against the net income of $165.6 million or $0.88 per diluted share on the revenue of $2.1 billion for the same period a year ago.
