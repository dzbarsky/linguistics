Molex Inc. Announces 2004 Fiscal Second Quarter and Six Months Results.
"Molex Inc. announced results for its second fiscal quarter ended December 31, 2003. Revenues were of $548.9 million increased 20.8 percent from last year's second quarter of $454.6 million. Net income was $41.2 million compared with last year's second quarter of $28.2 million, an increase of 46.3 percent. Earnings per share of $0.21 increased 40.0 percent compared with $0.15 reported for the second quarter a year ago. Revenues for the six months ended December 31, 2003 were $1,045.7 million, compared with $923.8 million in the prior fiscal year period, a 13.2 percent increase. Net income of $73.3 million increased 26.0 percent when compared with last year's net income of $58.2 million. Earnings per share were $0.38, compared with $0.30 reported for the prior fiscal year."
