Molex Inc.'s Equity Buyback in the amount of $250 mm has expired.
"Molex Inc. (NasdaqNM:MOLX) announced that the Board of Directors has authorized the Company to repurchase Molex shares on a discretionary basis for up to an aggregate value of $250 million. The authorization allows the repurchase of the Company's outstanding Common Stock and/or Class A Common Stock during the period ending December 31, 2006. This replaces the Company's current authorization to repurchase shares up to an aggregate value of $100 million during the fiscal year ending June 30, 2005. The repurchase program of Molex Inc. expired on August 13, 2007. Under the program, the company has repurchased approximately 8,721,000 shares of Common Stock and Class A Common Stock for $234.80 million."
