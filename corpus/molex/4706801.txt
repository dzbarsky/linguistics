Molex Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $200 mm.
The Board of Directors of Molex Inc. (NasdaqNM:MOLX) authorized a share repurchase program on August 13, 2007. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $200 million of its outstanding common stock and/or Class A common stock. The program will expire on June 30, 2008. Molex has approximately 184,100,000 shares outstanding.
