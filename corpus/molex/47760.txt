Molex Announces First Quarter 2003 Financial Results; Announces Second Quarter 2003 Earnings Guidance; Names Diane Bullock As Chief Financial Officer.
"Molex Inc. announced financial results for the first quarter of 2003. For first quarter 2003, the company reported a net income of $32.1 million or 17 cents a share on revenue of $496.8 million, where as it was reported a net income of $30 million or 15 cents a share. Thomson First Call expected the company to earn 16 cents a share for the first quarter of 2003. Molex named Diane Bullock as a chief financial officer who succeeds Bob Mahoney. After a transition period, Mahoney will move to Singapore to serve as regional president of the company's Far East South division."
