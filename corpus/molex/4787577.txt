Molex Inc., Annual General Meeting, Oct 26, 2007.
Molex Inc., Annual General Meeting, Oct 26, 2007, at 10:00 Central Daylight. Location: 2222 Wellington Court, Lisle, IL 60532, United States. Agenda: To approve the election of four Class II directors for a term of three years; to approve the amended 2000 Molex Long-Term Stock Plan; to approve the amended 2005 Molex Incentive Stock Option Plan; to approve theratification of the selection of Ernst & Young LLP as Molex's independent auditors for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2008; and to approve the any other business that properly comes before the meeting or any adjournments or postponements thereof.
