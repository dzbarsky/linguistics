Cyberstar Plans to Increase the Number of its Offices in India to 30 by 2008-2009.
Cyberstar Infocom Ltd. announced that it has been appointed as the national distributor for Molex. Belkin is also likely to appoint Cyberstar as its national distributor. Cyberstar has 22 offices across India. It has plans to increase this to 30 offices by 2008-2009.
