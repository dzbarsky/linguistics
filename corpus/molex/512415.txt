Molex Reports Results For Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended March 31, 2004; Company Announces the Repurchase of 950,000 Shares of MOLXA Common Stock; Revises Guidance For Fourth Quarter ending June 30, 2004.
"Molex Inc. reported results for its third quarter and nine months ended March 31, 2004. The company reported net income of $45.47 million or $0.24 per share on net revenue of $569.15 million compared to net income of $24.8 million or $0.13 per share on net revenue of $443.1 million for the same period a year ago. The company announced, during the quarter, that the Company repurchased 950,000 shares of MOLXA common stock at a total cost of $24.8 million. For the nine months ended March 31, 2004, the Company has repurchased 1,945,000 shares for a total cost of $50.2 million.
