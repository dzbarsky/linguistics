Molex Inc. (NasdaqNM: MOLX) acquired Century Circuits & Electronics, Inc..
"Molex Inc. (NasdaqNM: MOLX) acquired Century Circuits & Electronics, Inc. on November 1, 2005. The financial terms of the deal were not disclosed. Lincoln Partners LLC acted as financial advisor of Century Circuits & Electronics. Molex Inc. (NasdaqNM: MOLX) completed the acquisition of Century Circuits & Electronics, Inc. on November 1, 2005."
