Molex Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $200 mm.
The Board of Directors of Molex Inc. (NasdaqNM:MOLX) authorized a share repurchase program on August 5, 2008. Under the program the company will repurchase an amount of $200 million worth of common stock. The repurchase of Molex shares is on a discretionary basis. The authorization allows the repurchase of the outstanding Common Stock (MOLX) and/or Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) during the period ending June 30, 2009.
