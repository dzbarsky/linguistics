Molex Inc. closed an Equity Buyback in the amount of $200 mm.
"The Board of Directors of Molex Inc. (NasdaqNM:MOLX) authorized a share repurchase program on August 13, 2007. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $200 million of its outstanding common stock and/or Class A common stock. The program will expire on June 30, 2008. Molex has approximately 184,100,000 shares outstanding. Molex Inc. completed the repurchase program on June 30, 2008. Under the program, the company has repurchased 8,106,000 shares for a total cost of $200 million."
