Molex Inc., Annual General Meeting, Oct 31, 2008.
Molex Inc., Annual General Meeting, Oct 31, 2008, at 10:00 Central Daylight. Location: 2222 Wellington Court, Lisle, IL 60532,United States. Agenda: To elect four Class III directors for a term of three years; to approve the company's Annual Incentive Plan; to approve the company's 2008 Stock Incentive Plan; to ratify the selection of Ernst & Young LLP as independent auditors for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2009; and to consider any other business that properly comes before the meeting or any adjournments or postponements thereof.
