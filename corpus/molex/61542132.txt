Molex Inc. Announces Resignation of Kazumasa Kusaka as Member of the Board of Directors Effective July 31, 2009.
On July 21, 2009, Kazumasa Kusaka, who has served as a member of the Board of Directors of Molex Inc. since May 2007, notified that he is resigning from the Board, effective July 31, 2009. Mr. Kusaka is resigning due to a change in his principal occupation. Upon Mr. Kusakas resignation, the Board, in accordance with the companys Bylaws, will fix the size of the Board at 12 and the number of directors in Class II at three.
