Molex Inc. Reports Capital Expenditures for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended June 30, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Capital Expenditures Guidance for the Full Year 2010.
"Molex Inc. reported capital expenditures for the fourth quarter and full year ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, capital expenditures were $50.3 million. For the first quarter, the company expects revenue to be in the range of $590-$630 million and earnings per share to be $0.07."
