Molex Inc. Reports Consolidated Unaudited Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended December 31, 2008 ; Provides Revenue Guidance for the Third Quarter Ending March 2009 ; Announces Impairment Charge.
"Molex Inc. reported consolidated unaudited earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended December 31, 2008. For the second quarter, the company reported net loss of $87,244,000 or $0.50 per diluted share on net revenue of $666,728,000 against net income of $59,216,000 or $0.33 per diluted share on net revenue of $841,560,000 for the same period a year ago. The company announced that non-cash goodwill impairment charge of $93.1 million."
