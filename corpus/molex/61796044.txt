Molex Not To Sell French Plant.
The management of Molex Inc. said it did not foresee it self selling its plant in Villemur-sur-Tarn, which is scheduled for closure. The management, in a statement explained that it was not possible to execute a viable and durable economic project, compatible with the industrial strategies of the group. French Minister of Industry, Christian Estrosi said on August 2, that he would do everything possible to make the management accept selling this activity to an investor.
