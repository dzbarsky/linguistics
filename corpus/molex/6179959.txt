Update to Molex, Inc.s Equity Buyback on January 27, 2008.
On January 27, 2009, Molex, Inc. announced that during the quarter, it has repurchased 2,000,000 shares of common stock (MOLX) and 875,000 shares of Class A common stock (MOLXA) at a total cost of $37.5 million. As of December 31, 2008, Molex has repurchased approximately 2,732,000 shares of Class A common stock (MOLXA) and approximately 1,775,000 shares of common stock (MOLX) for a total cost of approximately $76.3 million under the program.
