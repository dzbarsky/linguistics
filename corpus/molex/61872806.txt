Molex Inc. Appoints Anirudh Dhebar to Board of Directors.
Molex Inc. announced that it has appointed Anirudh Dhebar as a Director. Dr. Dhebar is a professor of marketing at Babson College in Babson Park, MA. His election expands the Molex Board to 13 directors including nine outside directors. Professor Dhebar has a unique combination of technology, management and marketing education and experience. He is also very familiar with Molex's global business, having served as an instructor for more than 15 years in a Molex program that was specifically designed to develop the company's future leaders.
