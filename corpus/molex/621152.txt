Molex Inc. Declares Increased Cash Dividend Payable on October 25, 2004.
Molex Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has announced an increase in the regular quarterly cash dividend to $0.0375 per share, an increase of 50% from the previous cash dividend of $0.025 per share. The new regular quarterly cash dividend will be paid on October 25, 2004 to shareholders of record on September 30, 2004 for each share of Common Stock (MOLX), Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and Class B Stock.
