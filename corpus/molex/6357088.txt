Molex Inc. Announces Management Changes.
Dave B. Root, Executive Vice President and President of Molex Inc.'s Commercial Products Division was planning to retire in 2010, and has now decided to accelerate his retirement as a result of the consolidation of its Transportation Products Division and Commercial Products Division as part of global restructuring plan. As a result of such consolidation, effective March 31, 2009, David B. Root stepped down as President of the Commercial Products Division of company. Mr. Root will remain employed by Molex until his retirement on December 31, 2009, during which time he will remain available for consultation with the company. Effective April 1, 2009 James E. Fleischhacker has taken over as President of the newly consolidated division which will continue to be known as the Commercial Products Division.
