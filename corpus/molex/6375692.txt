Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) acquired the assets of Motorola Antenna/EMC Measurement Lab in Aalborg from Motorola Inc. (NYSE: MOT).
"Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) acquired the assets of Motorola Antenna/EMC Measurement Lab in Aalborg on April 13, 2009. Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) completed the acquisition of the assets of Motorola Antenna/EMC Measurement Lab in Aalborg on April 13, 2009."
