Multek, Inc. entered into an agreement to acquire Northfield Acquisition Co. from a group of private equity firms on August 16, 2004. The transaction is expected to close by the end of August 2004 and is subject to customary conditions and regulatory approvals. Multek will retain the Sheldahl name of the acquired business for its technical materials operations. William Payne of Dorsey & Whitney was the legal advisor for Northfield Acquisition Co.
