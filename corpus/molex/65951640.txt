Molex Inc. Revised Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter Ending September 30, 2009.
Molex Inc. revised earnings guidance for the first quarter ending September 30, 2009. Revenue for the September quarter is now expected to be in the range of $650 million to $665 million, compared with the prior outlook of $590 million to $630 million provided on August 4, 2009. At the midpoint of the new range, this represents a sequential increase of 15% from the June quarter. At the revised revenue level, a GAAP basis loss for the September quarter is now expected to be in the range of $0.12 to $0.08 per share, including pretax restructuring charges of approximately $60 million or $0.24 per share after-tax. The prior outlook for earnings per share was a range of $0.00 to $0.06, including a pretax restructuring charge of approximately $10 million or $0.04 per share after-tax. The company reported that non-GAAP basis earnings for the September quarter is now expected to be in the range of $0.12 to $0.16 per share. The prior outlook for earnings per share was a range of $0.04 to $0.10.
