Molex Inc. to Invest $30 Million Investment in Chengdu Plant.
Molex Inc., plans to invest an additional $30 million into its Chengdu plant in the capital city of Southwest China's Sichuan Province by the end of 2009. According to the supplementary agreement signed between Molex and Chengdu High-tech Industrial Development Zone, the company will increase registered capital in Chengdu plant by at least $10 million. In June 2005, Molex invested $70 million in the plant, and then in December added another $30 million. The enhancements were expected to prop it up as Molex's largest manufacturing base.
