Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended September 30, 2004; Provides Outlook for the Second Quarter Ending December 31, 2004.
"Molex Inc. reported record earnings results for its first fiscal quarter ended September 30, 2004. Revenue of $640.2 million increased 29% from last year's first quarter of $496.8 million. Net income was $55.6 million against last year's first quarter of $32.1 million, an increase of 74%. Earnings per share of $0.29 increased 71%, against $0.17 reported for the first quarter a year ago. The Company expects that revenue for the fiscal second quarter ending December 31, 2004, will be in a range of $635 - $650 million. This represents an increase of 16% - 18% over last year's fiscal second quarter. The Company expects that earnings per share will be in a range of $0.29 - $0.31, an increase of 38% - 48% over last year's fiscal second quarter earnings per share."
