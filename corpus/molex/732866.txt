Molex Inc. Announces Construction Plans for Proposed $70,000,000 Electronic Connector Plant.
Molex Inc. currently operates 3 electronic connector plants in China. And, the company is now gearing-up to build its 4th such factory. The new facility, of undisclosed size, is to be sited somewhere between the southwestern cities of Chongqing and Chengdu, China. In any case, Chongqing or Chengdu is most likely to become the location of the new plant. This is because both cities reportedly include some of the world's largest automobile and electronic makers. Companies that are potential consumers of the company's products. The cost to build the new facility has been estimated at $70,000,000. In so doing, MOLEX expects the unit to subsequently generate some $200,000,000 of business revenue each year.
