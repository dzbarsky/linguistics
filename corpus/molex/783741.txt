Molex Announces Executive Changes ; Appoints Ernst & Young as its Independent Auditor.
"Molex Inc. named co-Chairman Frederick Krehbiel as Chief Executive Officer. The company announced that under a new CEO succession plan, it is expected that President and Chief Operating Officer Martin Slark will replace Krehbiel as CEO in the first quarter of 2006. J. Joseph King resigned his roles of vice chairman and CEO. The company announced the appointment of Ernst & Young as its independent auditor."
