Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) acquired Zhenjiang Tean Telecom and Appliance Co., Ltd..
"Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) acquired Zhenjiang Tean Telecom and Appliance Co., Ltd. on November 18, 2009. The financial terms of the deal were not disclosed. Upon completion, Zhenjiang Tean Telecom will operate as a subsidiary of Molex and will be part of Molex' global integrated products division. Euan Rellie, Jeffrey Wang, Shan Yue and Xiang Li of Business Development Asia LLC acted as financial advisor for Molex. Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) completed the acquisition of Zhenjiang Tean Telecom and Appliance Co., Ltd. on November 18, 2009."
