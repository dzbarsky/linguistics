The New York Times Company, Annual General Meeting, Apr 27, 2011.
The New York Times Company, Annual General Meeting, Apr 27, 2011., at 10:04 US Eastern Standard Time. Location: TheTimesCenter. Agenda: To elect a Board of 13 members; to hold an advisory vote on executive compensation; to hold an advisory vote on the frequency of future advisory votes on executive compensation; to ratify the selection of Ernst & Young LLP, an independent registered public accounting firm, as auditors for the fiscal year ending December 25, 2011; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting.
