The New York Times Company Closes Its Offer to Exchange Its 6.625% Senior Notes Due 2016.
The New York Times Company announced the successful closing of its offer to exchange all of its outstanding $225.0 million aggregate principal amount of 6.625% Senior Notes due 2016, which were not registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, for an equal principal amount of its 6.625% Senior Notes due 2016, which have been registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. All of the company's unregistered 6.625% Senior Notes due 2016 have been exchanged.
