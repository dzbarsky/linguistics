Active International Ltd., Association of National Advertisers Inc., ANA Advertising Financial Management Conference, May 01, 2011 through May 04, 2011. Venue: Arizona Biltmore,, 2400 E Missouri Ave,, Phoenix, Arizona, United States. The annual ANA Advertising Financial Management Conference presented by Active International is the only event of its kind. It brings together top marketing finance and procurement professionals from the client side with agency CFOs and other key industry stakeholders interested in efficiencies, cost savings, return on investment, and delivering greater value to organizations.
