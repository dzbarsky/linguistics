Kushner Eyeing New England Media.
According to report in the Boston Globe, Aaron Kushner is preparing to offer more than $200 million to buy The New England Media Group from The New York Times Company. Citing a person who has been briefed on the plan, said Aaron Kushner, the report added that the Kushner will make a formal offer within the next few weeks for The New England Media Group. Kushner said, "When we have all of the pieces in place to not just purchase but enrich the institutions, we look forward to making a formal offer." Kushner refused to disclose how many investors have joined him or what he intends to bid. He also declined to say if his eventual bid will include the assumption of the Globe's pension liabilities. When contacted by Dow Jones, a spokeswoman for the Times declined to comment.
