New York Times Co. Reports Revenue Results for the Month of January 2005.
New York Times Co. reported revenue results for the month of January 2005. The company reported that in January 2005 advertising revenues for the Company's business units increased 1.2% to $191,049,000 against $188,876,000 the results for the same month in 2004 and total Company revenues increased 0.2% to $291,016,000 against $290,454,000 the results for the same month in 2004.
