New York Times Company Declares Regular Quarterly Dividend, Payable on March 18, 2005.
New York Times Co.'s Board of Directors declared a regular quarterly dividend of $0.155 per share on the Company's Class A and Class B common stock. The dividend is payable on March 18, 2005, to shareholders of record on March 1, 2005. This dividend is the 145th consecutive quarterly dividend paid on the Company's common stock since the Company went public in 1969.
