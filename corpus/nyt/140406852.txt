Betaworks announced that it has received $20 million in funding from AOL Ventures, Draper Fisher Jurvetson, Founder Collective, Intel Capital, RRE Ventures LLC, SoftBank Capital, SOFTBANK Corp., The New York Times Company..
"Betaworks announced that it has raised $20 million in its series B round of funding consisting of common shares led by RRE Ventures LLC and Intel Capital on March 10, 2010. The round included participation from Draper Fisher Jurvetson Growth 2006 Fund L.P., a fund managed by Draper Fisher Jurvetson, AOL Ventures, The New York Times, SOFTBANK Corp., SoftBank Capital, Lerer Investments, and Founder Collective. The securities will be issued to 24 investors pursuant to Regulation D. The company has raised $28,000,000 till date. On March 11, 2010, Betaworks closed the transaction."
