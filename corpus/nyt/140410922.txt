Ongo Inc. announced that it has received $12 million in funding from Gannett Co., Inc., The New York Times Company, The Washington Post Company..
"Ongo Inc. announced that it will raise $12 million in an equity series A round of funding from three investors on September 27, 2010. The securities will be issued pursuant to Regulation D under the Securities Act. On September 29, 2010, Ongo Inc. closed the transaction. The round saw participation from new investors Gannett Co., Inc., The New York Times Company, and The Washington Post Company. The investors contributed $4 million each and all will have a seat on the company's board."
