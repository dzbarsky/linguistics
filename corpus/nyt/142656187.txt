NewsStand, Inc. announced that it has received $7.2 million in funding from Adams Capital Management, Inc., Noro-Moseley Partners, SSM Ventures, The New York Times Company, and other investors..
NewsStand, Inc. announced that it has raised $7,200,000 in a round of funding led by existing investor Adams Capital Management, Inc. on June 22, 2004. The round also included participation from existing investors Noro-Moseley Partners which invested through its fund Noro-Moseley Partners V, L.P., SSM Ventures, The New York Times Company, and other investors.
