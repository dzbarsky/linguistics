LibreDigital, Inc. announced that it has received $15 million in funding from Adams Capital Management, Inc., HarperCollins Publishers L.L.C., Noro-Moseley Partners, The New York Times Company, and Triangle Peak Partners Inc..
"LibreDigital, Inc. announced that it has raised $15 million in its second round of funding co-led by new investor Triangle Peak Partners. Inc. and returning investor Adams Capital Management, Inc. on July 28, 2009. The round also included participation from existing investor HarperCollins Publishers L.L.C., The New York Times Company, and Noro-Moseley Partners which invested through its fund Noro-Moseley Partners V, L.P. As part of the round, David L. Pesikoff, President at Triangle Peak Partners. Inc. will join LibreDigital, Inc.'s board of directors. On July 28, 2009, LibreDigital, Inc. closed the transaction."
