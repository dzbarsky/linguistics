NYTimes.com Launches 'New York Style,' a Free Weekly E-mail On the Latest in Fashion and Entertaining.
NYTimes.com announced that it is launching New York Style, a free weekly e-mail newsletter focusing on the best that New York has to offer in shopping, food, entertainment, entertaining and trends. Users may sign up for the newsletter at www.nytimes.com/email. Delivered every Wednesday afternoon, New York Style highlights content from various sections of The New York Times including Sunday Styles, Arts & Leisure, Dining In/Dining Out, The City and the House & Home sections. New York Times Digital is the digital business unit of The New York Times Company and includes market leaders NYTimes.com.
