The New York Times Company Reports Second-Quarter Results 2003.
"New York Times Company announced that second-quarter diluted earnings per share were $0.47, compared with $0.51 in the 2002 second quarter, and net income was $72.8 million compared with $78.8 million in the second quarter last year. Total revenues for the Company rose 3.8 percent to $801.9 million in the second quarter from $772.2 million in the 2002 second quarter. For six months ended 2003 diluted earnings per share were $0.92, compared with $0.86 in the 2002, and net income was $141.7 million compared with $133.2 million in the second quarter last year. Total revenues of $1585.6 million for six months ended 2003 as compared from $1509.3 million in the 2002 same period."
