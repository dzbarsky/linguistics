New York Times Co. Announces Revenue Results for the Month of February 2005.
The New York Times Co. announced that in February 2005 advertising revenues for the Company's business units increased 3.1% to $172,350,000 against $167,088,000 for the same month in 2004 and total Company revenues increased 2.1% to $257,314,000 against $251,943,000 the results for the same month in 2004. Circulation revenues increased 0.8% to $67,869,000 against $67,319,000 the results for the same month in 2004.
