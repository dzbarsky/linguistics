The New York Times Introduces The New York Times Knowledge Network.
The New York Times unveiled a national education program, called The New York Times Knowledge Network, which will unite all of The Times's innovative education efforts nationwide. As the platform for a robust composite of new and well-established Times education initiatives, The New York Times Knowledge Network is a dedicated resource to help parents and educators enhance student learning at all education levels, particularly in the areas of analytical reading, critical thinking, problem-solving, vocabulary and writing. The New York Times Knowledge Network is dedicated to inspiring thought among future generations by providing students, teachers, and parents alike with an expanding array of programs and resources designed to stimulate thought in and outside of the classroom.
