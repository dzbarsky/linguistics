The New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter of 2005.
The New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the first quarter of 2005. The company reported net profits of $111 million or 76 cents per share, against $58.4 million, or 38 cents per share, in the comparable period a year ago. Revenues rose 0.5% in the quarter to $805.6 million from $801.9 million in the same period a year ago.
