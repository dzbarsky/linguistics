The New York Times Company Raises Quarterly Dividend Payable on June 15, 2005.
The New York Times Company's Board of Directors declared a 6.5% increase in the regular quarterly dividend to $0.165 per share from $0.155 per share on the Company's Class A and Class B common stock. The dividend is payable on June 15, 2005, to shareholders of record on June 1, 2005.
