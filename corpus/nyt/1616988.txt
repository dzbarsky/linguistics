New York Times Co. Appoints Ron McCoy as Chief Digital Architect for its Digital Operations.
New York Times Co. announced that Ron McCoy has been named chief digital architect for the Company's digital operations. The appointment is effective immediately and Mr. McCoy will report to Martin A. Nisenholtz, senior vice president of digital operations. In his new role, Mr. McCoy, 36, will work across the Company's digital operations to define and build new products and services, manage existing infrastructure and identify key partnerships that will leverage and grow the Company's Web properties.
