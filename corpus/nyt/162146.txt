The New York Times Names Tom Redburn Technology Editor.
Tom Redburn, an assistant business editor of The New York Times, has been appointed the newspaper's technology editor. The appointment was announced by Howell Raines, executive editor. In his new post, Mr. Redburn will oversee reporting and editing of technology news coverage for all sections of The Times. He succeeds Richard J. Meislin, who has been appointed editor of news surveys and election analysis. The appointments will take place after a brief transition.
