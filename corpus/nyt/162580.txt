Frank Rich Appointed Associate Editor of The New York Times.
Frank Rich, Op-Ed columnist and senior writer for The New York Times Magazine, has been appointed associate editor of The New York Times. In his new role, Mr. Rich will write a weekly cultural essay that will run as a column on the front page of the Sunday Arts & Leisure section beginning this spring. In keeping with The Times practice of having associate editors assist in planning the journalistic undertakings of the paper, Mr. Rich will also serve as an adviser to Steven Erlanger, cultural news editor.
