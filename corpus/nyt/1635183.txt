New York Times Co. Reports April 2005 Revenue Results.
The New York Times Co. announced that in April 2005 advertising revenues for the Company's business units increased 3.7% to $221,057,000 against $213,201,000 for the same month in 2004 and total Company revenues increased 2.7% to $324,014,000 against $315,405,000 the results for the same month in 2004. Circulation revenues increased 0.4% to $84,236,000 against $83,942,000 the results for the same month in 2004.
