New York Times Co. Appoints Vivian Schiller as Senior Vice President for Television and Video.
New York Times Co. announced that it has appointed Vivian Schiller, the general manager of the Discovery Times Channel, to the new position of senior vice president for television and video. Ms. Schiller, 43, will oversee continued efforts to extend the journalism of The Times beyond the printed page to media like television. She will also maintain responsibility for the Discovery Times Channel, a joint venture formed in 2002 by The New York Times Company and Discovery Communications.
