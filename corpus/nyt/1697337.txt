New York Times Co. Appoints James M. Kilts to its Board of Directors.
New York Times Co. announced that James M. Kilts chairman, president and CEO of The Gillette Company has been elected to its Board of Directors. Mr. Kilts has been chairman, president and CEO of Gillette since 2001. He was president and CEO of Nabisco Holdings Corporation from 1998 to 2000. Before joining Nabisco Mr. Kilts headed the $27 billion worldwide food group of Philip Morris where he was an executive vice president from 1994 to 1997.
