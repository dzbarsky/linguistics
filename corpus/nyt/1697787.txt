New York Times Co. Announces Revenue Results for the Month of May 2005.
"New York Times Co. announced revenue results for the month of May 2005. For the month, the company reported that total revenues increased 2.4% to $261,131,000 from $254,975,000 for the same month in 2004. For year-to-date 2005, the company reported that revenues increased 1.3% to $1,390,728,000 from $1,372,323,000 for the same period in 2004."
