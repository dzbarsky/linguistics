The New York Times Co. Promotes Stuart P. Stoller to Vice President, Process Engineering and Corporate Controller.
The New York Times Co. announced that Stuart P. Stoller, vice president and corporate controller, has been given responsibility for managing the Company's process engineering initiatives and has been promoted to vice president, process engineering and corporate controller, effective immediately. Mr. Stoller, 49, joined the Company as vice president and corporate controller in January 1996. In this role, he has been responsible for the accounting, financial reporting, budgeting, policies and procedures, risk management and tax departments of the Company.
