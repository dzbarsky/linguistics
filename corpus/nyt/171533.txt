NYTimes.com Launches New Cooking With the Times Online Videos With Jacques Pepin.
The New York Times Co. announced that it has launched a new Cooking with The Times online video feature as part of an instructional series that is produced in partnership with The French Culinary Institute. The lesson is hosted by Jacques Pepin, renowned master chef and dean of special programs at The FCI, who reviews simple and refined culinary techniques utilizing citrus.
