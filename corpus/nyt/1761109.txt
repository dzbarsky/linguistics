New York Times Co. Promotes Laurena L. Allert to Assistant Treasurer.
New York Times Co. announced that Laurena L. Allert, director-treasury, has been promoted to assistant treasurer, effective immediately. She will continue to report to R. Anthony Benten, vice president and treasurer. Ms. Allert, 35, has served as director-treasury at the Times Company since 2001. From 1999 to 2001, she was a consultant and principal consultant at PricewaterhouseCoopers.
