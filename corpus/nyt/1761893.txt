New York Times Company Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months of 2005.
"New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the Second Quarter and Six Month of 2005. For the second quarter, the company reported net income was $60.08 million or $0.42 per diluted share on revenues of $845.06 million against net income of $75.67 million or $0.50 per diluted share on revenues of $823.93 million for the same period a year ago. For the six months, the company reported net income was $171.86 million or $1.17 per diluted share on revenues of 1,650 million against net income of $134.11 million or $0.88 per diluted share on revenues of $1,625.87 million for the same period a year ago."
