New York Times Co. Names Richard J. Meislin as Associate Managing Editor for Internet Publishing.
New York Times Co. has appointed Richard J. Meislin to the new position of associate managing editor for Internet publishing. Mr. Meislin, 52, has been editor of news surveys and election analysis since 2003. Previously, he was the editor in charge of technology coverage for the Business Day section. In his next post, Mr. Meislin will help oversee the expansion of the journalism of The Times online and its integration with the newspaper. His appointment was announced by Jonathan Landman, who was recently named deputy managing editor in charge of digital journalism.
