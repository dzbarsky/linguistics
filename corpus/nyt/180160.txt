The New York Times Company Board of Directors Approves $300 Million Share Repurchase Authorization; Declares Quarterly Dividend, payable on March 19, 2002.
"The New York Times Company's Board of Directors authorized an additional expenditure of up to $300 million for repurchase of its Class A common stock. The Company's Board of Directors also declared a regular quarterly dividend of $0.125 per share on the Company's Class A and Class B common stock. The dividend is payable on March 19, 2002, to shareholders of record on March 1, 2002."
