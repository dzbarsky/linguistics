New York Times Co. Reports Revenue Results for the Month of July 2005.
New York Times Co. reported revenue results for the month of July 2005. For the period, the company reported ad revenue rose 5.6% over July 2004, to $178.3 million. Revenue from national ads rose 5.2% to $67.1 million, retail ad revenue rose by 2.6% to $35.8 million and classified ad revenue rose 1.2% to $54.8 million. At the New York Times Media Group, ad revenue rose 4.9%. Online ad revenue at New York Times Co. jumped 31.8%.
