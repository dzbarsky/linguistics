New England Media Group and Metro Boston Launch Boston Uncovered Magazine.
New England Media Group and Metro Boston announced the launch of Boston Uncovered, a magazine produced exclusively for the area's college and graduate students. Developed by Metro Boston, The Boston Globe and Boston.com, the insider's guide to Boston is being delivered directly to students on 50 local college campuses this fall. Along with the magazine, Boston Uncovered is updated regularly online at www.boston.com/uncovered and, beginning, appears as a weekly section in the Metro Boston newspaper. Boston Uncovered features articles, some written by local students, providing information on everything the city has to offer. Included are tips on how to find great restaurants as well as the hottest music and nightlife venues, useful advice on where to shop for almost anything, and information regarding public transportation and essential services.
