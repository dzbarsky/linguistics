New York Times Co. Names Suzanne Daley as New National Editor.
New York Times Co. announced that Suzanne Daley, the education editor of The New York Times, has been appointed the paper's next national editor. Daley, 48, succeeds Jim Roberts, who was national editor for three years. He will continue to work at The Times in a senior role. Ms. Daley joined The Times in 1978 and became a reporter for the metropolitan desk in 1982. She became the deputy metropolitan editor in 1994. Ms. Daley became the South Africa bureau chief in 1995 and was named Paris bureau chief in 1999. She became education editor in 2002.
