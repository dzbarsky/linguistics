The New York Times to Launch OnMovies In-Cinema Magazine.
The New York Times announced that it is launching a first-ever, national in-cinema entertainment magazine, called OnMovies, on Dec. 16. This first-of-its-kind publication will be published 18 times per year and will feature editorial content produced from the Culture desk of The Times and NYTimes.com. Moviegoers will be handed a copy of the pocket-sized OnMovies magazine upon buying their tickets. Initially, OnMovies will be distributed to 1.25 million moviegoers at select Loews Cineplex Theatres in the following major metropolitan areas: New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Boston, Washington, D.C., Dallas and Seattle.
