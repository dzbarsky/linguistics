The New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Month of September and Year to Date of 2005.
"The New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the month of September and year to date of 2005. For the month, the company reported a revenue of $278,291,000 against a revenue of $270,849,000 for the same period a year ago. For the year to date, the company reported a revenue of $2,441,735,000 against a revenue of $2,399,705,000 for the same period a year ago."
