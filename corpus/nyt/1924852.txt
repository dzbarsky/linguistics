The New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September, 2005.
"The New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported a net income of $23,081,000 or $0.16 diluted earnings per share on total revenue of $791,083,000 against net income of $48,272,000 or $0.33 diluted earnings per share on total revenue of $773,830,000 for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported a net income of $194,945,000 or $1.33 diluted earnings per share on total revenue of $2,441,735,000 against net income of $182,384,000 or $1.21 diluted earnings per share on total revenue of $2,399,705,000 for the same period a year ago."
