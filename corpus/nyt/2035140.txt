Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: International Herald Tribune; Buyer(s): New York Times Co.; Seller(s): Washington Post Co.; Transaction Size: $65 mm.
The Washington Post Company (NYSE: WPO) sold its 50% stake in International Herald Tribune to The New York Times Co. (NYSE: NYT), for $65 million. The Times Company, which had previously owned 50% of the International Herald Tribune, becomes the sole owner after this acquisition. The New York Times Co. will combine International Heralds operations with its own international news gathering. The New York Times plans to expand the Herald Tribune's staff in Asia, subsequent to the acquisition. Also, Times business and foreign desks will move to 24-hour operations so reporters can file more timely stories for the Herald Tribune.
