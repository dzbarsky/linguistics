Alta Vista Company announced the acquisition of Zip2 Corporation from a group of investors for $307 million..
AltaVista Company agreed to acquire Zip2 Corporation from a group of investors on February 16, 1999. Deutsche Bank served as the financial adviser to AltaVista Company in the transaction.The transaction is expected to close following the satisfaction of customary closing conditions, including clearance under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976, as amended. The Board unanimously approved the deal. Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom, L.L.P. acted as legal advisor to AltaVista Company. Greenhill & Co Inc. (NYSE:GHL) acted as the financial advisor to AltaVista Company.
