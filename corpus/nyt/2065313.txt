Alta Vista Company completed the acquisition of Zip2 Corporation from a group of investors for $307 million..
"AltaVista Company agreed to acquire Zip2 Corporation from a group of investors on February 16, 1999. Deutsche Bank served as the financial adviser to AltaVista Company in the transaction.The transaction is expected to close following the satisfaction of customary closing conditions, including clearance under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976, as amended. The Board unanimously approved the deal. Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom, L.L.P. acted as legal advisor to AltaVista Company. Greenhill & Co Inc. (NYSE:GHL) acted as the financial advisor to AltaVista Company. AltaVista Company completed the acquisition of Zip2 Corporation for $307 million in cash on April 5, 1999. The aggregate purchase price is $341 million which also include the issuance of employee stock options to purchase AltaVista stock with a fair value of $28 million and other acquisition costs."
