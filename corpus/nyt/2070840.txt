Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: The New York Times Company, Marco Island Eagle; Buyer(s): EW Scripps Co.; Seller(s): New York Times Co..
The New York Times Company and The E.W. Scripps Company (NYSE: SSP) have entered into an agreement whereby New York Times will sell its newspaper Marco Island Eagle to E.W. Scripps. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The acquisition is subject to government regulatory approval under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Act.
