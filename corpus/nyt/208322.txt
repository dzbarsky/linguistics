The New York Times Company Reports October 2002 Ad Revenue.
The New York Times Company announced that advertising revenue for the Company's Newspaper Group increased 3.9% in October 2002 compared with the same month last year. The New York Times's advertising revenue increased 4.7% for October 2002 compared with October 2001. The New England Newspaper Group's advertising revenue increased 1.8% for October 2002 compared with October 2001.
