Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Worcester Telegram & Gazette; Buyer(s): New York Times Co.; Seller(s): The Chronicle Publishing Company; Transaction Size: $295 mm.
The New York Times Company has entered into an agreement with The Chronicle Publishing Company whereby it will acquire Worcester Telegram & Gazette from The Chronicle Publishing Company. The deal was valued at $ 295 million. The closing of the acquisition is subject to regulatory approval. Credit Suisse First Boston advised Chronicle Publishing Company in the deal.
