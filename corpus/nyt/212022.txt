New York Times Co. reported earnings for the first quarter of 2003.
New York Times Co. earnings rose 26 percent in the first quarter 2003. Earnings rose to $68.8 million or 45 cents a share, in the three months ended March 31 from $54.5 million, or 35 cents a share, a year ago. Revenue rose 6.3 percent to $783.7 million for first quarter 2003 from $737.1 million a year earlier.
