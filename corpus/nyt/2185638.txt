The New York Times Company (NYSE: NYT) entered into an agreement with Viacom Television Stations Groups (VTSG), a subsidiary of Viacom Inc. (NYSE: VIA) on September 14, 2005, to acquire KAUT-TV from VTSG. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The acquisition is subject to FCC approval and other customary closing conditions and is expected to be closed in the fourth quarter of 2005. The New York Times Company had revenues of $3.3 billion in 2004.
