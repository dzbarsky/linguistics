NYTimes.com Launches Paid Mobile News Service With Vindigo Studios And Verizon Wireless.
NYTimes.com of The New York Times Co. announced that it is partnering with Vindigo Studios to launch NYTimes.com Mobile News with Verizon Wireless, the nation's largest wireless service provider. The new subscription service, priced at $3.75 monthly access, is available to Verizon Wireless customers with elect Get It Now(SM)-capable phones. These customers can now download The New York Times's award-winning news coverage in an easy-to-read format, to read on their phones via a fast and flexible interface.
