New York Times Co. Reports Revenue Results for the Month of October 2005.
New York Times Co. reported revenue results for the month of October 2005. For the period, the company reported revenue of $348.2 million, up 2.5% from revenue of $339.7 million in the year-earlier period. Total advertising revenue rose 4.8% to $241.7 million from last year's $230.5 million, while circulation revenue declined 1.4% to $85.2 million from $86.3 million.
