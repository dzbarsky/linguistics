New York Times Co. Names Roland A. Caputo as Chief Financial Officer.
New York Times Co. named Roland A. Caputo, a circulation manager, as chief financial officer of its flagship newspaper to replace Liam J. Carlos, who becomes the daily's controller. Caputo recently led the company's national expansion effort, including overseeing home delivery in the U.S. and distribution outside the New York Metropolitan area. He joined the Times in 1986. As the newspaper's finance chief, Caputo will report to Scott Heekin-Canedy, president and general manager of the paper.
