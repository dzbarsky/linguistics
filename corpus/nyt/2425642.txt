New York Times Co. Appoints Ray Pearce as Vice President of Circulation Fulfillment Services.
New York Times Co. announced that Ray Pearce, managing director for circulation, has been named vice president of circulation fulfillment services effective immediately. Mr. Pearce replaces Roland Caputo who was recently named chief financial officer for the paper. Mr. Pearce will report to Marc Kramer, senior vice president of circulation for The New York Times. In his new role Mr. Pearce will continue to lead the team responsible for developing and managing The Times's nationwide home delivery distribution network, national wholesaler relationships and the distribution of products from its 20 national print sites.
