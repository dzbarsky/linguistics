New York Times Co. Reports Revenue for the Months of November and Year to Date Ended November 30, 2005.
"New York Times Co. reported revenue for the months of November and year to date ended November 30, 2005. For the month the company reported that, total revenues increased 3.1% to $295,607,000 compared with November 2004 of $286,838,000. For the year to date the company reported that, total revenues increased 2.0% to $3,085,535,000 compared with November 2004 of $3,026,287,000."
