New York Times Co. Announces Promotions of Senior Legal Executives.
New York Times Co. announced that Kenneth A. Richieri, vice president and deputy general counsel, has been promoted to vice president and general counsel, succeeding Solomon B. Watson IV, senior vice president and general counsel, who has been promoted to the position of senior vice president and chief legal officer. In addition, Rhonda L. Brauer, corporate secretary and senior counsel, has been promoted to the newly-created position of corporate governance officer. The appointments are effective January 1, 2006.
