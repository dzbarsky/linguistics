New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2005.
"New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year 2005. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $64,808,000 or $0.45 diluted share on total revenue of $931,040,000 against net income of $110,173,000 or $0.75 diluted share on total revenue of $903,937,000 for the same quarter a year ago. For full year, the company reported net income of $259,753,000 or $1.78 diluted share on total revenue of $3,372,775,000 against net income of $292,557,000 or $1.96 diluted share on total revenue of $3,303,642,000 for the same period a year ago."
