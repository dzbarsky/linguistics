New York Times Co. Names Yasmin Namini as Senior Vice President for Circulation, Effective March 20, 2006.
The New York Times announced that Yasmin Namini, chief customer officer of The New York Times Company's New England Media Group, has been named senior vice president of circulation for The New York Times newspaper effective March 20. The announcement was made by Scott Heekin-Canedy, president and general manager for The Times. Ms. Namini was named chief customer officer for the New England Media Group in 2005. Previously, she was senior vice president of consumer marketing at the New England Newspaper Group and senior vice president of circulation at The Boston Globe since 2000. Before that she served as vice president of circulation marketing at The New York Times since 1999.
