New York Times Co. Names Joseph A. Sexton as its New Metro Editor.
New York Times Co. named Deputy Metro Editor Joe Sexton as its new Metro Editor, effective immediately. Mr. Sexton replaces Susan Edgerley who was named assistant managing editor for career development. Mr. Sexton has served as deputy Metro editor since 2003. Previously, he served as deputy editor on the Metro desk for investigations and enterprise and oversaw Clifford Levy's Pulitzer-Prize winning work on homes for the mentally ill. He also served as enterprise editor on the Sports desk from 1998 to 1999 after having been a reporter on the Metro desk since 1994. He joined The Times in 1987 as a sports reporter and covered major league baseball and the National Hockey League.
