The New York Times Announces Editorial Appointments.
New York Times Co. announced several editorial appointments. Susan Chira, editor of the Week in Review, has been named editorial director of book development, succeeding Mitchel R. Levitas, who plans to retire. Kathleen O. McElroy, a deputy sports editor, was named associate managing editor for weekends, in charge of news coverage in the Sunday and Monday newspapers. Katherine J. Roberts, national editor, will succeed Ms. Chira as editor of the Week in Review. Jim Roberts, the deputy national editor, was named national editor, overseeing the Times's 25-member reporting staff throughout the United States outside of Washington.
