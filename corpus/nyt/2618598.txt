The New York Times Company Names Robert Kraft Vice President, Enterprise Services.
The New York Times Co. announced that Robert Kraft, group director-enterprise services for The New York Times and chief technology architect for The New York Times Company, has been appointed to the newly created position of vice president of enterprise services for the Company effective immediately. The announcement was made by David Thurm, chief information officer for the Company. In his new role, Mr. Kraft, 48, will oversee Company-wide networks, data centers, server administration and server consolidation. During his 25 years in the IT industry Mr. Kraft has worked in all three communities of the industry: vendor, consultant and end user. He was group director-enterprise services for The New York Times and chief technology architect for The New York Times Company since 2000.
