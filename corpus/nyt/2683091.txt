New York Times Co., Annual General Meeting, Apr 18, 2006.
"New York Times Co., Annual General Meeting, Apr 18, 2006, at 10:00 US Eastern Time. Location: the New Amsterdam Theatre, 214 West 42nd Street, New York., United States. Agenda: To elect a Board of 13 members; to consider and act upon a proposal to ratify the selection of Deloitte & Touche LLP, an independent egistered public accounting firm, as auditors for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2006; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting. ."
