The New York Times Company Reports May Ad Revenue and Announces Second-Quarter and Full-Year Guidance for 2003.
"The New York Times Company announced that revenue for the Company's Newspaper Group in May 2003 increased 3.7% to $147,911 from $142,682 for the same month last year. The company anticipate that second-quarter earnings per share will be in the range of $0.43 to $0.47 based on generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP). The company expect 2003 earnings per share to increase in the low-to mid-single digits."
