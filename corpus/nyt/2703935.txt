Federated Media Publishing announced that it has received $2.2 million in funding from a group of investors..
"Federated Media Publishing announced that it has raised $2.20 million in its series A financing on March 15, 2006. JP Morgan Partners and existing investor Omidyar Network and led the round, which included participation from other existing investors The New York Times Company, Mitchell Kapor, Andrew Anker, Mike Homer, and Tim O'Reilly. On March 15, 2006, Federated Media Publishing closed the transaction."
