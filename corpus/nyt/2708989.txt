Federated Media Publishing Inc. announced that it has received funding from New York Times Co., Omidyar Network LLC, Mitchell Kapor, Andrew Anker, Mike Homer, and Tim O'Reilly..
"Federated Media Publishing Inc. announced that it has received seed round funding on June 16, 2005. Omidyar Network LLC led the round. New Investor New York Times Co. also participated in the funding along with individual investors Mitchell Kapor, Andrew Anker, Mike Homer, and Tim O'Reilly. On June 16, 2005, Federated Media Publishing Inc. closed the transaction."
