New York Times Co. Reports Revenue Results for the Month and Year to Date March 2006.
"New York Times Co. reported revenue results for the month and year to date March 2006. For the month, the company reported total revenue $265,422,000 against $257,254,000 in the same period of last year. For year to date, the company reported total revenue $831,772,000 against $805,583,000 in the same period of last year."
