New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter of 2006.
New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the first quarter of 2006. The company reported that it earned $35 million, or 24 cents a share, compared with a profit of $111 million, or 76 cents a share, in the year-ago quarter. Revenue rose to $831.8 million from $805.6 million. The company reported that its first-quarter profit declined on a charge related to job cuts announced last September.
