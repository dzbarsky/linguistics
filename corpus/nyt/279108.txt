The New York Times Company Announces Updated Earnings Guidance for 2002 and Outlook for 2003.
The New York Times Company announced updated earnings guidance for 2002 and its outlook for 2003. In October the company reiterated its expectation that full-year earnings for 2002 would be within the range of $1.90 to $2.00, said Leonard P. Forman, senior vice president and chief financial officer.
