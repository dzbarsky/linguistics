New York Times Co. Names Alexis Buryk, Group Vice President of Advertising, to the Position of Senior Vice President of Advertising, Effective Immediately.
New York Times Co. named Alexis Buryk, group vice president of advertising, to the position of senior vice president of advertising, effective immediately. She will succeed Jyll Holzman who has resigned for personal family reasons. Ms. Buryk will oversee all advertising sales for the newspaper and she will report to Denise Warren, chief advertising officer of The New York Times Media Group. Ms. Buryk joined The Times advertising department in 1977 and worked in positions of increasing responsibility before being named a vice president in 1993.
