The New York Times Style Magazine Appoints Bruce Pask as Men's Fashion Director, Effective May 15, 2006.
T: The New York Times Style Magazine, a publication of media group The New York Times Company, announced the appointment of Bruce Pask to men's fashion director for T: Men's Fashion, effective 15 May 2006. In his new position Pask will report to Stefano Tonchi, editor of T: The New York Times Style Magazine. Most recently Pask worked for Cargo magazine as founding style director. Prior to that he was a freelance fashion editor, stylist, and costume designer on a variety of projects for Vanity Fair, The Sopranos, and the Broadway production of Design for Living.
