New York Times Co. Reports Revenue Results for the Month and Year to Date April 2006.
"New York Times Co. reported revenue results for the month and year to date April 2006. For the month, the company reported total revenue of $324,260,000 against $324,014,000 in the same period of last year. In April 2006 advertising revenues for the Company's business units decreased 1.6% and total Company revenues increased 0.1% compared with April 2005. Online advertising revenues rose over 25% across the News Media Group. Advertising revenues climbed nearly 85% at About.com."
