New York Times Co. Appoints Marc Frons as Chief Technology Officer, Digital Operations, Effective from June 26, 2006.
New York Times Co. announced that Marc Frons has been named chief technology officer for its digital operations effective June 26, 2006. He will report to Martin Nisenholtz, senior vice president, digital operations. Mr. Frons, 50, previously worked at Dow Jones & Company, where he was most recently vice president and chief technology officer for its Consumer Media Group, responsible for online product development and applications.
