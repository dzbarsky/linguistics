New York Times Co. Names Terry L. Hayes as Vice President, Labor Relations, Effective July 17, 2006.
New York Times Co. announced that Terry L. Hayes has been named as vice president, labor relations, effective July 17, 2006. Mr. Hayes will be responsible for The Times's daily labor relations with its 13 unions. He will report to Scott Heekin-Canedy, president and general manager, The New York Times. Mr. Hayes, 53, has been director, labor relations and human resources, at Atlantic Southeast Airlines Inc. since 2003.
