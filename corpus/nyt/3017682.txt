New York Times Co. Appoints Carla Anne Robbins as an Editorial Board Member and Editorial Page Editor.
Carla Anne Robbins will join New York Times Co. as an editorial board member and assistant editorial page editor on July 17. Ms. Robbins, 53, has been a reporter and news editor at The Wall Street Journal since 1993, where most recently she wrote about proliferation and nuclear weapons. Earlier, Ms. Robbins became the paper's lead foreign policy writer, and from 1999 to 2005 she also edited the Washington bureau's feature articles on foreign policy, defense and national security.
