New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and First Half Year of 2006.
"New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the second quarter and first half year of 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $61,313,000 or $0.42 per basic and diluted share on total revenues of $858,748,000, against the net income of $60,815,000 or $0.42 per basic and diluted share on total revenues of $845,069,000 for the same period a year ago. For the six months period, the company reported net income of $96,268,000 or $0.66 per basic and diluted share on total revenues of $1,690,520,000, against the net income of $171,864,000 or $1.17 per diluted share on total revenues of $1,650,652,000 for the same period a year ago."
