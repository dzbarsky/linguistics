New York Times Co. Reports Revenue Results for the Month June, 2006.
"New York Times Co. reported revenue results for the month of June, 2006. For the month, the company had $175.8 million in advertising sales during the month, up 0.7% from $174.5 million in the year-ago period. Total revenues increased to $263 million from $259.9 million. For the year-to-date, the company reported total advertising revenue of $1.13 billion, up 2.4%. Total revenue since the beginning of the year also increased 2.4% to $1.69 billion from $1.65 billion in the same period last year."
