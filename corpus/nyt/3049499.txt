New York Times Co. Appoints Steve Knight as News Director.
New York Times Co. announced that Steve Knight has been appointed the news director of New York Times Radio and morning newscaster on WQXR FM. He succeeds Sam Hall, who retired on June 30. Mr. Knight is a veteran radio news anchor and correspondent, most recently with Associated Press Radio in Washington, DC. He has also worked for CBS Radio News in New York and he served as news director of KIRO AM in Seattle. He joins The New York Times Radio staff on August 1 and will make his first on-air appearance shortly thereafter.
