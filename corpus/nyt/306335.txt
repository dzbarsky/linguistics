The New York Times Co. Raises Quarterly Dividend payable on June 19, 2003.
The New York Times Co. 's Board of Directors declared a 7.4% increase in the regular quarterly dividend to $.145 per share from $.135 per share on its Class A and Class B common stock. The dividend is payable on June 19, 2003, to shareholders of record on June 2, 2003. This dividend is the 138th consecutive quarterly dividend paid on the Company's common stock since the Company went public in 1969.
