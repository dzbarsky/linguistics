New York Times Co. Appoints Denise Vickers as Director of WHNT-TV, effective 28 August.
New York Times Co. announced that WHNT-TV has appointed Denise Vickers as news director, effective 28 August. Most recently Vickers was news director with WSFA-TV in Montgomery, a position she has held since January 2004. Prior to that she served as news director for WCBD-TV in Charleston, South Carolina, and KIVI-TV in Boise, Idaho, US. She has also served as executive producer for WSPA-TV in Spartanburg, South Carolina, US.
