New York Times Co. Appoints Desiree Dancy as Vice President, Diversity and Inclusion Effective October 2, 2006.
New York Times Co. announced that Desiree Dancy has been named vice president, diversity and inclusion, effective October 2, 2006. Ms. Dancy will be responsible for developing, recommending and leading the implementation of diversity and inclusion program initiatives. She will report to David K. Norton, senior vice president of human resources. Ms. Dancy has 12 years of experience in corporate diversity, all of which have been spent with major investment banks in New York. Most recently she served as vice president, global diversity and inclusion at Credit Suisse, which she joined in 2002.
