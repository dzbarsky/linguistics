Jean-Christophe Demarta to Join as International Advertising Director for the New York Times Media Group.
New York Times Co. announced that Jean-Christophe Demarta, currently the International Herald Tribune S.A.S.'s advertising director for Europe, Middle East and Africa, will assume the role of International Advertising Director for The New York Times Media Group, working with Claire LaRosa, Executive Director for International Advertising, based in New York. Mr. Demarta will continue to report to Mr. Dunbar-Johnson.
