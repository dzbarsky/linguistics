International Herald Tribune and the New York Times Integrate Advertising Sales Teams.
International Herald Tribune S.A.S. and New York Times Co. announced plans to integrate their sales operations globally, effective January 1, 2007. With the creation of a unified sales network, The New York Times Media Group will be able to present one face to market, with regional sales offices in the United Kingdom, France, Switzerland, Germany and Hong Kong responsible for selling advertising into The New York Times and the International Herald Tribune.
