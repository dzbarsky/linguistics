Headquarters of the New York Times to Open in September 2007.
New York Times Co. announced that The New York Time's new headquarters, The Times Centre, will open to the public in September 2007. The new headquarters, designed by Renzo Piano and developed in conjunction with Forest City Ratner Companies, is located in the heart of Times Square on West 41st Street between Seventh and Eighth Avenues in New York, US. The company said that The Times Centre will include two major spaces, The Stage and The Hall.
