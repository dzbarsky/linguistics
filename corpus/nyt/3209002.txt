The New York Times Weekly Supplement Launches in Argentina's Clarin and Serbia's Politika.
The New York Times Co. announced that it has launched its New York Times International Weekly in two additional publications; Clarn of Argentina and Politika of Serbia. The first issues were published in Clarn on Sept. 30 and in Politika on Oct. 2. Politika and Clarn are among leading international papers that print and deliver the weekly supplement. Using the layout and typography of The New York Times, the International Weekly includes news, features, commentary, color photos and graphics about world affairs, business, culture and social trends from the United States and around the globe.
