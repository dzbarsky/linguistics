New York Times Co. Announces Revenue Results for the Month of September 2006.
New York Times Co. announced revenue results for the month of September 2006. For the month, the company's revenue fell 2.4% on continued weakness in print advertising. The company reported $172.5 million in ad sales from continuing operations during the month, down 3.9% from $179.4 million in the year-ago period. Total revenue from continuing operations fell to $260.4 million from $266.9 million.
