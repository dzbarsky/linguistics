New York Times Co. Reports Earning Results for the Third Quarter of 2006.
New York Times Co. reported earning results for the third quarter of 2006. For the quarter, the company reported profit from continuing operations increased 39.2% and the company reported net income the quarter of $19.2 million, or 19 cents per share, compared to $22.1 million, or 20 cents per share for the same period of last year. The company reported operating profit down 48% to $20.5 million on total revenues that slipped 2.4% to $739.6 million.
