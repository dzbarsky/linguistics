New England Media Group Appoints Robert N. Recore as Director of Commercial Print Sales.
New England Media Group announced the appointment of Robert N. Recore as director of commercial print sales. This is a new position created to oversee and further expand the Groups commercial print business, which has grown considerably with the addition of a new offset printing plant in Millbury, Mass. Mr. Recore is currently the advertising director for the Worcester Telegram & Gazette, and has worked at that newspaper for 39 years. In the past 12 years as advertising director, he has played a key role in the development of several new T&G weeklies, the Worcester Quarterly magazine and the newspapers zone strategy.
