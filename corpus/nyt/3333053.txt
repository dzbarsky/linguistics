New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months of 2006.
New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the nine months of 2006. For the period, the company reported net income of $110,291 or $0.76 diluted per share on total revenue of $2,359,040 against the net income of $194,945 or $1.33 diluted per share on total revenue of $2,339,954 for the same period a year earlier.
