The New York Times Names Roger Cohen as Foreign Editor.
The New York Times Co. announced that Roger Cohen, acting foreign editor, has been named foreign editor. In making the announcement, Howell Raines, executive editor, said, This appointment makes formal the leadership Mr. Cohen, was named acting foreign editor for The New York Times on September 10, 2001, having been named deputy foreign editor the prior month. He previously served as bureau chief of the newspaper's Berlin bureau since September 1998.
