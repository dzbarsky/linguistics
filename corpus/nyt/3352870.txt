New York Times Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Month of November 2006.
New York Times Co. reported earnings results for the month of November 2006. For the month, the company reported revenue slipped 1.7% as its print advertising continues to suffer. The company reported that it had $189.9 million in ad sales from continuing operations during the month, down 3.8% from $197.4 million in the year-ago period. Total revenue from continuing operations fell to $278.5 million from $283.3 million.
