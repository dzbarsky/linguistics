New York Times Co. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2006 and 2007.
"New York Times Co. provides earnings guidance for the full year 2006 and 2007. For the year 2006, the company now expects to generate approximately $270 million in revenues from Internet-related businesses. Net income from joint ventures is expected to be $18 million to $20 million. For fiscal 2007, the company's outlook estimates a growth in revenues from Internet-related businesses of approximately 30% or more than $80 million. The company estimates an addition of $12 million in circulation revenue, and income from joint ventures to be between $10 million and $15 million."
