New York Times Co. Announces Executive Changes.
New York Times Co. that it has named James Follo as its new chief financial officer, replacing Leonard Forman, who is retiring at the end of December. Follo had most recently been chief financial and administrative officer at Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia Inc. He had also been CFO of General Media International Inc. The company had announced in July that Forman would retire at the end of the year. Follo formally assumes his duties Jan. 8, and Forman will stay on in a consulting capacity for three months beginning Jan. 1, 2007.
