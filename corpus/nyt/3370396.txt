New York Times Co. Announces Executive Changes.
New York Times Co. announced the promotions of several key leaders. James C. Lessersohn, vice president, finance and corporate development, has been named senior vice president, corporate development. Stuart P. Stoller, vice president, process engineering and corporate controller, has been named senior vice president, process engineering. George A. Barrios, CFO and senior vice president, New England Media Group, has been named vice president, treasurer. R. Anthony Benten, vice president, treasurer, has been named vice president, corporate controller. All of the appointments are effective January 8. Messrs. Follo and Lessersohn will report to Janet L. Robinson, the Company's president and CEO. Messrs. Stoller, Barrios and Benten will report to Mr. Follo.
