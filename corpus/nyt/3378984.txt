New York Times Co. Announces Executive Changes.
New York Times Co. announced that it named Carla Anne Robbins and David Shipley as deputy editorial pages editors. The appointments are effective Jan. 7, shortly after Andrew Rosenthal takes over as editorial page editor on Jan. 1. Rosenthal is replacing Gail Collins, who is taking a leave to write a book. The company said that Robbins will work with Rosenthal to lead the editorial board and also oversee the 'Letters to the Editor' section and the production staffs. Robbins joined the Times as an editorial board member and assistant editorial page editor in July of this year. Shipley will continue in his role as Op-Ed page editor and will also work on expanding the online presence of the newspaper's editorial page.
