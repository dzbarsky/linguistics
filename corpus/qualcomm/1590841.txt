Tropos Networks Integrates Atheros Communications Technology into its Metro- Scale Wi-Fi Mesh Products.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that Tropos Networks has selected the Atheros AR5004G 802.11g chipset to support its next generation line of MetroMesh routers. Tropos Networks has incorporated Atheros' technology for the first time into its 5210 Outdoor MetroMesh Router, 3210 Indoor MetroMesh Router and 4210 Mobile MetroMesh Router. Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, a Tropos Networks customer, is deploying the new line of routers to support its metro-scale Wi-Fi mesh network used by law enforcement officers and other public safety officials.
