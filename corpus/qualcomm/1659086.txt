Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Resignation of L.M. Macnab as Vice President.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that Colin L.M. Macnab resigned as Vice President of Marketing, effective as of February 25, 2005. Atheros is currently conducting a search for a Vice President of Marketing to replace Mr.Macnab, and Craig Barratt, Atheross Chief Executive Officer, is acting as interim Vice President of Marketing during this search.
