Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2005 ; Appoints Todd Antes as Vice President of Marketing.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2005. Revenue in the second quarter was $43.4 million, up 5% from the $41.2 million reported in the first quarter. In accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP), the company recorded a second quarter net loss of $216,000, or $0.00 per diluted share. This compares with GAAP net income of $1.7 million, or $0.03 per diluted share in the first quarter of fiscal 2005. In the second quarter of 2005, non-GAAP net income was $401,000, or $0.01 per diluted share, compared with non-GAAP net income of $2.1 million, or $0.04 per diluted share, in the first quarter of fiscal 2005. For the six months ended June 30, 2005, the company reported GAAP net income of $1.45 million or $0.03 per diluted share on revenues of $84.6 million compared to GAAP net income of $7.9 million or $0.15 per diluted share for the same period of last year. Non-GAAP net income was $2.5 million or $0.05 per diluted"
