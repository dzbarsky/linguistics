Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2005.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced financial results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30 2005. For the quarter, the company reported net revenues were $45.8 million compared to revenue of $38.3 million reported for the same period last year. GAAP net income was $2.2 million, or $0.04 per diluted share, compared to $767,000 or $0.01 per diluted share for the same quarter a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $2.6 million, or $0.05 per diluted share, compared to $1.7 million, or $0.03 per diluted share, for the same quarter a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported net revenues were $130.4 million compared to the $127.9 million reported for the same period last year. GAAP net income was $3.6 million, or $0.07 per diluted share, compared to $8.7 million, or $0.17 per diluted share, for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $5.1 million, or $0.10 per diluted share, compared to $11.7 million, or $0.23 per diluted share, for the same period a year ago."
