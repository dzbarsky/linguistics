Atheros Communications Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 24, 2006.
"Atheros Communications Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 24, 2006, at 10:00 Pacific Daylight. Location: 5480 Great America Parkway, Santa Clara, California 95054.,United States. Agenda: to elect two Class III directors to serve until the 2009 Annual Meeting or until their successors are duly elected and qualified; to ratify the appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as independent registered public accounting firm; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the Annual Meeting and any adjournments or postponements of the Annual Meeting. ."
