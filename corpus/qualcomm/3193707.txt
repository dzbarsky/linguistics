Atheros Communications Appoints Sam Endy as Vice President and General Manager of Mobile Wireless Business Unit.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced the appointment of Dr. Sam Endy to the newly created position of vice president and general manager of the company's Mobile Wireless Business Unit. Endy has more than 20 years of experience in wireless technology and will lead Atheros' business efforts in the mobile wireless market. He will report to President and Chief Executive Officer Craig Barratt. Endy was most recently president and chief executive officer of TeleCIS Wireless.
