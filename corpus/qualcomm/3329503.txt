Atheros Communications Inc. Appoints Willy Shih as New Board Member.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that Willy Shih has joined the Atheros board of directors. Dr. Shih has more than 25 years of marketing and engineering experience in the consumer electronics, computing and digital imaging, and display markets. In January 2007 Shih will join the Harvard Business School as a member of the faculty. Until recently, Dr. Shih served as executive vice president and co-head of the Technology Division for Thomson, a leading provider of digital video technologies.
