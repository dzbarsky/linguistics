Atheros Announces Bluetooth 2.1 + EDR Solution to Deliver Enhanced Wireless Connectivity in PCs.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced the Atheros AR3011, the first member of the company's ROCm(TM), Radio-on-Chip for Mobile, family of Bluetooth solutions. It is the industry's first Bluetooth 2.1 + Enhanced Data Rate (EDR) solution for PCs. Atheros' AR3011 is targeted to the PC OEM market in which the company has deep technical experience and strong customer relationships. The single-chip solution is the smallest and enables the lowest-cost Bluetooth design to date, intended to drive adoption of Bluetooth technology in laptops and desktops. Atheros' Bluetooth solution for PCs supports wireless LAN (WLAN) co-existence and simultaneous usage of multiple Bluetooth-based devices.
