Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2006.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2006. For the quarter the company reported a net loss of $953,000 or $0.02 per diluted share on net revenue of $87.81 million compared to a net income of $13.08 million or $0.24 per diluted share on net revenue of $53.08 million for the same period last year. For the period the company reported a Non-GAAP net income of $12.71 million or $0.22 per diluted share compared to a net income of $5.89 million or $0.11 per diluted share for the same period last year. The company also reported for full year a net income of $18.94 million or $0.34 per diluted share on the net revenue of $301.69 million compared to a net income of $16.69 million or $0.31 per diluted share on the net revenue of $183.49 million for the same period last year.
