Atheros Communications Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 22, 2007.
Atheros Communications Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 22, 2007, at 10:00 Pacific Daylight. Location: 5480 Great America Parkway, Santa Clara, CA 95054, United States. Agenda: To elect three Class I directors to serve until the 2010 Annual Meeting or until their successors are duly elected and qualified;to ratify the appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as independent registered public accounting firm; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the Annual Meeting and any adjournments or postponements of the Annual Meeting.
