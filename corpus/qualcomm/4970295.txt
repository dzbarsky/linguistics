Atheros ETHOS Technology Portfolio Expands With New Gigabit Ethernet Solutions.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced two new designs which extend the company's Gigabit Ethernet (GbE) portfolio -- its second-generation GbE Controller, the AR8121, and its first 6-port GbE Switch, the AR8316. These are the newest members of the Atheros ETHOS(TM) portfolio which provides the company's PC, retail networking and carrier equipment customers with Atheros-designed Ethernet solutions. ETHOS brand technologies leverage Atheros' mixed-signal and analog design expertise honed in its industry-leading WLAN portfolio to deliver superior performance and value in the Ethernet market.
