Atheros Demonstrates Expanded Technology Portfolio to Enhance Connectivity and Multimedia At Home and On-the-go.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced it is demonstrating its broadly expanded technology portfolio at the 2008 International Consumer Electronics Show (CES) at its booth at the LVCC, South Hall 4, #35349. The company's technology momentum is intersecting the rapidly expanding demand for converged networking, computing and entertainment in an array of fixed and mobile devices.
