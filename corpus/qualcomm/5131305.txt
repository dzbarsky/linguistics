Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2007.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2007. The company revenue in the fourth quarter was a record $114.3 million, up 8% from the $106.3 million reported in the third quarter of 2007 and up 30% from the $87.8 million reported in the fourth quarter of 2006. In accordance with U.S. GAAP, the company recorded fourth quarter net income of $13.4 million or $0.22 per diluted share. This compares with GAAP net income of $9.7 million or $0.16 per diluted share in the third quarter of 2007. Net loss in the fourth quarter of 2006 was $1.2 million or $0.02 per diluted share. Non-GAAP net income in the fourth quarter was a record $21.4 million or $0.36 per diluted share, compared with $12.7 million or $0.22 per diluted share in the fourth quarter of 2006.
