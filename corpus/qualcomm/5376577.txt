Atheros Communications Inc. Appoints Christine King as Board Member.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that Christine King has joined Atheros' board of directors. With her addition, the board of directors now consists of seven members. In addition to her new appointment with Atheros, King serves on the boards of directors of the Idaho Power Company and ON Semiconductor, a global leader in efficient power solutions, which recently merged with AMI Semiconductor. Prior to the merger, she served as chief executive officer at AMI Semiconductor.
