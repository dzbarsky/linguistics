Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Unaudited Financial Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2008.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced unaudited financial results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported revenue was a record $121.5 million, compared with $100.8 million reported in the second quarter of 2007. In accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles, the company recorded second quarter net income of $10.1 million or $0.16 per diluted share. This compares GAAP net income in the second quarter of 2007 was $9.3 million or $0.16 per diluted share. Non-GAAP net income in the second quarter was $19.3 million or $0.31 per diluted share, compared with $14.7 million or $0.25 per diluted share in the second quarter of 2007. For the six months, the company reported net income of $13,542,000 or $0.22 diluted earnings per share on net revenue of $236,036,000 compared to net income of $16,881,000 or $0.29 diluted earnings per share on net revenue of $196,326,000 a year ago."
