Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Retirement of Paul Franklin as Vice President of Operations, Effective December 31, 2008.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that on September 29, 2008, Paul Franklin, Vice President of Operations, notified that he will retire from his position, effective on December 31, 2008. Mr. Franklin will continue to serve as Vice President of Operations on a part-time basis until December 31, 2008.
