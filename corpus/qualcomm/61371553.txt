Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Un-audited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009.
"Atheros Communications Inc. reported un-audited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $250,000 or $0.00 per share on net revenue of $112.2 million against net income of $10.1 million or $0.16 per share on net revenue of $121.5 million reported a year ago. Loss from operations was $1.5 million against income from operations $9.7 million. In case of Non-GAAP, the company reported net income of $12.3 million or $0.20 per share against $19.3 million or $0.31 per share a year ago. Income from operations was $12.12 million against $20.15 million a year ago. For the six months, the company reported net loss of $7.8 or $0.13 per share on net revenue of $200.1 million against net income of $13.5 million or $0.22 per share on net revenue of $236.0 million reported a year ago. Loss from operations was $10.7 million against income from operations $16.3 million. In case of Non-GAAP, the company reported net income of $16."
