Atheros Communications Inc. Provides Earnings Outlook for the Third Quarter of 2009.
Atheros Communications Inc. provides earnings outlook for the third quarter of 2009. For the third quarter, the company anticipates EPS to be in the range of $0.29 to $0.31 based on fully diluted shares outstanding of approximately 63.75 million. Based on the overall strength of the business and current visibility, the company anticipates third quarter revenue will increase 15% to 20%.
