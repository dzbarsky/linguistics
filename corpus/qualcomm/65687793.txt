Atheros Communications Inc. Revised Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter Ending September 30, 2009.
Atheros Communications Inc. revised earnings guidance for the third quarter ending September 30, 2009. The company updated its revenue and earnings guidance for its third quarter of 2009, which was previously provided on July 21, 2009. The company now expects third quarter revenue to range between $145 million and $150 million, a sequential increase of 29 to 34% from the company's second quarter revenue of $112.2 million and 5 to 9% higher than the company's record revenue of $138.1 million in the third quarter ended Sept. 30, 2008. The company's previous guidance for the third quarter ending Sept. 30, 2009 was for revenue to be up 15 to 20% from second quarter revenue, resulting in a revenue range of $129.0 million to $134.6 million. Non-GAAP diluted earnings per share for the third quarter are estimated to be in the range of $0.35 to $0.39. Prior expectations for the third quarter were non-GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.29 to $0.31.
