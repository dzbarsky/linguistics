NEC VersaPro Tablet PC Features Atheros Super AG(TM) Technology.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced its AR5004X chipset featuring Super AG(TM) technology has been embedded into the new NEC VersaPro VY11F/GL-R Tablet PC. Consumers who want the ability to take their ultra lightweight tablet PC anywhere in the home or office and still stay connected to the Internet, network resources or email will be able to take advantage of a wireless option employing Atheros' high-performance 802.11a/b/g technology.
