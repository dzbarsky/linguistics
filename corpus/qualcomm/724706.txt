Atheros Communications Announces Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended Sept. 30, 2004.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended Sept. 30, 2004. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $767,000 or $0.01 diluted earnings per share on net revenue of $38,262,000 against net loss of $2,633,000 or $0.21 loss per diluted share on net revenue of $25,168,000 for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported net earnings of $8,668,000 or $0.17 diluted earnings per share on net revenue of $127,954,000 against net loss of $12,943,000 or $1.07 loss per diluted share on net revenue of $49,699,000 for the same period a year ago."
