Atheros Communications Inc.'s ROCm(r) Technology Rocks Wireless in Microsoft's New Zune HD Portable Media Player.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that Microsoft's new Zune HD media player leverages the performance and power efficiency of the Mobile WLAN chip, Atheros' Radio-on-Chip for Mobile (ROCm), to deliver a great wireless experience. Consumers can seamlessly stream music directly from Zune Marketplace using their Zune Pass(1) via Wi-Fi, surf the web and enjoy rapid synching between a PC and a Zune HD.
