Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Consolidated Unaudited Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2009 ; Announces Impairment of Long-Term Investments.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced consolidated unaudited earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net revenue of $156,641,000 compared to $138,064,000 for the same period a year ago. Income from operations was $16,368,000 compared to $13,834,000 for the same period a year ago. Net income was $38,576,000 or $0.60 diluted per share compared to $10,088,000 or $0.16 diluted per share for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $29,305,000 or $0.46 diluted per share compared to $23,363,000 or $0.37 diluted per share for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP income from operations was $30,330,000 against $24,372,000 for the same period in the last year. For the nine months, the company reported net revenue of $356,790,000 compared to $374,100,000 for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $45,527,000 or $0.72 diluted per share compared to $59,813,000 or $0.96 diluted per share for the same period a year ago."
