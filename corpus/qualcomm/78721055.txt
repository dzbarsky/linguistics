Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Capital Expenditures for the Third Quarter of 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2009.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced capital expenditures for the third quarter of 2009. For the quarter, the company capital expenditures were $1.2 million. For the year, based on the mid-point of the fourth quarter revenue and EPS guidance the company just provided, the company now anticipate full-year 2009 revenue growth of over 10% and EPS growth of over 15%."
