Atheros Communications Inc. Announces 1-Stream 11n & Bluetooth Combo Solution.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced the 1-stream 802.11n and Bluetooth combo solution on a Half-Mini Card for the PC market. The features Atheros Align(TM) 11n 1-stream technology and Atheros ROCm? Bluetooth solution for PCs. Combining the throughput of 1-stream 11n with the ease and simplicity of Bluetooth, Atheros is enabling performance and connectivity options in PCs ranging from entry-level notebooks to netbooks. This new combination transforms the mobile computing experience to enable a wide array of simultaneous wireless applications on the same device. The AR9002WB-1NGB includes the AR9285 single-chip 1-stream 11n PCIe solution, which is shipping in tens of millions of PCs worldwide.
