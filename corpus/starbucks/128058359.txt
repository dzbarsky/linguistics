Starbucks Mulls Acquisitions.
Starbucks Corp will look for acquisitions over the next 12-18 months to expand its consumer products business, Chief Executive Howard Schultz told the Wall Street Journal. The company intends to strike deals in the fast-growing single-cup brewing segment. He said: "We are building and investing in resources and people to build a significant consumer products business that over time will rival the size and scale of our retail company."
