Starbucks and CFA to Open First Starbucks Location in Guatemala.
Starbucks Corporation, together with joint-venture partner Corporacion de Franquicias Americanas, or CFA, is planning to open the first Starbucks location in Guatemala. The coffeehouse, located in the Condado Concepcion shopping center, will feature Starbucks premium single-origin coffee, Guatemala Finca El Faro.
