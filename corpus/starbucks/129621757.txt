Starbucks Corporation Launches Starbucks Via Ready Brew in all China Stores.
Starbucks Corporation has announced the launch of Starbucks VIA Ready Brew in all China stores. Starbucks VIA is made from roasted arabica coffee in an instant and micro-ground form. Starbucks VIA can be made by adding hot (or cold) water which brews the coffee in an instant. Starbucks VIA Ready Brew is pre-portioned into single-serve packets and packaged in packs of three or boxes of 12.
