Starbucks Corporation Report Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended April 02, 2011; Declares Dividend, Payable on May 27, 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for 2011.
Starbucks Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended April 02, 2011. For the quarter, the companys net revenues were $2,785.7 million against $2,534.7 million, an increase of 10% over the same period last year. The increase was primarily due to a 7% increase in global comparable stores sales, comprised of a 6% increase in the number of transactions and a 1% increase in average ticket. Operating income totaled $376.1 million against $339.8 million a year ago, representing operating margin expansion of 10 basis points to 13.5%. This improvement was primarily due to sales leverage, offset by higher commodity costs. The increase in commodity costs, primarily coffee, negatively impacted operating margin in the quarter by approximately 200 basis points and EPS by $0.04.
