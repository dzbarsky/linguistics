Starbucks Corporation Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Fourth Quarter of 2011.
"Starbucks Corporation provided earnings guidance for the third quarter and fourth quarter of 2011. The company expects earnings per share to be in the range of $0.32 to $0.33 for the third quarter of 2011. For the fourth quarter, earnings per share to be in the range of $0.35 to $0.36."
