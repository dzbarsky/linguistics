Starbucks Corporation Opens New Coffee Bar at Copenhagen Airport.
Starbucks Corporation has opened a new coffee bar in the transit area of Terminal 3 at Copenhagen Airport. The coffee bar is equipped with seating, enabling passengers to enjoy their coffee in peace and quiet before departure unless they prefer coffee to go on their way to the gate. Just like the Starbucks in Terminal 2 and in the arrival hall in Terminal 3, the new coffee bar is run by HMSHost.
