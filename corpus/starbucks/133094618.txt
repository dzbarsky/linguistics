Starbucks Opens First Outlet in Yunnan.
Starbucks Corporation opened an outlet in Kunming, capital city of Yunnan province, southwest China. The outlet, the first opened in Yunnan, marks Starbucks' new push in its biggest market outside the US. The company said that it would soon open another two outlets in Kunming as part of an expansion in the 39th mainland China city it has set foot in.
