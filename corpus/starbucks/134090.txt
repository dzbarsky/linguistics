Starbucks Coffee's 'Siren' Makes Broadway Debut with Opening of Times Square Flagship Store.
The Great White Way gets a new addition to its line-up with the official opening of a flagship Starbucks Coffee Company store on 42nd Street. In a premiere that befits Broadway, Starbucks will celebrate its new store with a first pour by Chita Rivera at 11 a.m. Ms. Rivera star of the Tony-Award-winning Nine the Musical along with representatives from Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS, will be present to receive a $5,000 contribution from Starbucks. The store, located at 251 W 42nd Street (at the corner of 8th Avenue), is a part of Tishman Realty's E Walk entertainment/retail/restaurant complex on 42nd Street.
