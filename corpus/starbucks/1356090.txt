Starbucks to Restate 2002-2004 Results.
Starbucks Corp. reported that it will restate its annual results for 2002, 2003 and 2004 and its 2003 and 2004 quarterly results. The company said that its lease accounting does not meet generally accepted accounting principles and noted that it has completed its review of its lease accounting. Earnings will be reduced by $12.6 million from 2002 through 2004.
