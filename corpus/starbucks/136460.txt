Starbucks Coffee Company Introducing This Vs That(TM), an Innovative New Game.
Starbucks Coffee Company is introducing This Vs That(TM), an innovative new game that polls players for their top answers in more than 250 intriguing and contrasting categories. It debuts exclusively in more than 3,400 Starbucks locations throughout North America on July 30, 2003. This Vs That is a witty game of contrasting thoughts and strategies with a one-of-a-kind design. Two brainstorming teams race to create a list of five things that fit one of two contrasting categories and then try to guess what the other team has written.
