Starbucks Corporation to Open First Location in Brussels.
Starbucks Coffee Company has announced that it will open its first location in Brussels. Autogrill Belgium will manage the day to day operations of this store that will be located in Brussels Central Station. The Starbucks store in Brussels Central Station will be located in the main ticketing hall, close to the train platforms. This new location will offer travelers, local customers and commuters the Starbucks experience, Fairtrade Certified coffee.
