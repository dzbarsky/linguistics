Starbucks Corp. Reports Revenue and Sales Results for the Four Weeks and Twenty One Weeks Ended February 27, 2005.
"Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated net revenues of $468 million for the four-week period ended February 27, 2005, an increase of 23% from consolidated net revenues of $380 million for the same four-week period in fiscal 2004. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 9% for the four weeks ended February 27, 2005, as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2004. For the 21 weeks ended February 27, 2005, consolidated net revenues were $2.5 billion, an increase of 24% from consolidated net revenues of $2.0 billion for the same 21-week period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 9% for the 21 weeks ended February 27, 2005, as compared to the same 21-week period in fiscal 2004."
