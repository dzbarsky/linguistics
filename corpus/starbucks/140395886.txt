Cooking.com, Inc. announced that it has received $13 million in funding from Azure Capital Partners, Clearstone Venture Partners, Idealab, Starbucks Corporation..
"Cooking.com, Inc. announced that it has raised $7 million in series E funding on May 26, 2008. The round saw participation from new leading investor Azure Capital Partners, Idealab, and Starbucks Corp. and existing investor Clearstone Venture Partners. The company has raised over $90 million between 1998 and 2000. On June 18, 2008, Cooking.com, Inc. closed the transaction. The round also saw participation from ORIX Venture Finance, LLC which provided debt to the company. The company raised a total of $13 million in the transaction. As part of the round, Michael Kwatinetz, Partner at Azure Capital Partners will join the company's board of directors."
