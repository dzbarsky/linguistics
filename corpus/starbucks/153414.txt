Starbucks Appoints Myron E. Ullman, III to Board of Directors.
Starbucks Coffee Company announced that Myron (Mike) E. Ullman, III has joined the Company's Board of Directors and will participate as an independent member of the board in the Company's Annual Meeting of Shareholders on March 25. He joins nine other board members: Barbara Bass, Howard Behar, Craig Foley, Gregory Maffei, Arlen Prentice, Howard Schultz, James Shennan, Jr. Orin Smith, and Craig Weatherup.
