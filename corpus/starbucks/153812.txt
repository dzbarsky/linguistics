Starbucks Corporation reported results for the period ended May 25, 2003.
Starbucks Corporation reported consolidated net revenues of $319 million for the four-week period ended May 25, 2003, an increase of 23 percent from consolidated net revenues of $260 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. For the 34 weeks ended May 25, 2003, consolidated net revenues were $2.6 billion, an increase of 23 percent from consolidated net revenues of $2.1 billion for the same period in fiscal 2002.
