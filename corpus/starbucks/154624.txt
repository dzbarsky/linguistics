Class-Action Suit Calls Tax Exemptions by Some Texas Restaurants Fraudulent.
A class-action lawsuit claims some restaurants are violating state law exempting bottled water and coffee from sales tax. Jeffrey Aronoff, listed as the plaintiff along with others, accused the Houston-area restaurants of unjust enrichment, fraud and unfair business practices. The lawsuit names Starbucks, Outback Steakhouse, Pappas Restaurants, Olive Garden, Saltgrass Steakhouse, Boston Market Corp. Cafe Express and James Coney Island.
