Starbucks reports net revenues for the period ended April 27, 2003.
Starbucks Corporation, reported consolidated net revenues of $307 million for the four-week period ended April 27, 2003, an increase of 22 percent from consolidated net revenues of $252 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. For the 30 weeks ended April 27, 2003, consolidated net revenues were $2.3 billion, an increase of 23 percent from consolidated net revenues of $1.8 billion for the same period in fiscal 2002.
