Starbucks Announces Additional Stock Repurchase Plan.
Starbucks Corporation announced that its Board of Directors has authorized the repurchase of up to 10 million shares of the Company's common stock. The repurchase plan announced is in addition to the current repurchase plan, which was announced in June 2002. As of December 29, 2002, the Company had purchased approximately 3.6 million shares under the June 2002 plan.
