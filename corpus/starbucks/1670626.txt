Starbucks Corp. Reports Revenue Results for the Four Weeks and Thirty Four Weeks Ended May 29, 2005.
"Starbucks Corp. reported revenue results for the four weeks and thirty four weeks ended May 29, 2005. The company reported consolidated net revenues of $496 million for the four-week period ended May 29, 2005, an increase of 23% from consolidated net revenues of $405 million for the same four-week period in fiscal 2004. On a comparable store sales basis, sales at Company-operated stores increased 7% against the same four-week period in fiscal 2004. For the thirty four weeks ended May 29, 2005, consolidated net revenues were $4.1 billion, an increase of 23% from consolidated net revenues of $3.3 billion for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 9% against the same period in fiscal 2004."
