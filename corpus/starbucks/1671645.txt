Suntory Ltd, Starbucks Corp. to Tie-up in Ready-to-drink Products Business.
Starbucks Corp. and Suntory Ltd. announced they have agreed to tie up in venture that will make Starbucks-brand ready-to-drink products. Under the terms of the agreement signed Suntory was awarded exclusive rights to make and sell the ready-to-drink products in Japan. Suntory plans to start selling the new drinks before the end of this year at convenience stores in the greater Tokyo area.
