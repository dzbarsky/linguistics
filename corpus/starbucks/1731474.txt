Starbucks Corporation Reports Revenue Results for the Five Week and Thirty Nine Weeks Ended July 3, 2005.
"Starbucks Corporation reported consolidated net revenues of $620 million for the five-week period ended July 3, 2005, an increase of 20% from consolidated net revenues of $518 million for the same five-week period in fiscal 2004. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 7% for the five weeks ended July 3, 2005, as compared to the same five-week period in fiscal 2004. For the 39 weeks ended July 3, 2005, consolidated net revenues were $4.7 billion, an increase of 23% from consolidated net revenues of $3.8 billion for the same 39-week period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 8% for the 39 weeks ended July 3, 2005, as compared to the same 39-week period in fiscal 2004."
