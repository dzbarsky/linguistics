Starbucks Corp. Introduces Beverage Made with Tazo(R) Green Teas.
Starbucks Corp. will begin offering an assortment of fresh and flavorful summer beverages made with Tazo(R) green teas. The line-up includes the new Tazo(R) Green Tea Frappuccino(R) blended creme, made with real matcha (ground green tea leaves), and two refreshing twists on classic iced tea -- Tazo(R) Shaken Green Iced Tea and Tazo(R) Shaken Green Tea Lemonade. All three beverages will be available at Starbucks stores in the United States and Canada from July 13, 2005 through September 30, 2005.
