Starbucks Corp. Reports Revenue Results for the Four Weeks and Forty Three Weeks Ended July 31, 2005.
"Starbucks Corp. reported revenue results for the four-week and forty three weeks ended July 31, 2005. For the period, the company reported net revenues of $495 million for the four-week period ended July 31, 2005, an increase of 21% from consolidated net revenues of $410 million for the same four-week period in fiscal 2004. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 7% for the four weeks ended July 31, 2005, as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2004. For the forty three weeks ended July 31, 2005, the company reported consolidated net revenues were $5.2 billion, an increase of 22% from consolidated net revenues of $4.3 billion for the same forty three-week period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 8% for the forty three weeks ended July 31, 2005, as compared to the same forty three-week period in fiscal 2004."
