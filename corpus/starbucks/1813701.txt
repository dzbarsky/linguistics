Starbucks Corp. Enters into a $500 Million Senior Revolving Credit Facility.
Starbucks Corp. announced that it has entered into a $500 million senior revolving credit facility to mature on August 12, 2010. The company announced that the funds borrowed under the revolver will complement cash generated from its operations and will be used for share buybacks, potential acquisitions and other general corporate purposes. Starbucks also said it has the option to request an increase of up to $500 million in commitments under the new facility.
