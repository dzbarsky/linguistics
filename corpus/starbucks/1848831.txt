Starbucks to Launch Ready-to-Drink Latte at Japanese Convenience Stores.
Starbucks announced that it plans to try out a Japanese way of drinking coffee, launching latte in chilled cup packages at convenience stores. Starbucks will introduce a latte called Discoveries Seattle with a mild milky taste and Discoveries Milano with espresso-like dark roast coffee from September 27, initially in the Tokyo region. The 200-milliliter (roughly seven-ounce) cups of latte will go for ?200 ( $1.8).
