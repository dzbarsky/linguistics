Starbucks Announces Repurchase of Up to 5 Million Shares.
Starbucks Corporation announced that its Board of Directors has authorized the repurchase of up to 5 million shares of the Company's common stock. This authorization is in addition to the 7.2 million shares that remain available for repurchase under existing authorizations. Since the Company's initial share repurchase program was authorized in September of 2001, Starbucks has repurchased a total of 35.7 million shares at a cost of $1.5 billion under authorized programs through Tuesday, September 20, 2005.
