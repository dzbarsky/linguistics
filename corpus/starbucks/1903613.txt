Starbucks Corp. Reports Sales Results for the Five Weeks Ended October 2, 2005 ; Provides Sales Guidance for the Month of October.
"Starbucks Corp. reported same-store sales rose 10% for the five weeks ended October 2. The company reported revenue for the five weeks ended Oct. 2 was $653 million, up 5% from $625 million for the six-week period in fiscal 2004. However, revenue increased 25% when calculated on a comparative five-week basis for September in both fiscal 2004 and 2005. The coffee retailer expects October same-store sales growth to be in the range of 3% to 7%."
