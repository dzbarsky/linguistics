Starbucks Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Year Ended October 2, 2005.
Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated earnings results for the 52 weeks ended October 2, 2005. For the period, consolidated net revenues were $6.4 billion, an increase of 20% from consolidated net revenues of $5.3 billion for he 53 weeks ended in fiscal 2004. Net revenues increased 23% when calculated on a comparative 52-week basis or both fiscal 2005 and 2004.
