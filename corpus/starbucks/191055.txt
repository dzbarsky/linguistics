Starbucks sticks to plans to open 15,000 stores abroad.
Starbucks Corporation said that while it is mindful of the financial cycles in the global marketplace, they have not had significant impact on Starbucks overall business. The coffee retailer said it's committed to plans to open at least 15,000 stores in international markets. It said it's nearing profitability in the near-term in the U.K. market.
