Starbucks Corp. and Jim Beam Brands Co. Expand Spirits Offering with New Starbucks Cream Liqueur.
Starbucks Corp. and Jim Beam Brands Co. announced the launch of Starbucks Cream Liqueur, an artful blend of delicious cream, premium spirits and a hint of Starbucks? coffee. Like its coffee predecessor, Starbucks Cream Liqueur is available at licensed establishments such as restaurants, bars and retail outlets where premium distilled spirits are sold. It is not sold at Starbucks stores. A 750ml bottle of Starbucks Cream Liqueur will retail at an average price of $22.99, which varies by market. The product also is available in 1L and 50ml sizes.
