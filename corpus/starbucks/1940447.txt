Dong Suh Food Co. Obtains Exclusive License to Sell Bottled Frappuccino Coffee from Starbucks.
Dong Suh Food Co. announced that it has obtained an exclusive license to sell bottled frappuccino coffee from Starbucks Coffee Co. Under the deal, Dong Suh Food will either import or produce the bottled drink and start sales at convenience stores or discount outlets from December. The drink will be available in three flavors-coffee, mocha or caramel-to suit the demands of picky coffee drinkers.
