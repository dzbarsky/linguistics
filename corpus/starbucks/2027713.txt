Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Pasqua, Inc.; Buyer(s): Starbucks Corp..
"Pasqua Coffee has signed an agreement with Starbucks Coffee Company (Nasdaq:SBUX), pursuant to which Starbucks Coffee Company will acquire Pasqua Coffee. Specific terms of the agreement were not disclosed. The cash purchase, which is subject to satisfaction of certain conditions, is expected to close in early February 1999. Barrington Associates advised Pasqua Coffee on the deal. Deutsche Bank Alex. Brown, Inc., advised Starbucks Corp., on the deal. Pasqua Coffee was acquired by Starbucks Coffee Company (Nasdaq:SBUX) on 03/01/1999."
