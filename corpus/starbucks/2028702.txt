Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Coffee Connection; Buyer(s): Starbucks Corp.; Transaction Size: $24 mm.
"Starbucks Coffee Company (NASDAQ: SBUX) announced that it had entered into a definitive agreement with Coffee Connection, Inc. pursuant to which Starbucks has agreed to acquire 100% of the stock of The Coffee Connection for $24 million. Under the terms of the deal, Starbucks issued 833,263 shares of its common stock in exchange for all outstanding shares of The Coffee Connection common stock. Wertheim Schroder & Co. Inc. and State Street Boston Capital Corporation acted as the financial advisors to Starbucks and The Coffee Connection respectively. "
