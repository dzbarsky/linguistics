Starbucks Appoints Senator Bill Bradley and Olden Lee to Board of Directors.
Starbucks Coffee Company announced that Sen.Bill Bradley and former PepsiCo executive Olden Lee have joined the Company's Board of Directors. With the addition of Sen. Bradley and Lee, three-quarters of all the Starbucks directors meet all of the independence requirements of NASDAQ and the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. Bradley, 59, is a managing director of Allen & Company LLC.
