Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Tazo Tea Company; Buyer(s): Starbucks Corp..
Starbucks Coffee Company (Nasdaq: SBUX) signed an agreement to acquire Tazo. Specific terms of the cash agreement were not disclosed. Tazo's current management will remain in place as will the Tazo brand and their high-quality, authentic products. The cash purchase, which is subject to satisfaction of certain conditions, is expected to close by the end of January 1999.
