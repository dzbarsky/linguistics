Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Ethos Water; Buyer(s): Starbucks Corp..
"Starbucks Corporation (Nasdaq:SBUX) acquired Ethos Water. Financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. As a result of the transaction, Ethos Water will be available in Starbucks Company-operated stores, however The water bottles will not carry the Starbucks logo. Starbucks Corporation (Nasdaq:SBUX) acquired Ethos Water on 04/11/2005."
