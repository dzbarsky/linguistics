Starbucks Reports March 2003 Revenues.
Starbucks Corporation reported consolidated net revenues of $380 million for the five-week period ended March 30, 2003, an increase of 20 percent from consolidated net revenues of $316 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. For the 26 weeks ended March 30, 2003, consolidated net revenues were $2.0 billion, an increase of 23 percent from consolidated net revenues of $1.6 billion for the same period in fiscal 2002.
