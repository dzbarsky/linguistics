Starbucks Reports Four-week Period Ended January 26, 2003 Revenues.
Starbucks Corporation reported consolidated net revenues of $286 million for the four-week period ended January 26, 2003, an increase of 25 percent from consolidated net revenues of $229 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 9 percent for the four weeks ended January 26, 2003, as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2002. For the 17 weeks ended January 26, 2003, consolidated net revenues were $1.3 billion, an increase of 25 percent from consolidated net revenues of $1.0 billion for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales increased 9 percent for the 17 weeks ended January 26, 2003, as compared to the same 17 weeks in fiscal 2002.
