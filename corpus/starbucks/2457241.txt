Starbucks Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Four Weeks and Eight Weeks Ended November 27, 2005.
"Starbucks Corp. reported earnings results for the four-week period and eight weeks ended November 27, 2005. For the period, the company reported net revenues of $593 million an increase of 22% from consolidated net revenues of $486 million for the same period in fiscal 2005. On a comparable store sales basis stores increased 7% as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2005. For the eight weeks ended November 27, 2005, consolidated net revenues were $1.1 billion, an increase of 21% t from consolidated net revenues of $941 million for the eight weeks ended in fiscal 2005. Comparable store sales increased 7% as compared to the same eight weeks in fiscal 2005."
