Starbucks Corp. Reports Sales Results for the Five Weeks and Thirteen Weeks Ended January 1, 2006.
"Starbucks Corp. reported sales results for the five weeks and thirteen weeks ended January 1, 2006. For the five weeks, the company reported consolidated net revenues of $791 million an increase of 22% from consolidated net revenues of $648 million for the same period in fiscal 2005. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 7% as compared to the same five-week period in fiscal 2005. For thirteen weeks, the company reported consolidated net revenues of $1.9 billion, an increase of 22% from consolidated net revenues of $1.6 billion for the same period in fiscal 2005. Comparable store sales increased 7% as compared to the same period in fiscal 2005."
