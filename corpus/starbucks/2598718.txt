Starbucks Corp. Launches New Ready-to-Drink Bottled Coffee; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of Fiscal 2006.
"Starbucks Corp. has introduced a new ready-to-drink bottled coffee that will be distributed by PepsiCo. Starbucks Iced Coffee is a milky, sweetened coffee drink that will be sold in 8-ounce cans in the U.S. beginning in March. The company will also start selling a reduced-calorie version of its DoubleShot espresso-in-a-can and a new strawberry-flavored Frappuccino drink at its 7,700 U.S. stores, and convenience and grocery shops. The company reaffirmed earnings guidance for the full year of fiscal 2006. For the period, the company reaffirmed earnings guidance of 68 cents to 70 cents a share."
