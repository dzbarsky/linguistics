Starbucks Corp. Enters into Settlement Agreement with National Labor Relations Board.
Starbucks Corp. announced that it has entered into an informal settlement agreement with Region 2 of the National Labor Relations Board, resolving all unfair labor practice charges filed by the Industrial Workers of the World Local 660. Under the settlement, Starbucks has agreed to provide offers of reemployment to two former partners; post a notice in the three stores affirming partner rights under the National Labor Relations Act; and several other terms. The settlement provides for payments to some of the affected partners totaling less than $2,000.00.
