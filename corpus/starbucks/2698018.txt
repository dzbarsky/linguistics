Starbucks Corp. Introduces Rwanda Blue Bourbon Coffee.
Starbucks Corp. introduced Rwanda Blue Bourbon coffee, its first ever from Rwanda and the 10th coffee in its Black Apron Exclusives(TM) line-up. Arabica Bourbon, one of the world's oldest and rarest coffee species, thrives in Rwanda's high elevations and is prized for its elegant flavor. Rwanda Blue Bourbon, Starbucks second Black Apron Exclusives(TM) offering of 2006, imparts a subtle acidity with herbal, spice and cocoa notes.
