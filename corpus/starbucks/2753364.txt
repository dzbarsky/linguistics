Starbucks Corp. Reports Group Revenue Results for the Five-Week and Twenty Six Weeks Ended April 2, 2006.
"Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated revenue results for the five-week and 26 weeks ended April 2, 2006. For the five-week period ended April 2, 2006, the company reported an increase of 24% from consolidated net revenues of $599 million for the same five-week period in fiscal 2005. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 10% for the five weeks ended April 2, 2006, as compared to the same five-week period in fiscal 2005.
