Starbucks Corp. Announces Relationship With William Morris Agency.
Starbucks Corp. announced a relationship with the William Morris Agency Inc. (WMA) in which WMA will identify music, film and book projects for Starbucks to consider for marketing and distribution in its stores. The relationship signals the significant interest in Starbucks as a partner to labels and studios and provides Starbucks the opportunity to add value to its entertainment strategy and gain access to significantly more content for consideration.
