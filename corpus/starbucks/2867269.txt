Starbucks Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended April 2, 2006 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Full Year of 2006 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2006.
"Starbucks Corp. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended April 2, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $127,316,000, or $0.16 per diluted share on net revenues of $1,885,822,000 against net earnings of $100,461,000, or $0.12 per diluted share on net revenues of $1,518,716,000 for the same period a year ago. The company revised earnings guidance for the third quarter and full year of 2006. For the year, the company is raising earnings per share target range to $0.71 - $0.72 per share. This target range is an increase from the previous target range of $0.68 - $0.70 per share. Both the new target and the previous target ranges include stock-based compensation expense estimated at approximately $0.09"
