Starbucks Corp. Reported Consolidated Results for the Four-Week period ended October 26, 2003.
Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated net revenues of $363 million for the four-week period ended October 26, 2003, an increase of 30 percent from consolidated net revenues of $280 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 9 percent for the four weeks ended October 26, 2003, as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2003.
