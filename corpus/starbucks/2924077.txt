Starbucks Corp. to Expand into Brazil.
Starbucks Corp. announced to expand into Brazil, in a joint venture with a retailer there. The partnership with Cafes Sereia do Brasil Participacoes, a Brazilian holding company, is part of the big Seattle coffee retailer's overall plans to eventually have 30,000 Starbucks stores worldwide. Starbucks already operates in 37 countries. The first Brazilian Starbucks is scheduled to open in Sao Paulo within a year.
