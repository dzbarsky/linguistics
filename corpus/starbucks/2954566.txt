Starbucks Corp. Signs Distribution Deal with Pepsico Inc.
Starbucks Corp. signed a distribution deal with PepsiCo Inc. for its Ethos brand water through the companies' joint venture, the North American Coffee Partnership. The deal will significantly increase the distribution of the water to retail stores in the U.S. and Canada. Ethos is now sold at Starbucks coffee shops and small local retailers. The partnership will be responsible for co-packing, distributing and marketing of Ethos products. The companies, through the partnership, already sell Starbucks' Frappuccino and other bottled and canned coffee drinks in retail outlets.
