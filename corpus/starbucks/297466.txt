Starbucks Appoints Paula Boggs as New General Counsel.
Starbucks Coffee Company announced that Paula E. Boggs has been named executive vice president, general counsel and secretary. Boggs will be responsible for overseeing all facets of the company's legal matters, including real estate, employment, litigation, corporate and securities, international, intellectual property, and commercial. She will report directly to Starbucks president and chief executive officer, Orin Smith. Boggs joins Starbucks from Dell, a Texas-based provider of computer systems and services for building information-technology and Internet infrastructures.
