Starbucks Corp. to Open in Onalaska.
Starbucks coffee, FedEx Kinko's and Supercuts announced that they'll open locations this fall at the former Holiday Stationstore site at 9432 Hwy. 16, near Best Buy in Onalaska, Wis. There, they'll join Burrachos restaurant, which already has announced it will open soon. Starbucks Coffee Co. will open its 1,800-square-foot store in early fall. The company prefer to operate with family-oriented hours. Hours are 11 a.m. to 7 p.m. Monday through Friday, and 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. Saturday.
