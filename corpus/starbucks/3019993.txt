Starbucks Announces Promotion of Janet Parks to Vice President in Charge of Merchandise.
Starbucks Corp. has promoted Janet Parks to be its new vice president in charge of merchandise in an effort to build its merchandise sales into a larger part of its overall strategy. Parks has hired a new team to help her and merchandise is now on a faster promotional calendar. Merchandise, like beverages, will now be expected to introduce new items ever six to eight weeks.
