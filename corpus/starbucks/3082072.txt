Starbucks Corp. Reports Comparable-Store Sales Results for the Month of July 2006 ; Provides Sales Guidance for the Fiscal 2006 and 2007.
"Starbucks Corp. reported comparable-store sales results for the month of July 2006. The company reported that its comparable-store sales rose 4% in July, down from 7% a year earlier. Comparable-store sales for July grew at the lowest rate the company had seen since December 2001. The company expects comparable-store sales to range from 3% to 7% for the remainder of fiscal 2006 and 2007."
