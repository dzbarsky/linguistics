Starbucks Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Month of August and Year to Date to August 2006.
"Starbucks Corp. reported earnings results for the month of August and year to date to August 2006. For the month of August 2006, the company reported that total sales grew 21% in August to $617 million from $507 million the previous year. Year to date, the company reported that overall revenue is up 22% to $7 billion, while same-store sales are up 7%. Increases in both coffee and food sales helped drive the growth."
