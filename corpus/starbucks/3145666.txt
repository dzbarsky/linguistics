Great China International Holdings Signs Lease with Starbucks Dalian.
Great China International Holdings Inc. announced it has signed a 10-year lease with Starbucks Dalian, a subsidiary of Starbucks Corporation to open a Starbucks cafe in the ground floor lobby of its landmark twin-tower President Building in Shenyang, China. The 2,150 square-foot cafe is the third Starbucks location in Shenyang and the first situated in a commercial office complex. Shenyang, with a population of approximately seven million, is located in Northeast China. The new Starbucks, which opened this week, is owned and operated directly by Starbucks Corp. in the U.S.
