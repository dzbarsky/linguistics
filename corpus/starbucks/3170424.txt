The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission Sues Starbucks Coffee Co. over Firing of Disabled Woman.
The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission has sued Starbucks Coffee Co., saying the coffee retailer didn't accommodate the needs of a disabled woman, fired her, and violated the Americans with Disabilities Act. The suit seeks monetary damages, training on anti-discrimination laws, posting of notices at the work site, and other injunctive relief.
