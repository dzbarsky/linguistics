Starbucks Coffee Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended September 11, 2006.
"Starbucks Coffee reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended September 11, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported sales of $9.2 million, up 13.1% on prior year. Same store growth was up 2.5%. For six months, the company reported growth was 13.3% to total sales of $16.3 million. Sales were up 2.5% on a same store basis."
