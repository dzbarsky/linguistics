Starbucks Corp. and Apple Computer Inc. Announces Availability of Starbucks Hear Music Catalog on iTunes Store.
Starbucks Corp. and Apple Computer Inc. announced the availability of the Starbucks Hear Music catalog on the iTunes Store (), giving iTunes users the ability to preview, buy and download a wide variety of Starbucks popular Hear Music titles. Hear Music produces a wide range of albums showcasing new and classic artists including the well-known Artist's Choice series, Opus Collections, Debut Series releases by emerging artists, as well as new works from major artists. Starbucks Hear Music offerings will be available in a special new Starbucks Entertainment area within the iTunes Store and will include a wide variety of popular content as well as playlists created specifically for iTunes.
