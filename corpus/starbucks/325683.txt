Starbucks Corp. Announces Revenue For The Four Week Period Ended August 25, 2002.
"Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated net revenues of $267 million for the four-week period ended August 25, 2002, an increase of 25 percent from consolidated net revenues of $214 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 7 percent for the four weeks ended August 25, 2002 as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2001. For the 47 weeks ended August 25, 2002, consolidated net revenues were $3.0 billion, an increase of 24 percent from consolidated net revenues of $2.4 billion for the same period in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales increased 6 percent for the 47 weeks ended August 25, 2002, as compared to the same 47-week period in fiscal 2001."
