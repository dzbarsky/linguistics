Starbucks to Open First 24-Hour St. Louis Caf?.
Starbucks Corp. announced that it will open St. Louis' first 24-hour location Dec. 1 in Valley Park, Mo. The new 1,750-square-foot coffeehouse, with a drive-through window, is located west of Interstate 270 at 922 S. Meramec Station Road. The company also announced that it plans to open a new Olivette, Mo., location on Dec. 1, 2006.
