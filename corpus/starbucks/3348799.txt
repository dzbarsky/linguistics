Starbucks Corp. Strengthens Operational Control in Greater China.
Starbucks Corp. is strengthening operational control in Greater China following the establishment of a new Shanghai regional headquarters, with the company not ruling out further buyouts of joint venture partners in the region. The increased local presence of the Greater China business, formerly run out of Hong Kong, comes amid the company's recent moves to take full control of outlets in the Pearl River Delta and Beijing-Tianjin regions.
