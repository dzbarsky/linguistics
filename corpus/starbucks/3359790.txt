Starbucks Corp., Annual General Meeting, Mar 21, 2007.
Starbucks Corp., Annual General Meeting, Mar 21, 2007, at 10:00 Pacific Daylight. Location: Marion Oliver McCaw Hall, Seattle Center, Mercer Street, between Third and Fourth Avenues, Seattle, Washington,United States. Agenda: to elect 11 directors to serve until the 2008 Annual Meeting of Shareholders; to approve the material terms of the Starbucks Executive Management Bonus Plan; to ratify the selection of Deloitte & Touche LLP as the Company's independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2007; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting.
