Starbucks to Close 13 Cafes in Jewel Markets.
Starbucks Corp. announced that it planned to close 13 of its coffee cafes that it operates in Jewel grocery stores in the Chicago area. The decision was reached mutually between Starbucks and Supervalu Inc., parent of Jewel. The arrangement had been a licensing deal between the companies and the workers who staffed the cafes were employed by Jewel.
