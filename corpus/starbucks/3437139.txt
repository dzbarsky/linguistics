Starbucks Plans to Enter into India.
Starbucks Corp. is planning to enter into India in partnership with Pantaloon Retail India Ltd. The coffee-shop chain intends to increase the number of stores from 12,440 to 40,000 across the world. Starbucks has entered into a joint venture with New Horizons and will open its first store in New Delhi by the end of 2007. The Government of India has asked for clarification from Starbucks to permit the company set up its operations in India.
