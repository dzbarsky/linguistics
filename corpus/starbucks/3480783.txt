Starbucks Corp. Announces Earnings Results for First Quarter Ended December 31, 2006.
Starbucks Corp. announced earnings results for first quarter ended December 31, 2006 of fiscal 2007. For the first quarter the company reported, net revenues of $2.4 billion, an increase of 22% over $1.9 billion in the year ago period. Net earnings were $205 million, an increase of 18% over $174 million in the year ago period. The company reported earnings per share of $0.26, compared to $0.22 per share in the year ago period, an increase of 18%.
