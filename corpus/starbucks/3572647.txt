Starbucks Corp. to Open Store in Miami Township.
Starbucks Corp. will open new store on March 2, 2007 in Miami Township. The 9500 Springboro Pike store will employ up to 30. The 1,850-square-foot store will have a drive-through window, caf? tables and chairs and an outdoor patio. In addition to beverages and food, the store also will carry specialty beans, coffee and espresso machines and other merchandise. The coffee vendor is located in the Exchange at Spring Valley -- a $60 million joint effort between Oberer Construction Managers Ltd. and Mills-Morgan Development Cos. The new store will mark the sixth Starbucks location in the Dayton area.
