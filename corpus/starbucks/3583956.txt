Starbucks Corp. to Open 50 Coffee Shops in Mexico 2007.
Starbucks Corp. plans to open a total 50 coffee shops in Mexico in 2007. At present Starbucks has 120 coffee shops in a total 10 Mexican cities. The majority of the Starbucks outlets in the Latin American nation are located in Mexico City, Monterrey, Puebla, Acapulco and Cuernavaca. After the opening of the new 50 shops Starbucks will be present in five more cities in Mexico.
