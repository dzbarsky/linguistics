Starbucks Corp. Plans to Open a Fourth Unit.
Starbucks Corp. plans to open a fourth unit in Sao Paulo in May 2007. Since December 2006, Starbucks has inaugurated two coffee shops in Sao Paulo. The third is scheduled to open in March 2007. Starbucks runs a total 12,000 units in 38 countries. The chain's Brazilian subsidiary, Starbucks do Brasil, is 49% controlled by Starbucks and 51% owned by the Brazilian holding company Cafes Sereia. To attract customers in Brazil, it had to make some changes. The company started offering salty muffins, cheese loafs and a special Brazilian coffee brand, produced by coffee cultivated in the Minas Gerais state.
