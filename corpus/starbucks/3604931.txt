Starbucks to Open Taylorsville Road Store.
Starbucks Corp. is expanding its presence in Louisville with a new coffeehouse near the intersection of Taylorsville Road and Hikes Lane. The 1,750-square-foot store, located at 3954 Taylorsville Road, will open on March 2, 2007. The store, which is Starbucks' 32nd Louisville-area location, will employ about 20 to 30 workers.
