Starbucks Opens New Outlet in Miami.
Starbucks Corp. announced the opening of the new Starbucks, located at the corner of Spring Valley Pike and Ohio 741. The 1,850-square-foot building has a drive-through and an outdoor porch. It joins another new business, a new Milano's, 9572 Springboro Pike - just next door to the Starbucks - that opened .Oberer developed the Starbucks, which brings the number of coffee outlets in the Miami Valley to about a dozen, including the ones most recently opened in Lebanon and Springboro. The new properties mark a new line of development on previously vacant land, sitting between the Dayton Mall area and the proposed Austin Road interchange, which is predicted to be the site of a development boom.
