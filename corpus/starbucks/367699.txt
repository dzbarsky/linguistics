Starbucks Reports March Revenues For 2002.
"Starbucks Corp., reported consolidated net revenues of $316 million for the five-week period ended March 31, 2002, an increase of 25 percent from consolidated net revenues of $252 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 7 percent for the five weeks ended March 31, 2002 as compared to the same five-week period in fiscal 2001. For the 26 weeks ended March 31, 2002, consolidated net revenues were $1.6 billion, an increase of 22 percent from consolidated net revenues of $1.3 billion for the same period in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales increased 4 percent for the 26 weeks ended March 31, 2002, as compared to the same 26-week period in fiscal 2001."
