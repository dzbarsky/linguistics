Starbucks Corp. Plans to Open New Coffeehouse in Lake St. Louis.
Starbucks Corp. plans to open a new coffeehouse March 22 in Lake St. Louis, Mo. The new coffeehouse, located at 6319 Ronald Reagan Drive, is a 1,750-square-foot location with a drive-through window and outdoor patio. The Lake St. Louis caf? will employ between 20 and 30 people from the local area.
