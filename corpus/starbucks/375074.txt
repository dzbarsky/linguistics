Starbucks Reports Record 2002 Revenues.
"Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated net revenues of $260 million for the four-week period ended May 26, 2002, compared to net revenues of $204 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. For the 34 weeks ended May 26, 2002, consolidated net revenues were $2.1 billion, compared to consolidated net revenues of $1.7 billion for the same period in fiscal 2001."
