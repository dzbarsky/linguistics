Starbucks Corp. Proposes to Open its First Indian Store in Either Mumbai or New Delhi.
Starbucks Corp. proposes to open its first Indian store in either Mumbai or New Delhi by Dec 2007. The company, which is now awaiting approval from the Foreign Investment Promotion Board for its India foray, is already looking at expanding its product portfolio in India, to increase its market share in the country. Starbucks, which acquired Ethos Water in 2005, is likely to bring the brand into India, after opening its coffee chain. The company has announced a $1-million grant towards a clean water project for an estimated 120,000 beneficiaries in 80 rural villages and 40 urban slums in Madhya Pradesh in India.
