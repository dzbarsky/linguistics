AmRest Signs Preliminary Cooperation Agreement with Starbucks.
AmRest's announced that its polish branch signed a preliminary agreement with Starbucks Corp. under which it could open branches of the U.S. coffee store chain in Poland, the Czech Republic and Hungary. The first shops could possibly be built in Poland within eight or 10 months after a final agreement is signed. The company hopes to earn at least $50 million (37 million) annually from its Starbucks brand coffee shops.
