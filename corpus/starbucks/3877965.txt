Starbucks Corp. Appoints Peter Bocian as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer Designate, Effective 14 May 2007.
Starbucks Corp. announced that Peter Bocian has been named executive vice president and chief financial officer designate, effective 14 May 2007. The company said that upon joining Starbucks Bocian will take part in an intensive immersion process to learn the various facets of the company. On 1 October 2007 Bocian is expected to succeed Michael Casey, who is currently executive vice president, chief financial officer and chief administrative officer of the company. In his new role Bocian will be responsible for all aspects of Starbucks Corporate Finance, Information Technology, Global Business Systems Solutions and Global Strategy. He will report to Jim Donald, Starbucks president and chief executive officer. Most recently Bocian served as senior vice president and chief financial officer of NCR Corporation, a position he has held since 2004.
