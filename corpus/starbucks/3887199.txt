Starbucks to Open Three Outlets in Bucharest.
Starbucks Corp. plans to enter the Romanian market by opening at least three outlets in Bucharest this year. The first outlet will be located in the Plaza Romania shopping centre on Timisoara Boulevard. Starbucks will open at least two other outlets in Bucharest this year, one in the Bucuresti Mall, and the other will be opened within the Baneasa Business Center.
