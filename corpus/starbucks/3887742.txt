Starbucks Opens Outlets in Central Europe.
AmRest Holdings N.V and Starbucks of the U.S. have struck a preliminary agreement on the launch of Starbucks' outlets in Central Europe. AmRest, to run the coffeehouses, hopes to finalise the deal by the end of June. Flagship outlet openings are scheduled for 10 months after the deal completion. The deal struck is in line with Starbucks' strategy to expand its network to 40,000 stores, half of which are to operate in non-U.S. locations.
