Starbucks to Open Central Avenue Location.
Starbucks Corp. continues to expand in the Albany, N.Y., area with the opening of a new location on Central Avenue. The new location at 1818 Central Ave. in Colonie brings the number of Starbucks coffee shops within a 20 mile radius of Albany to about 17. Another Starbucks is scheduled to open soon on New Scotland Avenue in Albany. Rival Dunkin' Donuts is also expanding in the area, and has more than 100 stores locally.
