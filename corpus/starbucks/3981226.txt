Starbucks Corp. and Marinopoulos Holding SARL Open First Store in Bucharest, Romania.
Starbucks Corp. and its European joint venture partner, Marinopoulos Holding SARL announced the opening of the first Starbucks store in Bucharest. The coffee shop is located in Plaza Romania one of Bucharest's premiere shopping malls, on Timisoara Boulevard. Starbucks has extended its successful relationship with its Greek joint venture partner, Marinopoulos Holding, to create Marinopoulos Coffee Company III SRL, which will be responsible for the day-to-day operations of the business in Romania.
