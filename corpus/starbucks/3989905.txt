Starbucks to Open Coffee Shop on Eisenhower Drive in Hanover.
Starbucks coffee shop on Eisenhower Drive in Hanover will have a grand opening at 6 a.m. on April 19, 2007. Starbucks is opening in a 1,828-square-foot stand-alone building next to Texas Roadhouse. After the opening, Starbucks will have business hours from 6 a.m. to 10 p.m., Mondays through Fridays; 6 a.m. to 11 p.m., Saturdays; and 7 a.m. to 9 p.m., Sundays.
