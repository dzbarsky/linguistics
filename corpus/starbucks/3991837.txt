Starbucks Speeds Up Expansion in China.
Starbucks Corp. has decided to quicken up the expansion pace in China. The company expected to complete the strategic layout in the Chinese market by 2008. By 2008, Starbucks will have 50 to 150 outlets in Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Dalian, and Qingdao, according to a source close to the company. In addition, it will set management concepts of northern region and northeastern region for the five cities. The number of outlets in eastern and southern markets will be increased as well.
