Big Lots Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 27, 2010.
Big Lots Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 27, 2010, at 09:00 Eastern Daylight. Location: 300 Phillipi Road, Columbus, Ohio, United States. Agenda: To elect nine directors of Big Lots Inc.; to consider and vote upon a proposal to approve the amended and restated Big Lots 2005 Long-Term Incentive Plan; to consider and vote upon a proposal to approve the amended and restated Big Lots 2006 bonus plan; to consider and vote upon a proposal to amend its Amended Articles of Incorporation to institute a majority voting in uncontested director elections; to consider and vote upon a proposal to amend its Code of Regulations to establish procedures for the advance notice of shareholder nominations of directors at shareholder meetings; to consider and vote upon a proposal to ratify the appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as its independent registered public accounting firm for fiscal 2010; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the annual meeting.
