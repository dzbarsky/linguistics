Big Lots Reports Results fiscal year ended February 1, 2003.
"Big Lots Inc. reported net income for the fiscal year ended February 1, 2003, of $76.6 million, or $0.66 per share, versus a net loss from continuing operations last year of $28.7 million, or $0.25 per share. Net sales for the fiscal year ended February 1, 2003 were $3,868.5 million, a 12.7% increase compared to sales of $3,433.3 million for the same period of 2001. Net income for the quarter ended February 1, 2003 was $66.2 million, or $0.57 per share, compared to a fourth quarter loss from continuing operations of $1.9 million last year, or $0.02 per share. Net sales for the quarter ended February 1, 2003 increased 6.9% to $1,217.0 million compared to sales of $1,138.2 million for 2001."
