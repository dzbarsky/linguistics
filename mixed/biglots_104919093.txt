Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the First Quarter Ended May 1, 2010.
Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the first quarter ended May 1, 2010. The company has reported that its comparable store sales for the first quarter of fiscal 2010 ended May 1, 2010 increased 6% when compared to the first quarter of fiscal 2009. Retail sales for the first quarter of fiscal 2010 increased 8.1% to $1.22 billion, compared to $1.13 billion for the first quarter of fiscal 2009.
