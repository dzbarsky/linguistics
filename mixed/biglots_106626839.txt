Big Lots Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended May 1, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of 2010; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Ending January 29, 2011.
Big Lots Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended May 1, 2010. For the first quarter ended 2010. For the period, the company's income from continuing operations was $55,998,000, or $0.68 per diluted share, compared to income from continuing operations of $36,310,000, or $0.44 per diluted share, for the same period of fiscal 2009. Net sales increased 8.2% to $1 235.2 million, compared to $1,141.7 million for the same period in fiscal 2009. Operating profit was $90.1 million, an increase of 49% compared to operating profit of $60.3 million for the same period in 2009. Operating profit dollar growth was a result of comparable store sales increase of 6%, improvement in the gross margin rate and significant expense leverage as a percent of sales. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $89.912 million, an increase of 49% compared to $59.968 million for the same period in 2009.
