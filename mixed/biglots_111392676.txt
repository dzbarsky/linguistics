Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended July 31, 2010.
"Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the second quarter and six months ended July 31, 2010. The company reported second quarter retail sales for the fiscal quarter ended July 31, 2010 increased 5.7% to $1,132.5 million, compared to $1 071.2 million for the second quarter of fiscal 2009. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year increased 3.8% for the second quarter of fiscal 2010. This result is slightly lower than original estimates issued on May 27, 2010 which called for a comparable store sales increase in the range of 4% to 5%. For the twenty-six week year to date period ended July 31, 2010, retail sales increased 7.0% to $2,353.7 million, compared to $2,200.6 million for the same period in fiscal 2009. Comparable store sales increased 4.9% for the year to date period."
