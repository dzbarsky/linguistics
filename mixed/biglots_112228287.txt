Big Lots Inc. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Fourth Quarter of Fiscal 2010.
"Big Lots Inc. provided earnings guidance for the third quarter and fourth quarter of fiscal 2010. The company is forecasting a comp sales increase in the range of 2% to 4% for the third quarter. This is based on recent sales trends, incremental deals and one additional ad circular this year in August, and to generating more back-to-school business. Expect third quarter gross margin rate will be slightly down to last year. Turning to the fourth quarter, it is forecasting a comp sales increase in the range of 2% to 4% against best comp of the year last year at 5.1%. EPS from continuing operations is estimated to be in the range of $1.41 to $1.45 per diluted share compared to $1.31 per diluted share last year."
