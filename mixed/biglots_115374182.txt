Big Lots Inc. Announces Sales Results for Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 31, 2010.
"Big Lots Inc. announced sales results for the third quarter and nine months ended October 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company announced retail sales of $1,039.7 million, an increase of 2.4% compared to $1,015.6 million for the third quarter of fiscal 2009. Comparable store sales for stores open at least two years at the beginning of the fiscal year increased 0.7% for the third quarter of fiscal 2010. For the thirty-nine week year-to-date period, the company announced retail sales totaled $3,393.5 million, an increase of 5.5% compared to $3,216.2 million for the same period in fiscal 2009. Comparable store sales increased 3.6% for the year-to-date period."
