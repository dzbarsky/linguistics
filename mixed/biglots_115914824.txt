Big Lots to Open 17 New Stores on November 19, 2010.
Big Lots Inc. will expand its presence in the United States with the grand opening of 17 new stores on November 19, 2010. With these new openings, Big Lots has passed the 1,400-store threshold while operating in all of the 48 contiguous states. The new locations are: Ardmore, OK: 1211 N. Commerce Street; Brockport, NY: 6515 Brockport Spencerport Rd.; Cedar Park, TX: 850 N. Bell Blvd.; Columbia, MD: 6171 Columbia Crossing Circle; Exton, PA: 201 West Lincoln Hwy.; Franklin, MA: 275 E Central St.; Frisco, TX: 3333 Preston Road; Jacksonville, FL: 9625 Crosshill Blvd.; Lake City, FL: 2311 West US Hwy 90; Madison Heights, MI: 32399 John R Rd.; Manchester, CT: 1470 Pleasant Valley Rd.; North Augusta, SC: 406 E. Martintown Rd.; North Haven, CT: 380 Universal Dr. North; Rancho Santa Margarita, CA: 30501 Avenida De Las Flores; Sahuarita, AZ: 18705 S. I-19 Frontage Road; Worcester, MA: 7 Neponset St.; and West Deptford, NJ: 1075 Mantua Pike.
