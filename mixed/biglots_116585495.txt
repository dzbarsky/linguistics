Big Lots Inc Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ending January 29, 2011; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Year Ending January 29, 2011.
Big Lots Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended October 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net sales of $1,055,830,000 against $1,035,269,000 in the same period of last year. Operating profit was $26,903,000 against $47,577,000 in the same period of last year. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $26,198,000 against $47,084,000 in the same period of last year. Income from continuing operations was $17,745,000 or $0.23 per diluted common share against income from continuing operations of $30,256,000 or $0.37 per diluted common share in the same period of last year. Net income was $17,694,000 or $0.23 per diluted common share against net income of $30,329,000 or $0.37 per diluted common share in the same period of last year.
