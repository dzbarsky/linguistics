Big Lots Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 26, 2011.
Big Lots Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 26, 2011., at 09:05 Eastern Daylight. Location: 300 Phillipi Road. Agenda: To consider the election of nine directors; to approve, on an advisory basis, the compensation of named executive officers; to consider vote, on an advisory basis, on the frequency of the advisory vote on compensation of named executive officers; to consider and vote upon a proposal to ratify the appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as independent registered public accounting firm for fiscal 2011; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the annual meeting.
