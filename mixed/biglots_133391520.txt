Big Lots Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $400 mm.
The Board of Directors of Big Lots Inc. (NYSE:BIG) authorized a share repurchase program on May 25, 2011. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $400 million worth of its shares. The repurchases will be subject to market conditions and other factors. Common shares acquired through the repurchase program will be available to meet obligations under equity compensation plans and for general corporate purposes. The program will continue until exhausted and will be funded with cash and cash equivalents, cash generated during fiscal 2011 or, if needed, by drawing on the company's $500 million unsecured credit facility.
