Big Lots Inc. Reports Retail Sales for the Four Weeks, Quarter and Fiscal Year Ended January 29, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks, quarter and year ended January 29, 2005. For the four weeks ended January 29, 2005, retail sales of $257.9 million, a 9.2% increase compared to retail sales of $236.1 million for the same period in fiscal 2003. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 4.0% in January with the number of customer transactions increasing 0.3% and the value of the average basket increasing 3.7% compared to last year.
