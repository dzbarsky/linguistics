Big Lots Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Twelve Months Ended January 29, 2005; Provides Guidance for First Quarter and Full Year 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. announced earnings results for the fourth quarter and twelve months ended January 29, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported a net income of $57.7 million or $0.51 per share on net sales of $1,380.9 million, against a net income of $85.3 million or $0.73 per share on net sales of $1,328.6 million for the same period a year ago. The Company has issued fiscal 2005 earnings guidance of $0.54 to $0.60 per diluted share, which represents growth of approximately 95% to 115% over 2004."
