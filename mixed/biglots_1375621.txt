Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the Four Weeks Ended February 26, 2005.
Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended February 26, 2005 of $332.4 million, an 8.5% increase against retail sales of $306.2 million for the same period a year ago. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 3.3% in February, with the value of the average basket increasing 6.7% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 3.4%.
