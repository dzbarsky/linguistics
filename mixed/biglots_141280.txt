Big Lots Reports Second Quarter Ended Aug 2, 2003 Results.
"Big Lots Inc. reported a second quarter net loss of $8.0 million, or $0.07 per share, compared to net income of $3.2 million in the second quarter of fiscal 2002, or $0.03 per share. For the thirteen-week quarter ended August 2, 2003, the Company reported net sales of $949.3 million, an 8.0% increase compared to net sales of $879.3 million for the second quarter of fiscal 2002. Year to date net income was $2.2 million, or $0.02 per share, compared to net income of $15.4 million, or $0.13 per share, during the same twenty-six week period of fiscal 2002. On a year to date basis, net sales increased 6.4% to $1,897.7 million compared to $1,783.4 million in fiscal 2002."
