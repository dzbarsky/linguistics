Big Lots Reports Record December Sales for 2002.
Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended December 28, 2002 of $524.2 million, a 15.3% increase compared to retail sales of $454.8 million for the same period of 2001. For the forty-seven week year to date period ended December 28, 2002, retail sales were $3,538.6 million, a 13.1% increase compared to $3,127.9 million for the same period in fiscal 2001.
