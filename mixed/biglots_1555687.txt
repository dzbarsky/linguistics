Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the Five Weeks and Nine-Week Quarter Period Ended April 2, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the nine-week quarter period ended April 2, 2005. For the period, the company reported retail sales increased 6.7% to $748.1 million against $701.1 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 1.5% for the quarter. The company reported retail sales for the five weeks ended April 2, 2005 of $415.7 million, a 5.3% increase compared to retail sales of $394.9 million for the same period in fiscal 2004."
