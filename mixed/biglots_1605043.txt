Big Lots Reports Sales Results for the Four Weeks and Quarter Ended April 30, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended April 30, 2005 of $337.7 million, a 10.4% increase compared to retail sales of $306.0 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 4.4% in April, with the value of the average basket increasing 7.4% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 3.0%. For the quarter ended April 30, 2005, retail sales increased 7.8% to $1,085.8 million, compared to $1,007.1 million for the same period in fiscal 2004."
