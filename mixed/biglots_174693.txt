Big Lots Reports Sales Results for the Five Week, Nine Week and Thirty Five Week Ended October 4, 2003.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the five weeks ended October 4, 2003 of $347.6 million, a 10.0% increase compared to retail sales of $316.1 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales for all stores that have been open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 5.2% in September, with the number of customer transactions increasing 3.1% and the value of the average basket increasing 2.1%. For the thirty-five week year to date period ended October 4, 2003, retail sales were $2,501.6 million, a 7.4% increase compared to $2,329.9 million for the same period in fiscal 2002.
