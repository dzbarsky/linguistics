Big Lots Inc. Names Senior Vice President of Merchandise Planning, Allocation and Presentation.
Big Lots Inc. announced the appointment of Lisa M. Bachmann, senior vice president of merchandise planning, allocation and presentation. Ms. Bachmann will report to Kent Larsson, executive vice president of merchandising and sales promotion. Ms. Bachmann will oversee all merchandise planning, allocation, and presentation groups and will be responsible for setting the strategic direction and managing the execution of Big Lots supply chain initiatives. Last year the company identified supply chain management as a key initiative toward driving the business forward and improving customer counts.
