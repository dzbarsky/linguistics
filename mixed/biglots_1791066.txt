Big Lots Reports Sales Results for the Four Weeks, Quarter and Year to Date Ended July 30, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the four weeks ended July 30, 2005 of $292.6 million, a 4.5% increase compared to retail sales of $280.0 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year decreased 0.6% in July, with the value of the average basket increasing 4.0% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 4.6%. For the twenty-six week year to date period ended July 30, 2005, retail sales increased 6.7% to $2,126.2 million, compared to $1,992.2 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 1.3% for the year to date period with the value of the average basket"
