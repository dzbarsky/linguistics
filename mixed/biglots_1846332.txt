Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the Four Weeks and Thirty Weeks Ended August 27, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the four weeks and thirty weeks ended August 27, 2005. The company reported retail sales for the four weeks ended August 27, 2005 of $305.5 million, a 5.7% increase compared to retail sales of $289.2 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 0.6% in August, with the value of the average basket increasing 5.0% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 4.4%. For the thirty week year to date period ended August 27, 2005, retail sales increased 6.6% to $2,431.8 million, compared to $2,281.4 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 1.2% for the year to date period with the value of the average basket increasing 4.2% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 3.0%."
