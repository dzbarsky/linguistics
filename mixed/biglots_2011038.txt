Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Mac Frugal's Bargains; Buyer(s): Big Lots Inc.; Transaction Size: $1175.9195 mm.
"Mac Frugal's Bargain*Close-outs Inc. (NYSE: MFI) has signed a definitive agreement to be acquired by Consolidated Stores Corporation (NYSE: CNS). for $1111.18 million in a stock deal. Under the terms of the deal, Consolidated Stores will issue between 0.88 and 1.00 shares of stock for each Mac Frugal's share. The final exchange ratio will be based upon the average closing price per share of Consolidated Stores stock during a twenty day period prior to the effective time of the merger. The transaction is expected to close in January 1998. Batchelder & Partners, Inc. and NationsBanc Montgomery Securities, Inc. acted as financial advisors to Mac Frugal's Bargains*Close-outs Inc. Mac Frugal's Bargain*Close-outs Inc. (NYSE: MFI) was acquired by Consolidated Stores Corporation (NYSE: CNS) on January 16, 1998."
