Big Lots Reports 2002 Third Quarter financial results.
Big Lots Inc. reported a third quarter net loss of $5.1 million, or $0.04 per share, compared to a net loss of $16.4 million, or $0.14 per share, in the third quarter of 2001. Year to date net income was $10.3 million, or $0.09 per share, an increase of $37.1 million, or $0.33 per share, over the prior year. For the quarter ended November 2, 2002, the Company reported sales of $868.2 million, a 12.3% increase compared to sales of $773.1 million in the same period of 2001.
