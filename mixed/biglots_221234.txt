Big Lots Reports 2003 January Retail Sales results.
"Big Lots Inc. reported retail sales for the five weeks ended February 1, 2003 of $274.5 million, a 5.4% increase compared to retail sales of $260.5 million for the same period last year. Retail sales increased 6.9% to $1,204.3 million for the quarter ended February 1, 2003, compared to $1,126.7 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. For the year ended February 1, 2003, retail sales were $3,813.2 million, a 12.5% increase compared to $3,388.4 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales increased 7.7% for the year to date period, with the number of customer transactions increasing 2.8% and the value of the average basket increasing 4.9%."
