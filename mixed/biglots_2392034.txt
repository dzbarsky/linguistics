Big Lots Inc. Reports Sales Results for the Four Weeks, Third Quarter and Year to Date Period Ended October 29, 2005.
"Big Lots Inc. reported sales results for the four weeks, third quarter and year to date period ended October 29, 2005. The retail sales for the four weeks ended October 29, 2005 of $333.1 million, a 4.8% increase compared to retail sales of $317.7 million for the same period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales for all stores open at least two years as of the beginning of the fiscal year increased 1.3% in October, with the value of the average basket increasing 5.3% and the number of customer transactions decreasing 4.0%.
