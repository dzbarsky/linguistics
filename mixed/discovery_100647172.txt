Discovery Communications, Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 05, 2010.
Discovery Communications, Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 05, 2010, at 10:00 US Eastern Time. Location: One Discovery Place, Silver Spring, MD 20910, United States. Agenda: To elect 6 directors, 3 of whom will be elected by the holders of shares of Series A common stock and Series B common stock voting together as a single class, and 3 of whom will be elected by the holders of shares of Series A convertible preferred stock voting separately as a class; to consider and vote upon a proposal to ratify the selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2010; and to act on any other business that may properly come before the meeting or any adjournments thereof.
