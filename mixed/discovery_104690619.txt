Discovery Communications, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2010; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Ending December 31, 2010.
Discovery Communications, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net income attributable to companys stockholders of $169 million increased $50 million or $0.39 per diluted share compared to net income attributable to companys stockholders $119 million or $0.28 per diluted share for the first quarter a year ago. The increased results primarily reflect strong operating performance and a lower provision for income taxes due to a reversal of a foreign tax liability. The companys revenues of $879 million increased $64 million, or 8% compared to $815 million for the first quarter a year ago. Operating income was $283 million and income before income taxes of $220 million compared to operating income of $248 million and income before income taxes of $193 million for the first quarter a year ago.
