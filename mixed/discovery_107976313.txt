Discovery Communications, Inc. Promotes Bill Goodwyn to President of Global Distribution and CEO of Discovery Education.
Discovery Communications Inc. promoted Bill Goodwyn to president of global distribution and CEO of Discovery Education. In the newly created role as president of global distribution, Goodwyn will be responsible for driving ongoing value for the company's content, leading efforts to secure additional carriage for Discovery's worldwide networks and maintaining its leadership position on next-generation platforms including HD, 3-D and digital opportunities. Goodwyn previously was president of domestic distribution and enterprises.
