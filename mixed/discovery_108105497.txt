"Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers, SMPTE International Conference on Stereoscopic 3D for Media and Entertainment, Jul-13-2010 through Jul-14-2010. Venue: New York Marriott Marquis, 1535 Broadway, New York, NY 10036, United States. The SMPTE International Conference on Stereoscopic 3D for Media and Entertainment is the only 3D conference to focus on stereoscopic 3D technology for entertainment and only as SMPTE can do it! The program looks beyond the hype deep into the state of the art, science, and technology of stereoscopy. This exclusive two-day program cuts across the gamut of stereoscopic 3D technology for entertainment, stereoscopic image acquisition, 2D to 3D conversion, computer generated 3D space, plus display and transmission technologies."
