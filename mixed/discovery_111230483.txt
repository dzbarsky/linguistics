Discovery Communications, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Ending December 31, 2010.
Discovery Communications, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $963 million, operating income of $372 million, income before income taxes of $151 million and negative free cash flow of $44 million compared to total revenue of $865 million, operating income of $497 million, income before income taxes of $445 million and free cash flow of $173 million over the same period last year. The company reported net income available to companys stockholders of $106 million or $0.25 per diluted share decreased $71 million compared to $177 million or $0.42 per diluted share for the second quarter a year ago which included a gain on the sale of 50% of the Discovery Kids channel. Adjusted operating income before depreciation and amortization grew 18% to $455 million, driven by a 45% increase at International Networks and an 11% increase at U.S. Networks.
