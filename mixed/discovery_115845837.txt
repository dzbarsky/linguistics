"Discovery Communications, Inc. (NasdaqGS: DISC.A) acquired remaining 50% stake in Animal Planet and 50% stake in Liv from BBC Worldwide Limited for approximately $150 million on November 12, 2010. Richard D. Bohm, Peter A. Furci, Michael D. Devins, Andrew M. Herman, Sue Meng, Michael Schaper, Antony Stokes and Noam B. Waldoks of Debevoise & Plimpton, LLP acted as legal advisor to Discovery Communications. Discovery Communications, Inc. (NasdaqGS: DISC.A) completed the acquisition of remaining 50% stake in Animal Planet and 50% stake in Liv from BBC Worldwide Limited on November 12, 2010."
