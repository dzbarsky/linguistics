Discovery Communications, Inc. Announces Repurchase of 13,732,491 Shares of Series C Preferred Stock on December 13, 2010.
Discovery Communications, Inc. announced that it has agreed to repurchase 13,732,491 shares of Series C preferred stock for approximately $500 million, or $36.41 per share, from Advance/Newhouse Programming Partnership. This transaction is outside the company's existing $1 billion stock repurchase program and represents less than 10% of the Advance/Newhouse Programming Partnership holdings in Discovery. Following the transaction, which is expected to close on December 13, 2010, Advance/Newhouse Programming Partnership will remain the largest shareholder in the company with approximately 31% of the company's outstanding stock.
