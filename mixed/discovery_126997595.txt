Discovery Communications, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Ending December 31, 2011.
Discovery Communications, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported revenues of $1,015 million increased $65 million or 7% over $950 million for the fourth quarter a year ago, led by 9% growth at U.S. Networks and 4% growth at International Networks. Adjusted OIBDA grew 16% to $461 million compared to $396 million reported a year ago, driven by a 16% increase at U.S. Networks and a 7% increase at International Networks. Net income from continuing operations available to the company stockholders of $205 million or $0.48 per diluted share, increased $50 million compared to $155 million or $0.36 per diluted share for the fourth quarter a year ago. The current quarter results primarily reflect the strong operating performance partially offset by higher other expense of $32 million, mainly from losses related to its joint venture investments and increased provision for income taxes.
