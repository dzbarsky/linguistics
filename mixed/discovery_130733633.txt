Discovery Communications and Crave Games Announces Availability of Man vs. Wild The Game.
Discovery Communications, Inc. and Crave Games, the full-service publishing division of Fillpoint LLC announced that Man vs. Wild(TM) The Game shipped to outlets nationwide. Man vs. Wild(TM) The Game is available for the Xbox 360(R) video game and entertainment system from Microsoft, the Nintendo Wii(TM) and the PlayStation(R)3 computer entertainment system. The game will retail for $29.99 and supports both the Xbox LIVE(R) online entertainment network and PlayStation(R)Network.
