Discovery Communications, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Ending December 31, 2011.
Discovery Communications, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2011. For the quarter, the company reported total revenues of $951 million against $869 million for the same quarter last year. Operating income was $507 million against $282 million for the same quarter last year. Income before income taxes was $451 million against $220 million for the same quarter last year. Net income available to the company's stockholders was $305 million or $0.74 per diluted share against $169 million or $0.39 per diluted share for the same quarter last year. Cash provided by operating activities was $217 million against $126 million for the same quarter last year. Purchases of property and equipment was $11 million against $12 million for the same quarter last year. Free cash flow was $206 million against $114 million for the same quarter last year.
