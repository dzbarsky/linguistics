Discovery Communications, Inc. Announces New Cross-Platform Advertising Partnership with Mark.
Discovery Communications, Inc. announced a new cross-platform advertising partnership with Maker's Mark(R), which will launch on May 10, 2011. This is Maker's Mark(R) Bourbon's first-ever entry into television advertising, and its exclusive partnership with Discovery represents the brand's single largest financial commitment to an advertising campaign and totals 100% of its 2011 television and online budget.
