Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Announces Quarterly Cash Dividend Payable on June 30, 2010.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has declared an increased quarterly cash dividend of $0.18 per share. The $0.18 per share cash dividend represents a 20% increase over the most recent quarterly cash dividend of $0.15 per share. The dividend will be payable June 30, 2010, to stockholders of record as of June 16, 2010.
