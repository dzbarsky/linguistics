Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2010.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company has posted total revenue of $1,213.4 million against $1,346.52 million a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations before income taxes were $42.91 million against loss from continuing operations before income taxes of $1.11 million a year ago. Net earnings from continuing operations were $18.91 million against net loss from continuing operations of $12.73 million a year ago. Net earnings attributable to common shareholders were $16.52 million against net loss attributable to common shareholders of $12.39 million a year ago. Net earnings from continuing operations and attributable to common shareholders was $0.07 basic and diluted per share against net loss from continuing operations and attributable to common shareholders of $0.06 basic and diluted per share a year ago. Cash flow used in operations was $88.00 million against $128.30 million a year ago.
