Fidelity National Financial Appoints George P. Scanlon as Chief Operating Officer.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. announced the appointment of George P. Scanlon as Chief Operating Officer. Scanlon will assume responsibility for the finance, treasury, investor relations and corporate functions, as well as FNF's specialty insurance business. He joins FNF from Fidelity National Information Services, where he served as Corporate Executive Vice President, Finance since October 2009 and Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer from July 2008 to September 2009.
