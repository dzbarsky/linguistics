Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) and Newport Global Advisors, L.P. completed the acquisition of VICORP Restaurants, Inc..
"Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) and Newport Global Advisors, L.P. signed an agreement to acquire VICORP Restaurants, Inc. for $55 million in January 2009. The purchase price includes $11 million debt assumption also. The deal has been approved by bankruptcy court. Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) and Newport Global Advisors, L.P. completed the acquisition of VICORP Restaurants, Inc. on March 27, 2009."
