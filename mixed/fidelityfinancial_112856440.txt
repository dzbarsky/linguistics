Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) acquired Commerce Velocity Inc..
"Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) acquired Commerce Velocity Inc. on September 2, 2010. Commerce Velocity technology will be strategically aligned with ServiceLink, the national lender platform for Fidelity National Financial. Adam M. Fox of Dechert LLP acted as legal advisor to Commerce Velocity. Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) completed the acquisition of Commerce Velocity Inc. on September 2, 2010."
