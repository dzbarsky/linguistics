Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Reports Unaudited Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2010; Provides Dividend Guidance for 2011.
"Fidelity National Financial, Inc. reported unaudited earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $130.8 million or $0.58 per diluted share on total revenue of $1.61 billion against net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $69.3 million or $0.30 per diluted share on total revenue of $1.46 billion for the same period a year ago. Cash flow from operations was $113.3 million against $12.6 million for the same period a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations before taxes were $178.9 million against $105.9 million for the same period a year ago. Net earnings from continuing operations were $132.5 million against $69.5 million for the same period a year ago. Pre-tax earnings were $207.2 million as compared to $110.8 million for the same period last year.
