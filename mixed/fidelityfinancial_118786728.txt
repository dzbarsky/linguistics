Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Announces Quarterly Cash Dividend Payable on March 31, 2011.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.12 per share. The dividend will be payable March 31, 2011, to stockholders of record as of March 17, 2011. FNF expects to maintain this $0.12 quarterly dividend throughout 2011, based on a 2011 dividend payout target ratio of 30% of 2010 net earnings.
