Fidelity National Financial, Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 25, 2011.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 25, 2011., at 10:05 US Eastern Standard Time. Location: 601 Riverside Avenue. Agenda: To elect four Class III directors to serve until the 2014 annual meeting of stockholders or until the successors are duly elected and qualified or until their earlier death, resignation or removal; to to approve an amendment and restatement of the Fidelity National Financial, Inc plan; to approve a non-binding advisory resolution on the compensation paid to named executive officers; to select on a non-binding advisory basis the frequency with which will solicit future non-binding advisory votes on the compensation paid to named executive officers; to ratify the appointment of KPMG LLP as independent registered public accounting firm for the 2011 fiscal year; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment thereof.
