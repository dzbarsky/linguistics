Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2011.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2011. For the first quarter ended March 31, 2011, the company reported total revenues of $1,208.2 million, earnings before income taxes of $82.3 million, net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $42.5 million or $0.19 diluted per share against total revenues of $1,213.4 million, earnings before income taxes of $42.9 million, net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $16.5 million or $0.07 diluted per share for the same period a year ago. The company reported book value per share of $15.46 as at March 31, 2011. The company also reported cash flow used in operations of $21.0 million against cash flow used in operations of $88.1 million for the same period a year ago.
