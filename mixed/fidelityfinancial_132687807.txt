Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Appoints Dan Wallace as Vice President, Florida State Manager.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. announced that Dan Wallace has been appointed as Vice President, Florida State Manager, Major Accounts Division. Dan will be responsible for the development and maintenance of business between builders, developers, brokers, lenders, asset managers, non-agent Attorneys, CPAs and Financial Planners with Fidelity National Title across the entire state of Florida. With over 35 years experience in the title insurance industry, Dan has spent the last 15 years with Fidelity National Title as a Central Florida and East Florida Division Manager encompassing all elements of operations including escrow, title, sales and administration.
