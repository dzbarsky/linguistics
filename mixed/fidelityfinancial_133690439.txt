Fidelity National Financial Seeks Acquisitions.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc., which has filed a Shelf Registration, is looking for acquisitions. The company stated, Unless the applicable prospectus supplement states otherwise, the net proceeds from the sale of securities offered by us will be used for working capital, capital expenditures, acquisitions and other general corporate purposes. Until we use the net proceeds in this manner, we may temporarily use them to make short-term investments or reduce short-term borrowings.
