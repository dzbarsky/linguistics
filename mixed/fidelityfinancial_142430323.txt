Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE: FNF) acquired LoanCare Servicing Center, Inc. from LandAmerica Financial Group, Inc. (OTCPK: LFGR.Q) for $16.3 million on April 21, 2009. LoanCare generated revenue of $19 million and adjusted pre-tax earnings of $4.4 million in 2008. LoanCare also reported tangible equity of $12.4 million as of February 28, 2009. The transaction is subject to customary closing conditions and approval of the bankruptcy court. Robert Rachofsky, Andrew Fawbush and Dean Agnos of Dewey & LeBoeuf LLP acted as legal advisors for Fidelity National Financial, Inc.
