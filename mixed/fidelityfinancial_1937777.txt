Fidelity National Title Group Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2005 ; Declares Quarerly Cash Dividend.
"Fidelity National Title Group Inc. reported operating results for the third quarter and nine-month ended September 30, 2005. Total revenue for the third quarter of 2005 was $1.78 billion, compared with $1.56 billion for the third quarter of 2004. Net earnings were $169.7 million, or $0.98 per diluted share, as compared to Net earnings of $135.9 million, or $0.78 per diluted share reported for the same period a year ago. The company Announced Quarterly Cash Dividend of $0.25 per share."
