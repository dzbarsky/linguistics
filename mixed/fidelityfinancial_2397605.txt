Fidelity National Financial Inc. and Fidelity National Title Group Inc. Announce Board Changes.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced that Willie M. Davis, Philip G. Heasley, William A. Imparato, Donald M. Koll, General William Lyon and Frank P. Willey resigned from the Board of Directors of the company in connection with their joining the Board of Directors of Fidelity National Title Group Inc., a subsidiary of the Company. Messrs. Davis, Heasley, Imparato, Koll, Lyon and Willey joined the Board of Directors of the Title Group on October 18, 2005.
