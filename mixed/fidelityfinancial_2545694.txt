Fidelity National Title Group, Inc. Announces Expiration and Settlement of Exchange Offers.
"Fidelity National Title Group, Inc. announced the expiration and settlement of its previously announced exchange offers for outstanding notes of FNF. The exchange offers expired at 5:00 p.m., New York City time, on January 17, 2006. As of that time, $241,347,000 aggregate principal amount (approximately 96.54%) of the 7.30% FNF Notes due 2011 and the entire $250,000,000 aggregate principal amount of the 5.25% FNF Notes due 2013 had been validly tendered and not withdrawn in the exchange offers. FNT has accepted all FNF notes tendered (and not withdrawn) in the exchange offers, and in exchange for those notes has issued FNT notes with the same principal amounts, interest rates, redemption terms and payment and maturity dates as the FNF notes accepted in the exchange offers. "
