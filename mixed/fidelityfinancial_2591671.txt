Fidelity National Title Group Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2005.
"Fidelity National Title Group Inc. announced earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $126,350,000 or $0.73 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,586,543,000 against net earnings of $144,996,000 or $0.84 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,410,535,000 for the same period a year ago. For the full year, the company reported net earnings of $538,981,000 or $3.11 per diluted share on total revenue of $6,315,861,000 against net earnings of $558,164,000 or $3.22 per diluted share on total revenue of $5,889,413,000 for the same period a year ago."
