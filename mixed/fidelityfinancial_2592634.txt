Fidelity National Title Group Declares Increased Quarterly Dividend Payable on March 28, 2006.
Fidelity National Title Group Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has declared an increase in the quarterly cash dividend to $0.29 per share. The dividend will be payable on March 28, 2006 to stockholders of record as of March 15, 2006. The $0.29 dividend represents a 16% increase over the most recent quarterly cash dividend of $0.25.
