Fidelity National Title Group Inc. Reports Operating Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2006.
Fidelity National Title Group Inc. reported operating results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $79.1 million or $0.46 per diluted share on total revenue of $1.393 billion against net earnings of $82.3 million or $0.47 per diluted share on total revenue of $1.265 billion for the same period a year ago.
