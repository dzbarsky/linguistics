Robert M. Clements Resigns from the Board of Fidelity National Title Group Inc. to Join the Board of Fidelity National Information Services Inc.
Fidelity National Title Group Inc. announced that on July 1, 2006, Robert M. Clements resigned from the Board of Directors of the company to join the Board of Directors of Fidelity National Information Services Inc. Mr. Clements is the Chairman and CEO of EverBank Financial Corp., the holding company for EverBank. Mr. Clements joined EverBank in 1994 and has served as the President and CEO of EverBank Financial Corporation since its formation in 1997.
