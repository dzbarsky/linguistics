Fidelity National Title Group Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2006 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter of 2006.
"Fidelity National Title Group Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $116,513,000, or $0.67 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,565,599,000 against net earnings of $160,578,000, or $0.93 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,687,213,000 for the same period a year ago. The company provided earnings guidance for the third quarter of 2006. For the quarter, the company expects pre-tax margins and overall financial results to exceed those of the second quarter."
