Fidelity National Title Group Announces Increased Quarterly Cash Dividend, Payable on December 28, 2006.
Fidelity National Title Group Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.30 per share, which represents a $0.01, or 3.4%, increase over the previous quarterly cash dividend of $0.29 per share. The dividend will be payable December 28, 2006 to stockholders of record as of December 14, 2006.
