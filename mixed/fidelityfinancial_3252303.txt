Fidelity National Title Group, Inc. announces an Equity Buyback.
"The Board of Directors of Fidelity National Title Group, Inc. (NYSE:FNT) authorized a share repurchase program on October 25, 2006. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to 25,000,000 shares over a 3-year period. The purchases will be made in the open market, in block purchases or in privately negotiated transactions, depending on market conditions and other factors. "
