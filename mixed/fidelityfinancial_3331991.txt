Fidelity National Financial Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended Sept. 30, 2006.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced earnings results for the nine months ended Sept. 30, 2006. The company reported a net income of $366.6 million or $2.02 diluted per share on total revenues of $7.63 billion against net income of $848.9 million or $4.79 diluted per share on total revenues of $7.23 billion for the same period a year ago.
