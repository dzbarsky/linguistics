Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter of 2006.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter of 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $71.2 million, or 34 cents a share, compared with $115.2 million, or 64 cents a share, in the same period last year, as revenue dropped sharply. Excluding transaction costs related to the reorganization completed in the fourth quarter, the company posted per-share earnings of 42 cents vs. 86 cents last year. Revenue fell 26% to $1.8 billion from $2.42 billion.
