Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Full Year Ended December 31, 2006.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. reported earnings results for the full year ended December 31, 2006. For the period, the company reported net earnings of $437.8 million or $2.39 per diluted share on total revenue of $9,436,101,000 against net earnings of $964.1 million or $5.43 per diluted share on total revenues of $9,654,579,000 for the same period last year.
