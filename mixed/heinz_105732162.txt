HJ Heinz Co. Announces Appointment of Officers.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that Karen Alber has been promoted to Senior Vice President, Chief Information Officer and Global Program Management Officer and is named to the Office of the Chairman, which is the most senior policy-making executive management group in the Company led by William R. Johnson, Heinz's Chairman, President and CEO. As an OOC member, Ms. Alber will lead the newly established Program Management Office, which will focus on integrating and driving Company-wide initiatives to improve global effectiveness and efficiency. The company also announced that Tony Cassavechia has been appointed Global Processes and Business Enablement Officer, where he will work with functional and business leaders to enhance Heinz's process capabilities and he will assume business leadership for Project Keystone.
