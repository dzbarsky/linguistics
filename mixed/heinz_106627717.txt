HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividend on Common Stock, Third Cumulative Preferred Stock and First Series Payable on July 10 and July 1, 2010 Respectively.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividend on common stock. The common stock dividend will be raised from 42.0 cents to 45.0 cents quarterly for all shareholders of record as of June 24, 2010, payable July 10, 2010. The new annualized dividend is $1.80, which represents an increase of $0.12, or 7.1%, compared to last year's dividend. The Board also declared a dividend of 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable on July 1, 2010 to shareholders of record at the close of business on June 24, 2010.
