HJ Heinz Co. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended April 28, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Year 2011.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended April 28, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net income attributable to company of $192,366,000 or $0.60 per diluted share on sales of $2,724,810,000 compared to net income attributable to company of $175,135,000 or $0.55 per diluted share on sales of $2,515,768,000 for the same period a year ago. Income from continuing operations was $194,983,000 or $0.60 per diluted share compared to $181,210,000 or $0.56 per diluted share last year. Operating income was $345,241,000 compared to $338,968,000 last year. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $280,196,000 compared to $265,810,000 last year. For the year, the company reported net income attributable to company of $864,892,000 or $2.71 per diluted share on sales of $10,494,983,000 compared to net income attributable to company of $923,072,000 or $2.89 per diluted share on sales of $10,011,331,000 for the same period a year ago."
