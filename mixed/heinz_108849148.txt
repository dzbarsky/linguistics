Saatchinvest and the management of Complan and Casilan agreed to acquire 66% stake in Complan and Casilan from HJ Heinz Co..
Saatchinvest and the management of Complan and Casilan agreed to acquire 66% stake in Complan and Casilan from HJ Heinz Co. on August 15, 2002. Heinz has retained a 34% shareholding. The management team will acquire a 15% stake while Saatchinvest will be the majority shareholder with 51% stake. Olswang acted as legal advisor and KPMG acted as accountant to Saatchinvest while PricewaterhouseCoopers acted as financial advisor to Complan and Casilan.
