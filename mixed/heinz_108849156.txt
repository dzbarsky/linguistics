H.J. Heinz Company (NYSE:HNZ) acquired Petrosoyuz..
"H.J. Heinz Company (NYSE:HNZ) acquired Petrosoyuz on April 28, 2005. Financial terms of the agreement were not disclosed. Petrosoyuz has annual revenue of approximately $140 million. Co-founders of Petrosoyuz, Mark Budyko and Dmitry Filatov, will continue to provide their leadership and market knowledge, as co-Managing Directors of the joint venture. UBS Investment Bank advised H.J. Heinz Company on the deal. HJ Heinz Co. completed the acquisition of Petrosoyuz on April 28, 2005."
