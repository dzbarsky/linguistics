HJ Heinz Co., Annual General Meeting, Aug 31, 2010.
HJ Heinz Co., Annual General Meeting, Aug 31, 2010., at 09:08 US Eastern Time. Location: The Westin Convention Center Hotel. Agenda: To elect as directors the 12 nominees named in the attached proxy statement; to ratify the audit committee's selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the company's independent registered public accounting firm; to consider a shareholder proposal; and to consider and act on such other business as may properly come before the meeting.
