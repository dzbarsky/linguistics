HJ Heinz Co. Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter of Fiscal 2011; Reaffirms Earnings Outlook for the Fiscal Year 2011; Declares Dividend for Fiscal 2011.
"HJ Heinz Co. provided earnings guidance for the first quarter of fiscal 2011. For the period, the company expects to report organic sales growth of more than 3% (1.6% reported), marking the company's 21st consecutive quarter of organic top-line growth, almost 10% growth in operating income, net income growth of 13% and earnings per share of $0.75, an increase of more than 10% from continuing operations last year and operating free cash flow of over $200 million, an increase of 80% from last year. The company believes that its solid first-quarter results put Heinz on track to deliver its financial targets for the full year, even though the consumer and economic environment remains challenging. The company reaffirmed earnings outlook for the full year fiscal 2011. The company still expects to report sales growth of 3% to 4%, operating income growth of 7% to 10% and earnings per share growth of 7% to 10%. The company also expects operating free cash flow of more than $1 billion for the second consecutive year."
