HJ Heinz Co. Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended July 28, 2010.
HJ Heinz Co. reported consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended July 28, 2010. For the quarter, the companys sales were $2,480.8 million against $2,441.7 million a year ago. Operating income was $405.7 million against $369.1 million a year ago. The growth in operating income was driven by higher sales and gross margin as SG&A expenses were essentially flat in the quarter despite higher marketing expenses. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $332.8 million against $309.3 million a year ago. Income from continuing operations was $248.6 million against $221.3 million a year ago. Net income was $248.6 million against $219.1 million a year ago. Net income attributable to the company was $240.4 million or $0.75 diluted per share against $212.6 million or $0.67 diluted per share a year ago. Cash provided by operating activities was $272.4 million against $168.8 million a year ago. Capital expenditures were $55.6 million against $48.7 million a year ago.
