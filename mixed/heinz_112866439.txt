HJ Heinz Co. Ratifies the Selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as Independent Registered Public Accounting Firm for Fiscal Year 2011.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that at its annual meeting of shareholders was held on August 31, 2010, ratified the selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as independent registered public accounting firm for Fiscal Year 2011.
