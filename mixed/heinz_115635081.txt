HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Dividend on Common Stock Payable on January 10, 2011 and on Third Cumulative Preferred Stock Payable on January 1, 2011.
HJ Heinz Co. announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The board declared dividend of 45 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable on January 10, 2011 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 22, 2010 and 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable on January 1, 2011 to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 22, 2010.
