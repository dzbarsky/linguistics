HJ Heinz Co. Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended October 27, 2010; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2011.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended October 27, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported sales of $2,614,623,000, operating income of $416,799,000, income from continuing operations before income taxes of $346,530,000, income from continuing operations of $253,942,000 or $0.78 per diluted share and net income attributable to the company of $251,435,000 or $0.78 per diluted share against sales of $2,646,786,000, operating income of $408,400,000, income from continuing operations before income taxes of $334,666,000, income from continuing operations of $248,966,000 or $0.76 per diluted share and net income attributable to the company of $231,435,000 or $0.73 per diluted share for the same period last year. Cash provided by operating activities was $359,681,000 against $340,008,000 for the same period last year. Capital expenditures were $66,477,000 against $47,462,000 for the same period last year.
