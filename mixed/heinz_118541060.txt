HJ Heinz Co. (NYSE: HNZ) agreed to acquire the remaining 49% stake in Cairo Food Industries, S.A.E. from Kuwait Food Company (Americana) S.A.K. (KWSE: FOOD) for $62.1 million on December 15, 2009. The stake comprises approximately 0.12 million shares of Cairo Food Industries. Kuwait Food Company (Americana) would generate a profit of $39 million from the deal.
