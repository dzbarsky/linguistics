HJ Heinz Company Launches Heinz E Zee Squirt Purple in Canada.
HJ Heinz Company announced that Heinz E Zee Squirt Purple is coming to Canada joining Heinz E-Zee Squirt Red and Green in its growing complement of coloured condiments.Consumers will start seeing the colour purple on grocers shelves from coast to coast by just in time for Valentine's Day. The suggested retail price for Heinz E-Zee Squirt Purple is $3.49. The tremendous success of Heinz E-Zee Squirt Green showed us that kids love decorating their food with colours that are bright wild and fun.
