HJ Heinz Co. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter Ended January 26, 2011; Revises Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2011.
"HJ Heinz Co. provided earnings guidance for the third quarter ended January 26, 2011. The company anticipates earning of 84 cents per share for its fiscal third quarter. The company said that the results were fueled by growth in emerging markets. The company expects very strong operating free cash flow of around $440 million in the quarter. The company expects cash provided by operating activities of $510 million, and capital expenditures of $73 million. The company raised the outlook for full-year EPS to a range of $3.04 to $3.10, from its previous range of $2.95 to $3.05. The company also raised its outlook for full-year operating free cash flow to $1.2 billion, up from a previous projection of $1.15 billion."
