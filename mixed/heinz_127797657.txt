HJ Heinz Co. Announces Availability of Dip & Squeeze Ketchup.
HJ Heinz Co. has announced the national availability of Heinz Dip & Squeeze Ketchup, a new packaging that allows for both dipping and squeezing and holds three times as much Heinz Ketchup as the traditional packet. Heinz Dip & Squeeze Ketchup is now available at Chick-fil-A restaurants nationwide and will be rolling out in select quick-service restaurants in the coming months.
