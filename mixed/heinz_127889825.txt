HJ Heinz Co. Announces Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended January 26, 2011.
HJ Heinz Co. announced consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended January 26, 2011. For the quarter, the companys sales were $2,722,350,000 compared to $2,681,702,000 for the same period in 2010. Operating income was $438,398,000 compared to $436,510,000 for the same period in 2010. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $374,015,000 compared to $366,260,000 for the same period in 2010. Income from continuing operations was $276,527,000 or $0.84 per diluted share compared to $266,737,000 or $0.83 per diluted share for the same period in 2010. Net income attributable to company was $273,785,000 or $0.84 per diluted share compared to $228,527,000 or $0.72 per diluted share for the same period in 2010. Income from continuing operations, net of tax attributable to H.J. Heinz Company common shareholders were $273,785,000 compared to $264,115,000 for the same period in 2010.
