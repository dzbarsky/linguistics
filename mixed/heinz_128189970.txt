HJ Heinz Co. Declares Quarterly Common and Preferred Stock Dividends, Payable on April 10, 2011.
HJ Heinz Co. declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. 45 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value common stock payable on April 10, 2011 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2011 and 42.5 cents per share on the company's Third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable on April 1, 2011 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2011.
