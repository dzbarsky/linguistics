"Lehman Brothers Merchant Banking, through its fund Lehman Brothers Merchant Banking Partners III, agreed to acquire the UK and European seafood operations of HJ Heinz Co. (NYSE: HNZ) for approximately 430 million on February 2, 2006. Lehman Brothers Merchant Banking would retain Adolfo Valsecchi, the current chief executive of the European seafood business, and bring in David Williams as executive chairman. The Heinz seafood business has annual sales of approximately 425 million. The sale is expected to be completed in April 2006, following approval from the French Works Council and EU competition authorities. On March 9, 2006, the European Commission approved the transaction. Rabobank International and Icelandic bank acted as arranger for the financing package for the transaction. The transaction is subject to customary EU competition review and regulatory approval. The transaction has been approved by Borad of Directors of H.J. Heinz Company. The transaction is expected to close by the end of March 2006."
