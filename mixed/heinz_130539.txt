Heinz reports slight earnings increase in the Second Quarter of 2002.
"H.J. Heinz reported earnings rose 2 percent to $212 million, or 60 cents per share, in the three months ending Oct. 30, compared with $208 million, or 59 cents per share, in the same period a year ago. Excluding one-time costs, Heinz earned $219 million, or 62 cents per share, in the latest quarter, beating analysts' expectations of 58 cents per share. In the first six months of the year, Heinz reported earnings of $312 million, or $1.10 per share, down from $408.7 million, or $1.16 per share, during the same time last year. Revenues were up 6.3 percent to $4.8 billion from $4.5 billion in the first two quarters of last year."
