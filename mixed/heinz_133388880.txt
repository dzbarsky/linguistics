"HJ Heinz Co. announced consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended April 27, 2011. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $320,833,000 compared to $280,196,000 for the same period in 2010. Net income attributable to company was $223,863,000 or $0.69 per diluted share compared to $192,366,000 or $0.60 per diluted share for the same period in 2010. Income from continuing operations, net of tax attributable to the company's common shareholders were $223,863,000 compared to $192,574,000 for the same period in 2010.
