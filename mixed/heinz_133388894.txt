HJ Heinz Co. Declares Common Stock Dividend Payable on July 10, 2011; Declares Preferred Stock $1.70 First Series Dividend Payable on July 1, 2011.
"The Board of HJ Heinz Co. declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The company common stock dividend will be increased from 45.0 cents to 48.0 cents quarterly for all shareholders of record as of June 24, 2011, payable on July 10, 2011. The new annualized dividend is $1.92, which represents an increase of $0.12, or 6.7%, versus last year's dividend. The company also declared a dividend of 42.5 cents per share on the company's third cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable on July 1, 2011 to shareholders of record at the close of business on June 24, 2011."
