Heinz Announces Job Cuts Around the World.
Heinz has revealed it is to slash up to 1,000 jobs globally. As part of plans to deliver cost savings and productivity gains, Heinz plans to close five factories, including two in Europe, two in the US and one in the Pacific region, leaving it with 76 factories around the world. The food manufacturer expects to cut its global workforce by between 800 and 1,000. One of the European facilities earmarked for closure is its Miedzychod factory in Poland.
