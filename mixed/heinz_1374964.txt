HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months ended January 26, 2005 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for Fiscal 2005.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the nine months ended January 26, 2005. For the period, the company reported that sales were $6,463,805,000 against $6,083,166,000 for the same period a year ago. Net income was $546,212,000 or $1.54 per share against $607,749,000 or $1.72 per share for the same period a year ago. The company said that it sees its fiscal 2005 net income, excluding items, coming in nearer the lower end of a $2.32-$2.42 per share range."
