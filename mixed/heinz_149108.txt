Heinz/Del Monte Announced Results For Second Quarter Fiscal 2003.
HJ Heinz Co. announced second quarter Fiscal 2003 operating highlights (three months ending October 30, 2002). Second quarter total sales for the period ended Oct. 30, 2002 is $471.7 million when compared to $479.1 million to the same period last year. Operating income for the second quarter fiscal 2003 is $79.7 million when compared to $71.1 million to the same period last year.
