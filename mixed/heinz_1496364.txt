Heinz Declares Quarterly Dividend on Common and Preferred Stock Payable on April 10, 2005 and April 1, 2005 Respectively.
The H. J. Heinz Company announced that its Board of Directors declared quarterly dividends on both common and preferred stock. The board declared dividend of 28.5 cents per share on the company's 25 cent par value Common Stock payable April 10, 2005 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2005 and dividend of 42.5 cents per share on the company's third Cumulative Preferred Stock, $1.70 First Series, payable April 1, 2005 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 24, 2005.
