Heinz reports profits for fourth quarter 2003.
"HJ Heinz Co. reported fourth-quarter earnings fell to $102.6 million, or 29 cents per share, compared with $223.5 million, or 63 cents per share, in the same period last year. Excluding nonrecurring items, Heinz earned $184.8 million, or 52 cents per share, just shy of Wall Street expectations. Fourth-quarter sales from continuing operations rose 6 percent to $2.19 billion, compared with sales of $2.07 billion in the last quarter of 2002. Heinz reported a net income of $566.3 million, or $1.60 per share, for the year compared with $833.9 million, or $2.36 per share, in 2002. Excluding special items, Heinz reported annual earnings of $717.7 million, or $2.03 per share, compared with $684.1 million, or $1.94 per share, last year."
