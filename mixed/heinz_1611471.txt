HJ Heinz Co. Announces the Retirement of Michael J. Bertasso.
The H.J. Heinz Co. announced the retirement of Michael J. Bertasso (55), effective June 1, 2005. Currently located in Singapore, Mr. Bertasso is Senior Vice President and President - Heinz Asia Pacific. Mr. Bertasso has been with the company for 17 years and serves on Heinz's Management Committee.
