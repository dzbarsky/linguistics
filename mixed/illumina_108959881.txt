Illumina Inc. Appoints Nicholas J. Naclerio as Senior Vice President, Corporate Development.
Illumina Inc. announced that it has appointed Nicholas J. Naclerio, Ph.D., to the position of senior vice president, Corporate Development, reporting to Jay Flatley, president and chief executive officer. In this capacity, Dr. Naclerio will be responsible for all Corporate and Business Development activities, including licensing, strategic planning and acquisitions. Before joining the company, Dr. Naclerio served as co-founder and executive chairman of Quanterix, focusing on high-value clinical diagnostics measuring rare serum proteins.
