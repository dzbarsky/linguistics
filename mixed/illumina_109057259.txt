Illumina Inc. Names Gerald Moller to its Board of Directors.
Illumina Inc. announced that Gerald Moller has joined the company's board of directors. Dr. Moller is currently an advisor at HBM Bio Ventures AG. Previously, Dr. Moller spent 23 years at Boehringer Mannheim in Germany, Japan and the US, where he held a number of leadership positions, including president of Decentralized Diagnostics, president of Advanced Diagnostics and Biochemicals, and CEO of Boehringer Mannheim Therapeutics.
