BIOCOM, Inc., BIOCOM's Global Life Science Partnering Conference, Feb 22, 2011 through Feb 24, 2011. Venue: Estancia La Jolla Hotel and Spa, 9700 North Torrey Pines Road, La Jolla, San Diego, CA 92037, United States. BIOCOM's Global Life Science Partnering Conference is an exclusive global partnering and networking forum that brings together senior executives and business development professionals from leading pharmaceutical and biotech companies. The conference includes case study presentations, individual company presentations, one-one-one meetings, and numerous networking opportunities.
