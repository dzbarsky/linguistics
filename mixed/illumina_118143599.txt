Illumina Announces Preliminary Financial Results for Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2010 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2010 and Full Year of 2011.
Illumina Inc. reported revenue results for the fourth quarter and full year of 2010. The company preliminary revenue for the fourth quarter of 2010 of approximately $260 million. This implies fiscal year 2010 revenue of approximately $901 million or annual growth of 35%.
