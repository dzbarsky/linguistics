Illumina Inc Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended January 2, 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2011.
Illumina Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended January 2, 2011. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $261,298,000 against $180,564,000 in the same period of last year. Income from operations was $68,668,000 against $25,687,000 in the same period of last year. Income before income taxes was $50,782,000 against $22,288,000 in the same period of last year. Net income was $38,440,000 or $0.25 per diluted share against net income of $11,705,000 or $0.09 per diluted share in the same period of last year. Net cash provided by operating activities was $81,481,000 against $61,290,000 in the same period of last year. Purchases of property and equipment were $12,384,000 against $6,385,000 in the same period of last year. Free cash flow was $69,097,000 against $54,905,000 in the same period of last year.
