Illumina Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 10, 2011.
Illumina Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 10, 2011., at 09:05 Pacific Standard Time. Agenda: To elect the two director nominees named in the 2011 Proxy Statement to hold office for three years until the 2014 annual meeting of stockholders; to elect the one director nominee named in the 2011 Proxy Statement to hold office for two years until the 2013 annual meeting of stockholders; to ratify the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending January 1, 2012; to hold an advisory vote on executive compensation; to hold an advisory vote on the frequency of holding an advisory vote on executive compensation; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting and any adjournment or postponement thereof.
