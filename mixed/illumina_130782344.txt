Illumina Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended April 3, 2011.
Illumina Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended April 3, 2011. For the quarter, the company has posted total revenue of $282,515,000 against $192,131,000 a year ago. Income from operations was $69,388,000 against $38,225,000 a year ago. Income before income tax was $36,008,000 against $33,361,000 a year ago. Net income was $24,137,000 or $0.16 diluted per share against $21,208,000 or $0.16 diluted per share a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $88,578,000 against $59,065,000 a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $50,267,000 or $0.35 diluted per share against $26,558,000 or $0.21 diluted per share a year ago. Non-GAAP operating profit was $99,328,000 against $57,763,000 a year ago. Capital expenditures were $12,300,000.
