Illumina Inc. (NasdaqGS: ILMN) acquired EPICENTRE Technologies Corporation for $90 million in cash and stock on January 10, 2011. Total consideration exchanged for the acquisition includes $60 million in cash, $15 million in stock that is subject to forfeiture if certain non-revenue based milestones are not met, and up to $15 million in contingent consideration payments based on the achievement of certain revenue-based milestones by January 10, 2013. All of Illumina's Madison, Wisconsin based employees are expected to remain in their current location.
