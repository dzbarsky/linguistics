Illumina Inc.'s Equity Buyback in the amount of $425 mm has expired.
"The Board of Directors of Illumina, Inc. (NasdaqGS: ILMN) authorized a share repurchase program on March 14, 2011. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $425 million of its shares in privately negotiated transactions. The repurchases will be funded from the net proceeds received from the offerings of $800 million principal amount of 0.25% Convertible Senior Notes due 2016.
