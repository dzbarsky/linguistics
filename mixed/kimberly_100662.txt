Former FASB Chairman Dennis R. Beresford Elected to Kimberly-Clark's Board Of Directors.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced that Dennis (Denny) R. Beresford, former chairman of the Financial Accounting Standards Board from 1987 to 1997, has been elected to its board of directors. Mr. Beresford, 63, is the Ernst & Young Executive Professor of Accounting at the University of Georgia's Terry College of Business.
