Kimberly-Clark Corporation Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2010; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Year 2010.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company has reported income before income taxes and equity interests of $609 million, income before equity interests of $368 million and net income attributable to company of $384 million or $0.92 per diluted share on net sales of $4,835 million compared to income before income taxes and equity interests of $563 million, income before equity interests of $399 million and net income attributable to company of $407 million or $0.98 per diluted share on net sales of $4,493 million reported in the same period last year. Operating profit was $665 million in the first quarter of 2010, up 6% from $628 million in 2009. Cash provided by operations in the first quarter of 2010 totaled $464 million compared to $692 million in the prior year.
