TRIMLINE Medical Products Corp. acquired the Blood Pressure Cuff products business from Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB)..
"TRIMLINE Medical Products Corp. acquired the Blood Pressure Cuff products business from Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB) on July 19, 2005. TRIMLINE Medical will continue to service current Kimberly-Clark customers with equivalent products under the existing part numbering system. TRIMLINE Medical Products Corp. completed the acquisition of the Blood Pressure Cuff products business from Kimberly-Clark Corporation (NYSE: KMB) on July 19, 2005."
