Kimberly-Clark Corporation Appoints Nancy J. Karch to the Board of Directors.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced that it has elected Nancy J. Karch to the board of directors, effective immediately. Ms. Karch is a retired Director of McKinsey & Co. Ms. Karch also was appointed to the audit committee of the Corporation's board of directors. Ms. Karch will participate in the corporation's outside director compensation plan.
