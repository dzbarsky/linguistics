Kimberly-Clark Corporation and Kimberly-Clark Professional Ltd. Announce Management Changes.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced that Jan Spencer, currently president of Kimberly-Clark Professional Ltd., has been elected as the senior vice president of continuous improvement, sourcing and sustainability. Christian Brickman, senior vice president and chief strategy officer for the company, has been elected as the president of Kimberly-Clark Professional Ltd., succeeding Mr. Spencer. Elane Stock, currently national vice president of the American Cancer Society, will join the company in mid-August 2010 as the senior vice president and chief strategy officer. In his new role, Mr. Spencer will be responsible for accelerating the adoption of business process improvement initiatives across the company's global operations, as well as driving sustainability initiatives deeper into the business units. Mr. Brickman joined Kimberly-Clark in 2008. Prior to joining the company, he served as a principal at McKinsey and Company.
