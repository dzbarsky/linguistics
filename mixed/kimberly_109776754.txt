Kimberly-Clark Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2010; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2010.
"Kimberly-Clark Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2010.
