Kimberly-Clark and Yahoo! Launch New Internet Parenting Category on Yahoo! Health.
Kimberly-Clark Corp. and Yahoo! Inc. announced an agreement to provide best-of-the-Web parenting information to Yahoo! Health, a comprehensive destination for people seeking health information online. This collaboration brings together Kimberly-Clark's worldwide infant and child care expertise with the extensive reach of the leading global Internet brand. Through its award-winning Web site, Parentstages.com Kimberly-Clark will supply Web-based information and resources to the new Parenting category on Yahoo! Health.
