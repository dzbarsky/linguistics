Kimberly-Clark Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2010; Provides Earnings Outlook for the Full Year of 2011; Declares Quarterly Dividend Payable on April 4, 2011.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net sales of $5,075 million, operating profit of $699 million, income before income taxes and equity interests of $640 million and net income attributable to company of $492 million or $1.20 diluted per share against net sales of $4,982 million, operating profit of $717 million, income before income taxes and equity interests of $658 million and net income attributable to company of $492 million or $1.17 diluted per share for the same period a year ago. Cash provided by operations was $948 million against $1,001 million for the same period a year ago. Capital spending was $353 million against $285 million for the same period a year ago.
