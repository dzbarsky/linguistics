Kimberly-Clark To Sell Plants.
Kimberly-Clark Corp intends to streamline, sell or close five or six plants by the end of 2012. Everett Mayor Ray Stephanson stated that he will "fully support Kimberly-Clark's efforts to secure a buyer that will protect jobs and minimize impacts on our community." Kimberly-Clark also plans to offload two plants in Australia, Chairman and CEO Thomas Falk said. Kimberly-Clark's stock added 2.6% to close at $65.61.
