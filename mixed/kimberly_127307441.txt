Kimberly-Clark Corporation Announces Dennis R. Beresford Not to Stand for Re-Election to the Board of Directors.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced that Dennis R. Beresford will not stand for re-election to the Board of Directors when his current term expires at the corporation's 2011 Annual Meeting of Stockholders to be held on April 21, 2011. Mr. Beresford will continue to serve as a director until the Annual Meeting. Mr. Beresford has served on the Corporation's Board since 2002. He currently serves on the Executive Committee and as Chairman of the Audit Committee.
