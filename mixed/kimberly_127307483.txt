Kimberly-Clark Corporation, Annual General Meeting, Apr 21, 2011.
Kimberly-Clark Corporation, Annual General Meeting, Apr 21, 2011., at 09:04 Central Standard Time. Location: The Four Seasons Resort and Club. Agenda: To consider Dennis R. Beresford will not stand for re-election to the Board of Directors when his current term expires; to elect as directors the twelve nominees named in the accompanying proxy statement; to ratify the selection of Deloitte & Touche LLP as independent auditors for 2011; to approve the 2011 Outside Directors' Compensation Plan; to approve the 2011 Equity Participation Plan; to approve the executive compensation program for named executive officers; to approve the frequency of stockholder votes on named executive officers' compensation; and to take action upon any other business that may properly come before the meeting or any adjournments of the meeting.
