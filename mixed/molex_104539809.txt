Molex Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended March 31, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ending June 30, 2010.
Molex Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended March 31, 2010. Revenue for the March 2010 quarter was $756.3 million increased 49.6% from the March 2009 quarter. Net income was $23.8 million or $0.14 per diluted share, compared with net income of $19.3 million or $0.11 per share, for the December 2009 quarter. For the March 2010 quarter, net income included a pretax restructuring charge of $9.0 million ($7.4 million after-tax or $0.04 per share) and a pretax loss of $31.0 million ($19.8 million after-tax or $0.11 per share) related to unauthorized activities in Molex Japan. The effective tax rate for the quarter was 30.5%. On a non-GAAP basis, net income for the March 2010 quarter was $51.0 million or $0.29 per share, compared with net income of $41.4 million or $0.24 per share, in the December 2009 quarter.
