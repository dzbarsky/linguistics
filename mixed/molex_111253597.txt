Molex Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended June 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter Ending September 2010.
"Molex Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended June 30, 2010. For the quarter, net revenue was $547.3 million against $570.6 million a year ago. Income from operations was $62.9 million against loss from operations of $218.7 million a year ago. Income before income taxes was $61.2 million against loss before income taxes was $217.97 million a year ago. Net income was $39.8 million or $0.23 per diluted share against net loss of $220.5 million or $1.27 per diluted share a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $67.5 million or $0.39 per dilute share against non-GAAP net loss of $15.6 million or $0.09 per diluted share a year ago. Capital expenditures were $79.5 million or 9.4% of revenue due to investments for new product introductions and general capacity increases to keep pace with customer demand.
