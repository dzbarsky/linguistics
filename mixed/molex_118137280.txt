Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) acquired Active Optical Cable business of Luxtera, Inc..
"Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) acquired Active Optical Cable business of Luxtera, Inc. on January 11, 2011. The terms of the deal were not disclosed. Katie Lister of Vantage Communications acted as the PR advisor for Luxtera. Christa Carroll of Outlook Marketing Services acted as the PR advisor for Molex. Fenwick & West, LLP acted as the legal advisor to Luxtera, Inc. Molex Inc. (NasdaqGS: MOLX) completed the acquisition of Active Optical Cable business of Luxtera, Inc. on January 11, 2011."
