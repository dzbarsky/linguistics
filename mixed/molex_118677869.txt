Molex Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended December 31, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Quarter Ending March 2011.
Molex Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended December 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net revenue of USD 901.5 million, net income of USD 78.3 million or diluted earnings per share of USD 0.45 compared to net revenue of USD 729.6 million, net income of USD 13.8 million or earnings per share of USD 0.08 for the same period last year. Net income for the quarter was impacted by legal costs related to unauthorized activities in Japan of USD 2.7 million (USD 1.7 million after-tax or USD 0.01 per share). The company reported income from operations of USD 109.29 million and income before income taxes of USD 112.29 million compared to income from operations of USD 28.25 million and income before income taxes of USD 26.26 million for the same period last year.
