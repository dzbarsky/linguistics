Molex Inc. Declares Regular Quarterly Common Stock Cash Dividend Payable on April 25, 2011.
Molex Inc. announced that a regular quarterly cash dividend of $0.175 per share will be paid to holders of Common Stock (MOLX), Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and Class B Common Stock, payable on April 25, 2011 to stockholders of record on March 31, 2011.
