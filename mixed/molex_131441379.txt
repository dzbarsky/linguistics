Molex Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended March 31, 2011; Approves Quarterly Cash Dividend Payable on July 25, 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2011.
"Molex Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended March 31, 2011. For the third quarter ended March 31, 2011, the company reported net revenues of $874.5 million, income from operations of $98.3 million, income before income taxes of $97.9 million, net income of $68.1 million or $0.39 diluted per share against net revenues of $756.3 million, income from operations of $62.2 million, income before income taxes of $57.2 million, net income of $38.4 million or $0.22 diluted per share for the same period a year ago. Capital expenditures were $64.2 million or 7.3% of revenue. For the nine months ended March 31, 2011, the company reported net revenues of $2,673.7 million, income from operations of $310.0 million, income before income taxes of $321.0 million, net income of $221.5 million or $1.26 diluted per share against net revenues of $2,159.9 million, income from operations of $74.8 million, income before income taxes of $70.3 million, net income of $37.2 mil"
