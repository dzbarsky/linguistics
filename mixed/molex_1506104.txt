Molex to Invest $70 Million in New China Plant.
Molex Inc. will invest around $70 million to build a factory in Chengdu, capital city of southwestern China's Sichuan Province. 'The new factory, Molex's fourth one in China after Dalian, Shanghai and Dongguan, will reach full capacity in January 2007, with an expected annual revenue of US $200 million'.
