Henry F. McCance, Chairman of Greylock Partners acquired 1.18% stake in New England Sports Ventures, LLC from The New York Times Company (NYSE: NYT)..
"Henry F. McCance, Chairman of Greylock Partners acquired 1.18% stake in New England Sports Ventures, LLC from The New York Times Company (NYSE: NYT) on April 1, 2010. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The sale was for 50 of a total of 750 units owned by The New York Times Company. The New York Times Company will continue to own a 16.57% interest in New England Sports Ventures, LLC. The New York Times will record a pre-tax gain in the second quarter of 2010 relating to the sale. Hale Holden of Barclays Capital acted as financial advisor in the transaction for The New York Times Company. Goldman Sachs Group Inc. (NYSE: GS) acted as financial advisor for The New York Times Company. Henry F. McCance, Chairman of Greylock Partners completed the acquisition of 1.18% stake in New England Sports Ventures, LLC from The New York Times Company (NYSE: NYT) on April 1, 2010."
