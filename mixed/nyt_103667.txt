The New York Times Company Reports Results for Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2002.
"New York Times Company announced that fourth-quarter adjusted diluted earnings per share were $0.69, up 23.2 percent from $0.56 in the 2001 fourth quarter, and adjusted net income for the fourth quarter rose 24.0 percent to $106.7 million from $86.1 million in the fourth quarter of 2001. Total revenues for the Company rose 7.6 percent to $840.2 million in the fourth quarter from $780.6 million in the 2001 fourth quarter. For the full year, adjusted diluted earnings per share were $1.97, up 8.2 percent from $1.82 in 2001 and adjusted net income was $304.4 million, up 4.5 percent from $291.2 million in 2001. Total revenues for the full year of 2002 were $3,079,007,000, when compared to the revenues of $3,015,958,000 for the full year of 2001."
