The About Group Names Mitchell Kreuch as Senior Vice President of Sales.
The About Group announced that it has named Mitchell Kreuch as senior vice president, sales. He will manage and oversee all online sales efforts including strategy, programming and after-sale account support. Additionally, he will be responsible for achieving targeted advertising sales revenues. Most recently, he was the vice president of sales for the eastern and midwest regions at MySpace Inc. In his new role, Mr. Kreuch will report directly to Cella Irvine, president and C.E.O.
