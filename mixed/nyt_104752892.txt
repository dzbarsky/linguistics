Ford Foundation, Levy Economics Institute of Bard College, 19th Annual Hyman P. Minsky Conference on the State of the U.S. and World Economies, Apr-14-2010 through Apr-16-2010. Venue: Ford Foundation, 320 East 43 Street, New York City, United States. The Levy Economics Institute gathered top policymakers, economists, and analysts at the 19th Annual Hyman P. Minsky Conference on the State of the U.S. and World Economies to discuss efforts to reconstitute and reregulate the U.S. and international financial system in the wake of the global financial crisis.
