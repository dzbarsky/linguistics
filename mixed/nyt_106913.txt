The New York Times Company Promotes Rhonda L. Brauer to Corporate Secretary.
The New York Times Co. announced the promotion of Rhonda L. Brauer to the position of secretary. In her new role, Ms. Brauer, 42, will continue to report to Mr. Watson and will retain her responsibilities in the Legal Department for providing corporate and securities law advice. Ms. Brauer joined the Times Company in 1992 as an attorney and was promoted to senior counsel in 1994 and to assistant secretary in 1996.
