Anil Ambani Withdraws $2.1 Billion Defamation Lawsuit against The New York Times Company.
Anil Ambani has withdrawn $2.1 billion defamation lawsuit against The New York Times Company and his estranged elder brother Mukesh. Bombay High Court judge granted Anil's request to withdraw the $2.1 billion suit, though he declined to say why Anil had decided to drop the litigation. Anil had not filed suit in the U.S.
