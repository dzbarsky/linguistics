Thomas Middelhoff Joins The New York Times Company Board Of Directors.
The New York Times Co. announced that Thomas Middelhoff, a managing director at Investcorp, has been elected to its Board of Directors. Dr. Middelhoff is Head of Europe for corporate investment at Investcorp, the global investment firm, which he joined this year. From 1998 to 2002, he was chairman and chief executive of Bertelsmann AG.
