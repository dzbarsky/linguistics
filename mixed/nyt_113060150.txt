New York Times Gains On Takeover Rumors.
The New York Times Company gained stock rose 62 cents, or 8% in New York Stock Exchange composite trading for the biggest gain since June 10 on renewed speculation the company may be acquired.  The speculators are betting that someone may come in and bid for the New York Times, said Chris Rich, head options strategist at JonesTrading Institutional Services LLC. The rumor mill is being cranked up again saying Carlos Slim wants to buy the company, said Edward Atorino, an analyst at Benchmark Co. LLC in New York.
