The New York Times Company announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $400 mm.
The Board of Directors of The New York Times Company (NYSE: NYT) authorized a share repurchase program on April 13, 2004. Under the program, the company will repurchase an additional $400 million of its Class A common stock. The repurchased shares will be used for general corporate purposes including the company's stock-based employee benefit plans. Under the authorization, purchases may be made from time to time either in the open market or through private transactions. Purchases may be suspended from time to time or discontinued. The repurchase program has no expiration date. As of July 30, 2010, the company has repurchased 8,721,161 shares for $308.61 million, under the program.
