The New York Times Company Names Kerrie Gillis as Vice President for Advertising.
The New York Times Company announced that Kerrie Gillis had been named a vice president for advertising. Ms. Gillis, who joined The Times in 1985, was most recently the advertising director, corporate, for The Times and as the advertising director for the Americas for The International Herald Tribune. She will now be in charge of print and digital advertising in various categories including advocacy, corporate, sports marketing and classified advertising.
