The New York Times Company Appoints Hugo Lindgren as Editor of the New York Times Magazine Effective October 25, 2010.
The New York Times Company announced that Hugo Lindgren has been named editor of The New York Times Magazine effective October 25, 2010. He will report to New York Times Executive Editor Bill Keller. Mr. Lindgren, 42, was executive editor of Bloomberg Business week since March 2010, where he helped conceive and manage a complete redesign of the magazine under new ownership.
