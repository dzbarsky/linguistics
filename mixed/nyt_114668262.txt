The New York Times Company Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months of 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth-Quarter and Full Year 2010; Announces Write Down of Assets for the Third Quarter of 2010.
"The New York Times Company reported consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months of 2010. For the quarter, the company reported revenues of $554,333,000 compared to $569,462,000 in the same quarter a year ago. Operating profit was $8,993,000 compared to operating loss of $23,693,000 for the same period last year. Loss from continuing operations before income taxes was $6,152,000 compared to $37,223,000 for the same period last year. Loss from continuing operations was $4,134,000 compared to $34,741,000 for the same period last year. Loss attributable to company was $4,261,000 or diluted per share of $0.03 compared to loss attributable to company of $35,624,000 or diluted per share of $0.25 for the same period last year.
