The New York Times Company Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2010.
"The New York Times Company provided an update on its outlook for fourth quarter of 2010 and full year 2010 at the UBS 38th Annual Global Media and Communications Conference. For the quarter, print advertising revenues were expected to improve over third quarter levels, with a decline of approximately 4% year-over-year. Digital advertising revenues were expected to be up by approximately 10%, while circulation revenues are expected to decrease by 4% to 5%. The company expected full year 2010 operating profit, excluding depreciation, amortization, severance and special items, to show significant improvement over 2009."
