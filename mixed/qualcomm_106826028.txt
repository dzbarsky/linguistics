Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Cessation of Ben Naskar as Vice President and General Manager of Networking.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that on May 28, 2010, Ben Naskar ceased to be Vice President and General Manager of Networking. Mr. Naskar will serve as an employee of the company in an advisory capacity through June 15, 2010, on which date his employment with the company will terminate without cause.
