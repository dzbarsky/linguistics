Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Board Changes.
On August 17, 2010, John Hennessy, the Chairman of Atheros Communications Inc. Board of Directors, notified the Board of Directors of his intent to retire from the Board, effective on September 30, 2010. The Board has appointed Willy Shih, a member of its Board of Directors since November 2006, as the Chairman of the Board, to be effective following Dr. Hennessys retirement from the Board. Dr. Shih is currently a Professor of Management Practice at The Harvard Business School and previously served in senior management positions at Thomson (now Technicolor) and Eastman Kodak Company.
