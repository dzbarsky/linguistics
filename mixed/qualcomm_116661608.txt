Atheros Communications Inc. Wi-Fi(R) and Bluetooth(R) Solutions Provide Best-in-Class Wireless Connectivity for Toshiba's New Tablet PC.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that its mobile Wi-Fi(R) and Bluetooth(R) combination solution is delivering best-in-class wireless connectivity for the new Toshiba Folio 100 tablet PC. Toshiba is using Atheros' ROCm(R) single-stream 802.11n technology to provide high-speed access to media content, online applications and social networks, and its Bluetooth technology to easily connect the tablet to wireless keyboards, mice and stereo headsets.
