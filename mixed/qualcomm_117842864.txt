QUALCOMM In Talks To Buy Atheros For $3.5 Billion.
QUALCOMM Incorporated is in talks to buy Atheros Communications Inc. The shares of Atheros rose 19% after reports of a deal. The New York Times reported on January 4, 2011 that QUALCOMM could be close to buying Atheros for around $45 a share, or $3.5 billion. Reuters said that QUALCOMM and Atheros could not immediately be reached for comment. The shares of Atheros closed up 19% at $44 and rose to $44.24 in after-hours trading. Qualcomms shares closed 1.6% higher at $50.97.
