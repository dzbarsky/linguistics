Atheros Communications Inc. (NasdaqGS: ATHR) signed a definitive agreement to acquire Intellon Corporation (NasdaqGM: ITLN) for approximately $230 million in cash and stock on September 8, 2009. As reported under the terms of the transaction, Intellon's shareholder will elect to receive 0.135 shares of Atheros' common stock and $3.6 in cash; or approximately 0.267 shares of Atheros' common stock; or $7.3 in cash for each share of Intellon. Each issued and outstanding share of Intellons common stock will be automatically converted into the right to receive Atheros common stock and equivalents representing between 45 and 55% of the total consideration and with the remainder being paid in cash. All outstanding options to purchase Intellon common stock will be converted into an option to acquire shares of Atheros common stock on the basis of a pre-defined option exchange ratio.
