Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Unaudited Preliminary Consolidated Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2010.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced unaudited preliminary consolidated financial results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net revenue of $226.822 million against $185.678 million a year ago. Loss from operations was $19.176 million against income from operation of $16.504 million a year ago. Net loss was $23.217 million or $0.32 per diluted share against net income of $15.633 million or $0.24 per diluted share a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $41.956 million or $0.57 per diluted share against $41.174 million or $0.62 per diluted share a year ago. Non-GAAP income from operations was $42.813 million against $43.368 million a year ago. The company generated $32.9 million in cash flow from operations. For the year, the company reported net revenue of $926.832 million against $542.468 million a year ago. Income from operations was $61.691 million against $22.178 million a year ago.
