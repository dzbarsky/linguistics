Wilocity Ltd. announced that it has received $20 million in funding from Atheros Communications Inc., Benchmark Capital, Sequoia Capital Israel, and Tallwood Venture Capital..
"Wilocity Ltd. announced that it has raised $20 million in its third round of funding led by new investor Atheros Communications Inc. on July 15, 2010. The round saw participation from existing investors including Benchmark Capital, Tallwood Venture Capital, and Sequoia Capital Israel. The company has raised some $47 million in funding till date. On July 15, 2010, Wilocity Ltd. closed the transaction."
