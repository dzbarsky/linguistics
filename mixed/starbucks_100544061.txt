Inform Business Impact, 2010 Workforce Analytics & Planning Conference, Apr-19-2010 through Apr-20-2010.
Inform Business Impact, 2010 Workforce Analytics & Planning Conference, Apr-19-2010 through Apr-20-2010. Venue: Capital Hilton hotel, Washington, DC, United States. The conference will offer attendees groundbreaking insights and research on workforce planning, reporting and analytics from international thought leaders. In addition, the conference will offer a range of case study presentations - for practitioners in the early stages of launching workforce analytics and planning, as well as experts seeking advanced practices - practical workshops and networking opportunities.
