Starbucks Launches Starbucks VIA(R) Coffee Essence in Japan.
Starbucks announced the launching of Starbucks VIA(R) Coffee Essence in Japan. Starbucks VIA(R) Coffee Essence is Starbucks first premium coffee stick product in Japan. With the body, flavor and rich aroma expected from Starbucks(R) coffee, Starbucks VIA(R) Coffee Essence will create a new category for coffee drinkers looking to enjoy Starbucks quality coffee anytime and anywhere.
