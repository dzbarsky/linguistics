Starbucks Reports results for December month and for the quarter ended december for fiscal 2003.
"Starbucks Corporation has reported consolidated net revenues of $418 million for the five-week period ended December 29, 2002, an increase of 22 percent from consolidated net revenues of $343 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 7 percent for the five weeks ended December 29, 2002 as compared to the same five-week period in fiscal 2002. For the 13 weeks ended December 29, 2002, consolidated net revenues were $1.0 billion, an increase of 25 percent from consolidated net revenues of $805 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. Comparable store sales increased 9 percent for the 13 weeks ended December 29, 2002, as compared to the same 13 weeks in fiscal 2002."
