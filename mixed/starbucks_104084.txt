Starbucks Appoints Myron E. Ullman to Board of Directors.
Starbucks Coffee Company, a leading retailer roaster and brand of specialty coffee in the world, has announced that Myron E. Ullman has joined the Company's Board of Directors. He joins nine other board members: Barbara Bass, Howard Behar, Craig Foley, Gregory Maffei, Arlen Prentice, Howard Schultz, James Shennan, Jr., Orin Smith, and Craig Weatherup.
