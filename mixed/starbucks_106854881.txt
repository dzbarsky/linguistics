Starbucks Corp Announces Sales Results for the First Quarter Ended May 24, 2010.
Starbucks Corp. announced sales results for the first quarter ended May 24, 2010. The company's first quarter sales fell 4.7% to $6.9 million, down 1.8% on a same-store-sales basis. The sales decline had slowed and was expected to revert to same-store-sales growth with new promotions and customer offers.
