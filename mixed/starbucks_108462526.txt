Starbucks Files Shelf Registration; Mulls Acquisitions.
Starbucks Corp. has filed a Shelf Registration with the SEC. The company may offer and sell debt securities from time to time. The company will provide a prospectus supplement each time it offer and issue the securities. The company intends to use the net proceeds from the sale of the securities under this prospectus for general corporate purposes, which may include the repayment of indebtedness outstanding from time to time, the repurchase of common stock under its ongoing share repurchase program as updated and approved by our board of directors, or the financing of possible acquisitions or business expansion.
