Starbucks Corp. Announces Launch of Two New Quick-Brew Products in the UK and Japan.
Starbucks Corp. announced the launch of two new quick-brew products to expand the company's presence in the UK and Japan. Starbucks is selling its instant coffee in Tesco stores throughout the United Kingdom and will expand that to Sainsbury's Waitrose and other major retailers will later this year. The company announced that it will soon be available in more than 2,000 grocery stores across the UK. The company announced that it will launch a line of new coffee products this fall in grocery and convenience stores in Japan. This will be the first time Starbucks has sold coffee products outside its own retail stores in that country. The company announced that it will sell a single-use pour-over brewing system dubbed Origami Personal Drip. It will also sell a version of its Via product there.
