Starbucks Corp. to Introduce Starbucks VIA Ready Brew in Philippines Starting Mid-September 2010.
Starbucks Corp. has announced that it will introduce Starbucks VIA Ready Brew in Philippines starting mid-September 2010. All Starbucks stores in the Philippines will reportedly carry Starbucks VIA Ready Brew in Colombia, Italian Roast and Decaffeinated Italian Roast. Starbucks VIA Ready Brew is made from arabica coffee.
