Starbucks Corp. Plans to to Debut its Latest Caffeinated Concoction: Flavored Instant Coffee.
Starbucks Corp. announced that it is set to debut its latest caffeinated concoction: flavored instant coffee. The four slightly sweetened varieties of popular Via instant brew will be sold in Starbucks stores this fall. The single-serving packages of mocha, vanilla, caramel and cinnamon dissolve in water. They'll be added to grocery store shelves early next year.
