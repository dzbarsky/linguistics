Starbucks Corp. Opens 400th Bar in China.
Starbucks opened its 400th bar in China in the central Hunan province, expanding its presence in China's second-tier cities. Starbucks opened two bars the same day in two business centers in Changsha, capital of Hunan, making it the second city in central China to host Starbucks after Wuhan, capital of neighboring Hubei province.
