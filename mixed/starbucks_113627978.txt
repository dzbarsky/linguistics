Starbucks Corp. Opens Store in New York with New Design Approach.
Starbucks Corp. opened a store in New York with a new design approach inspired by Starbucks Shared Planet which reportedly showcases locally sourced materials as well as reused and recycled elements, from the artwork to the community table. In addition, this store is registered for Leed certification. The customers will find plaques that identify and explain specific green design and construction elements throughout the store. The new store uses LED and CFL light bulbs reduce energy use and that the paints used in the store are low volatile organic compounds and water based.
