Starbucks Corp. Opens New CCM Starbucks on UC's Uptown Campus.
Starbucks Corp. announced the grand opening of the new CCM Starbucks on UC's Uptown Campus. The new CCM Starbucks offers later hours, open until 9 p.m., Monday through Friday. Another Starbucks is located in the Steger Student Life Center, plus Java City gourmet coffee is offered at other college cafes as well as at Tangeman University Center (TUC).
