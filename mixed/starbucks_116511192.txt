Starbucks Seeks Acquisitions.
Starbucks Corporation said that the company is looking to grow through acquisitions. Starbucks still wants to expand its international store base including more than tripling its stores in mainland China to 1,500 by 2015, Starbucks executives told analysts at a conference in New York. CEO, Howard Schultz told the analysts Starbucks plans to consider acquisitions "small and large" for its new direction, although he and other officials declined to say what firms the company is eyeing.
