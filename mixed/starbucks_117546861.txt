Starbucks Corporation Launches Frappuccino Blended Beverage in US and Canada.
Starbucks Corporation has announced the introduction of the new, However-You-Want-It Frappuccino blended beverage in all US and Canada retail stores. Customers can prepare their Frappuccino beverage with a variety of fresh milks or soy, choose the intensity of their coffee and pick any combination of syrups or toppings, the same way they customize their Starbucks espresso beverage. The new Frappuccino blended beverage line includes Extra Coffee Caramel Frappuccino Blended Beverage, Mocha Light Frappuccino Blended Beverage, and Soy Strawberries & Creme Frappuccino Blended Beverage.
