Starbucks Corporation Opens a Store in Xiamen.
Starbucks opened a store at Lujiang Road, Xiamen city on January 5, a further step toward expansion in South China market. The new store is Starbucks' largest in China. Xiamen is the 33rd mainland city where Starbucks has built a presence. Another three Starbucks stores will be opened there in near term, said a top executive for Starbucks Greater China.
