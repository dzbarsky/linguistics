Starbucks Corporation Announces Retirement of Barbara Bass to Its Board of Directors.
On January 7, 2011, Barbara Bass, a member of the Board of Directors of Starbucks Corporation since 1996, notified the Board of Directors of her intention not to stand for re-election at the Companys 2011 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Ms. Basss distinguished service on the Board of Directors includes serving as the Chair of the Compensation and Management Development Committee since 2003.
