Starbucks Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended January 2, 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second, Third, Fourth Quarter of Fiscal 2011 and Full Year of Fiscal 2011; Declares Cash Dividend Payable on February 25, 2011; Plans to Open Approximately 500 Net New Stores Globally.
Starbucks Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended January 2, 2011. For the quarter, the company has total net revenue of $2,950.8 million against $2,722.7 million a year ago. The increase in net revenues were primarily due to a 7% increase in global comparable stores sales, comprised of a 5% increase in the number of transactions and a 2% increase in average ticket. Operating income was $501.9 million against $352.6 million a year ago. Earnings before income taxes were $508.4 million against $369.5 million a year ago. Net earnings attributable to company were $346.6 million or $0.45 diluted per share against $241.5 million or $0.32 diluted per share a year ago. Non-GAAP operating income was $501.9 million against $370.9 million a year ago. Non-GAAP diluted EPS was $0.45 against $0.33 a year ago.
